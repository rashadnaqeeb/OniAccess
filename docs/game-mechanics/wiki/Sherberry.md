Sherberries
are a nonrenewable, nonperishable
Food
item. They can be obtained by digging up
Sherberry Plants
,  or they can sometimes be found when excavating
Buried Objects
in the
Ice Cave Biome
. Due to their convenient location and early availability, they are often the second food source in the game to be utilized (after
Pemmican
) when starting on the
Ceres Asteroid
.
Sherberries can only be eaten directly; they cannot be used in any recipes.
Like other foods that cannot expire, Sherberries cannot be
composted
.
Usage
Hatches
and
Sage Hatches
can eat 700kcal of Sherberry per cycle to produce 656.3g and 850g of
Coal
per cycle, respectively.
