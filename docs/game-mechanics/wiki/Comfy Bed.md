Comfy Bed
Provides a sleeping area for one Duplicant and restores additional Stamina.
Duplicants will automatically sleep in their assigned beds at night.
Duplicants prefer comfy beds to cots and gain more stamina from sleeping in them.

Comfy Bed
provides a sleeping area for one
Duplicant
and restores additional stamina, providing more stamina regeneration than a
Cot
. It can be used to create a
Luxury Barracks
or a
Private Bedroom
which improve morale.
Duplicants
will automatically sleep in their assigned beds
during scheduled bedtime
.
Comfy beds can be found as part of
ruins
.
Comfy Beds vs. Cots
Pros
A Luxury Barracks provides +1 morale and a Private Bedroom +2 morale versus a
Barracks
Comfy Beds in Private Bedrooms will allow dupes to spend
~6 seconds per cycle
more time awake over Cots, as it raises the stamina recovery rate to 1230%/cycle.
The Comfy Bed is wide enough to negate the Loud Sleeper
trait
.
Cons
Twice as wide as a Cot. This increases overall base size and thus overall commute times.  This erodes some of the benefits of less time spent sleeping mentioned above.
Provides less decor than a Cot and a Masterwork
Blank Canvas
painting in the same space. However, decor's morale bonus can be capped by other sources and bedroom's
+1
/
+2
to morale can only be gained from the room.
Several Comfy Beds in a Bedroom gives the same stamina recovery rate of 1030%/cycle compared to Cots in a Barrack.
Conclusion
Overall the only noteworthy effects of switching from cots to beds are +1/+2 morale for the rooms and -200 kg of plastic per bed (assuming that space have been planned for ahead of time).

For
radiation
exposure, Duplicants are considered in the middle left cell when sleeping.
