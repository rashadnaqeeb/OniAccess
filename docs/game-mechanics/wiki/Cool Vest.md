# Cool Vest

The subject of this article was removed from the game.
* The in-game information in this article is kept purely for historical purposes.

Cool Vest
is a
clothing item
manufactured from the
Textile Loom
. Once manufactured clothes can be assigned to
Duplicants
. Like all other clothes it takes 4
Reed Fiber
to construct. It decreases
decor
by 10, and changes the conductivity barrier from the default 0.25 cm to 0.05 cm. This is in addition to the 0.2 cm of insulation afforded by the stunningly thick skin of Duplicants. This reduction helps to keep your Duplicants cool by allowing more heat exchange.
Trivia
Despite its description, reducing the insulation thickness will more likely get the Duplicants scalded in hot environments.
