Polluted Oxygen
is a
gas
resource
. Polluted Oxygen is breathable and works similarly to
Oxygen
, allowing
Duplicants
to survive. However, it also acts as the primary vector of transmission for
Slimelung
germs, and can just as easily spread
Food Poisoning
germs. Polluted Oxygen can be found in the world naturally in the
Swamp Biome
.
Breathing too much Polluted Oxygen causes the
Yucky Lungs
debuff, which increases the air consumption rate and causes coughing
A Polluted Oxygen atmosphere causes
Food
nearby to rot at a +100% rate (double default speed) . Further Information:
Food (Resource)
.
Polluted Oxygen has the same density as Oxygen. When Polluted Oxygen and Oxygen are mixed, they tend to group with the same element and form gas layers; however, the heights of such layers is random, with neither density nor temperature having any effect.
Polluted Oxygen spawns naturally as part of
Swamp Biomes
.
Usage
Deodorizers
produce
Oxygen
and
Clay
from Polluted Oxygen using a
Filtration Medium
.
Pufts
and Puft Princes can breathe Polluted Oxygen and excrete
Slime
when they do.
Production
Slime
,
Polluted Dirt
, and bottled
Polluted Water
all emit Polluted Oxygen into the air in environments with <1800g of air pressure.
This spreads any
germs
present on the substance into the air.
Unbottled
Polluted Water
will also emit germy Polluted Oxygen into the air in environments with <1800g of air pressure. The rate of production is random.
Morbs
produce Polluted Oxygen into the air in environments with <1000g of air pressure without any inputs. They also release Polluted Oxygen upon death.
There are two types of
geysers
that produce Polluted Oxygen – Hot and Infectious Polluted Oxygen Vents. Both yield ~95 g/s and are stifled at 5 kg of air pressure, with the latter spreading
Slimelung
germs. Each of them produces enough polluted oxygen to sustain ~5 wild Pufts, or 1-2 tame ones, but the hotter geyser must be cooled first to not kill critters, especially since it is viable for powering
Steam Turbines
.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Oxygen Rich Asteroid Field at a rate of 30–100 kg per cycle.
In Organic Mass Field at a rate of 54–162 kg per cycle
Sublimation Station
can turn 1000g/s
Polluted Dirt
into 660g/s
Polluted Oxygen

Tips
Polluted Oxygen can be converted to
Liquid Oxygen
by cooling it down to its condensation point using an installation involving
Thermo Regulators

Guide/Cleaning Polluted Oxygen
for an example).
Trivia
