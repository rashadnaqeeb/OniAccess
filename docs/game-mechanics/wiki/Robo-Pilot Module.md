The
Robo-Pilot Module
allows flying rockets without a duplicant pilot.
While flying, it consumes data banks at a set rate of 2
Data Banks
per hex traveled. When it runs out of databanks, it becomes unable to fly any further, leaving the rocket stranded in space unless directly adjacent to an asteroid with a rocket platform on it.
The module can operate alone or in conjunction with a duplicant pilot in a spacefarer module; in the latter case the rocket gains a speed boost of 50%.
If you plan on having the rocket fly fully autonomous without duplicants, make sure to enable the roundtrip feature or have databanks stocked at the target destination, as the amount of databanks requested is equal to the amount consumed to travel to the target destination (e.g. travel distance of 10 hexes -> 20 data banks requested, 40 with roundtrip enabled)
