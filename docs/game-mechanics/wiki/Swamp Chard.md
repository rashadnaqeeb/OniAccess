Swamp Chard
Edible Plant
Swamp Chard is a unique member of the Amaranthaceae family that has adapted to grow in humid environments, in or near pools of standing water.
While the leaves are technically edible, the most nutritious and palatable part of the plant is the heart, which is rich in a number of essential vitamins.
