Ancient Specimen
is a
Story Trait
that can generate in
asteroids
or
planetoids
, which is focused around the structure of the same name. Once the story trait is completed, the Ancient Specimen turns into the Fossil Quarry and allows to mine lots of
Fossil
with some
Diamond
.
Description
The Ancient Specimen Story Trait generates 4 points of interest: The eponymous Ancient Specimen and three smaller fossils:
Amber
,
Frozen
and
Petrified
. All four always generate on the same asteroid.
Progress
Initiation
Once a fossil is discovered no further action is required to start excavating it. Prior to the February 2024 QoL update the select of any fossil would reveal the remaining three.
Fossil Excavating
A duplicant with the Masterworks
Skill
can excavate the fossil. To be excavated the fossil must fully stand on solid floor and can't be entombed. Each small fossil type comes with its own danger and can be deconstructed after excvatation (even if the story trait isn't completed).
Ancient Specimen
The main Ancient Specimen generates in a rectangle together with  two Large
Fossil Fragment
and one Small Fossil Fragment. They are partially buried in some natural tiles, open parts are filled with a small amount of
Carbon Dioxide
.
It is usually found in a
Sandstone Biome
.

.

Completion
Once all four fossil are excavated, the story trait is ready for completion via the notifications in the interface. Completing the story trait turns the Ancient Specimen into the Fossil Quarry and gives a reward of one
Keepsake
, the Critter Collar, which can be displayed on a
Pedestal
.
Fossil Quarry
Once the story trait is completed, the Ancient Specimen is renamed to Fossil Quarry and can still be utilized, although it works a bit differently than before. The lantern of the Fossil Quarry emits light and production of Fossil can be requested. Operating the Fossil Quarry still requires the Masterworks
skill
and turns 1 kg
Diamond
into 100 kg
Fossil
. Fossil can be processed further to
Sedimentary Rock
and
Lime
in a
Rock Crusher
.
Due to the high return ratio of the Fossil Quarry you can create a positive resource-loop in the Spaced Out DLC with Rock Crusher,
Hatches
,
Kiln
and
Diamond Press
.
The temperature of the
Fossil
output is based on the temperature of
Diamond
stored in the Fossil Quarry (which is not insulated). This can be exploited for heat multiplication due to the thermal mass of the output being much higher than that of the input. Note that if the output temperature is higher than
1341.85 °C
/
2447.33 °F
the output material will immediately turn into
Magma
or
Rock Gas
.
