Refined Metal

Nectar

Plastic

without having to go through a hot
Oily Biome

can consume Nectar straight from the tree to produce
Ethanol

Rocket Modules
and the
Rocket Platform
as these buildings do not exchange heat with their environment.

Nectar Biome
The
snow
-laden Nectar Biome is home to the massive
Bonbon Tree
. This complex plant produces
Nectar
, which can be refined into
Sucrose
and
Steam
.
Spigot Seals
consume this sweet liquid and produce
Ethanol
to shore up a colony's fuel supplies. Spigot Seal ranches also yield
Tallow
, a greasy substance that can be used for
Food
or refined into
Crude Oil
to support local
Power
systems.
Beneath the
Snow
and
Ice
are generous deposits of solid
Mercury
, a rare metal that can be liquefied for use in industrial cooling systems.
This biome is truly a sight to behold: in addition to the soft charm of the occasional
Idylla Flower
, there is something quite heartwarming about the way that the
Shine Bugs'
glowing lights glitter on the frozen landscape.
