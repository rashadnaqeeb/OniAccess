The Sweepy
is an automated sweeping robot that cleans up debris left on the ground.
Sweepy can be made by the
Sweepy Dock
, which is located in the Utilities bar
A Sweepy can hold up to 500 kg of materials. When it collects over 500 kg of debris, it will try to return to its
Sweepy Dock
to deliver collected materials and liquids.
The Sweepy will explode when its dock is destroyed.
The Sweepy can be renamed to anything you wish by clicking on it, and then clicking on the "rename" button next to its name.
The Sweepy cannot move vertically through any means, and is unable to cross gaps of any size.
It also does an expression that runs for several in-game seconds when it encounters any gap; thus, it should be deployed in a pit.
Suggested Uses
Where Dupes are available, or in locations that are monitored by an
Auto-Sweeper
, Sweepy is vastly outperformed, especially in the matter of gathering Solids.
However, Sweepy is of use in
Spaced Out!
, which features some places that are very hazardous to Duplicants. The most notable example of this is radioactive biomes that are inhabited by
Beetas
; the locals ignore the robot.
Furthermore, compared to the Auto-Sweeper, Sweepy can mop floors and collect liquids. Again, this is of use in radioactive biomes with Beetas; Beetas have fast life cycles and will release
Nuclear Waste
when they die. Sweepy can collect this more safely than Dupes with
Lead Suits
could.
Where Sweepy can shine is in frequently flooded places, like in vicinity of
Jet Suit Dock
, if the player uses Yellow Alert
Priority
frequently, thus leading to making a mess by
Duplicants
.

The Sweepy is a domesticated sweeping robot programmed to clean solid and liquid debris. The
Sweepy's Dock
will automatically launch the Sweepy, store the debris the robot picks up, and recharge the Sweepy's battery, provided it has been plugged into a power source.
Though the Sweepy can not travel over gaps or uneven ground, it is programmed to feel really bad about this.
Trivia
Since any Sweepy will explode when its dock does not exist, a Sweepy can be spawned in
sandbox
/
debug
mode but immediately explodes upon doing so. Instead, spawn 25kg of refined metal and get a
Duplicant
to deliver it to a
Sweepy Dock
and it will be crafted.
Sweepy does not have a decor bonus on its own, but can be used as a display (like the
Pedestal#Displayables
).
Sweepy cannot fall off a tile unless the tile underneath is destroyed.
Sweepy will not pick up critter eggs.
When Sweepy is on a horizontal
Mechanized Airlock
or
Manual Airlock
when it opens, the Sweepy can fall and become separated from its dock, requiring a
Duplicant
to retrieve it and return it to its dock.
