Igneous rock
is a
Mineral
found in abundance in the
Caustic
and
Oil
Biomes and in smaller quantities in the
Frozen
and
Temperate
Biomes. It can be used as a general construction material.
Usage
While Igneous Rock possesses no Decor bonus, its primary value lies in its high thermal volume and low conductivity. Together, this makes it extremely useful as an insulator in the early- to mid-game before
Ceramic
and
Insulation
is readily available. It is especially useful when used to build
Insulated Tiles
,
Insulated Liquid Pipes
and
Insulated Gas Pipes
.
Igneous Rock also possesses together with
Obsidian
the highest
Radiation Absorption
rate of Raw Minerals.
Rock Crusher
can break Igneous Rock into
Sand
.
100 kg Igneous Rock = 100 kg Sand.
Stone Hatches
can eat
Igneous Rock
and excrete
Coal
.
Production
It forms from solidified
Magma
, which is a renewable resource on maps with
volcanoes
. Alternatively it can be sourced from melted
Regolith
, which is a renewable on every map, it also in addition creates enormous amount of
heat
to be
utilized
.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Forested Ore Fields at a rate of 378 – 1134 kg per cycle.
In Rocky Asteroid Fields at a rate of 216 – 648 kg per cycle.
