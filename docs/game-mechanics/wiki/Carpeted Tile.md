Usage
Carpeted Tile can be used to increase the Decor values of rooms. When building Carpeted Tile, selecting
Granite
as building material will add +20% bonus to decor values.
Walking over Carpeted Tiles gives duplicants a runspeed penalty of 25%, but also the
Tickled Tootsies
effect, which results in a -15%/cycle stress reduction for 15 seconds. This also applies if they are not actually moving or even touching the ground - so sleeping in a Cot on Carpeted Tiles also gives this buff.
Trivia
Duplicants cannot get the "Tickled Tootsies" effect when Sopping Wet or have Soggy Feet.
