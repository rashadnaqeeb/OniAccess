Interstellar Ice
is a space destination with a distance of 30,000 km. It is composed entirely of
Solid
elements with no
Gases
or
Liquids
, though all of the elements are either gas or liquid at standard temperature.
Utilization
The Interstellar Ice is a renewable source of
Water
,
Oxygen
,
Methane
and
Carbon Dioxide
, which are stored in the
Cargo Bay
. Aside from coming up as a rare choice in the Printing Pod (one seed), it is also the only renewable source of
Wort Seeds
, which can be gathered along with
Sleet Wheat Grains
in a
Biological Cargo Bay
. The most efficient
Rocket
configuration for missions to the Ice Planet consists of 5 cargo modules and 3
Liquid Fuel Tanks
, and is capable of returning 1.852 kilograms of cargo for each kilogram of
Petroleum
fuel.
Tips
The Methane acquired from a mission can provide much more power than the
Petroleum
required to fuel the rocket would, 4 times as much on average assuming optimal rocket configuration.
Carbon Dioxide acquired from the Ice Planet can be fed to
Slicksters
or
Molten Slicksters
to produce more Petroleum for rocket fuel. This is not sustainable, however.
