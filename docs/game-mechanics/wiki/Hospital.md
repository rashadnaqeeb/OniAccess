Hospital
is a special room that is good for quarantining Duplicants assigned to it, to reduce the spread of illnesses. It needs to be at least 12 tiles big in area and requires the following:
Medical bed (ex: Triage Cot)
Table (ex: Mess table)
Toilet (ex: Outhouse)
No industrial machinery
Tips
Restores Duplicant's health, speeds up timing out of negative conditions such as Hypothermia and diseases, and regenerates energy.
The Toilet can be disabled (to save the germ hassle) and the room will still qualify for Hospital.
The Toilet and the Mess Table will automatically be assigned to the room and not be used by any duplicant not using the room currently.
