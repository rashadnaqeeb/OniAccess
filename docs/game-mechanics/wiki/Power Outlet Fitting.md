. A Battery Module is necessary for this device to function properly on the rocket. Care should be taken with load balancing, the battery module does not electrically isolate the rocket's interior wiring from the exterior wiring. If the load on the circuit that is used to charge the battery module exceeds 1kW, do not use ordinary wires on the inside of the rocket or they will burn out from being overloaded.
