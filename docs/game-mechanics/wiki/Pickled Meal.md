Pickled Meal
is a food item made from 3
Meal Lice
(1800 kcal). It has a
Food Quality
of -1 (Grisly) and provides 1800 kcal. Pickled Meal (and
Grubfruit Preserve
) have the highest shelf life possible (besides the items with unlimited shelf life). Since it is cooked at an Electric Grill, any food poisoning germs present on the ingredients (meal lice) will not survive.
There are no recipes using Pickled Meal as an ingredient to create more sophisticated food.
Tips
Meal lice gains 500 calories when turned into
Liceloaf
for 50 kg
water
versus having the same amount calories when turned into Pickled Meal.
One side-effect of not using water for meal preparation, is that your dupes will not get sick from germy water.
Pickled Meal has a very peculiar effect in that it is the only cooked food to destroy
Food Poisoning
germs in room temperature, meaning that it eliminates most of the risk of food poisoning, even if a dupe has not washed up recently.
