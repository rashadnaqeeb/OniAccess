# Heat Stroke

The subject of this article was removed from the game.
* The in-game information in this article is kept purely for historical purposes.
Heat Stroke
is a temporary status appearing when
Duplicants
get overheated. When it is applied Duplicant will begin to take health damage until their body
temperature
drops. Additionally,
athletics
penalty from health damage is also applied until the injury is healed.
Duplicants will try to leave overheated location, unless they have active
errands
in there.
The typical sources of Heat Stroke are
Geysers
, hot bodies of
water
and overheating
machinery
.
Oil Biome
is also usually hot enough to quickly induce Heat Stroke, and
Volcanic Biome
is straight up lethal.
The opposite of Heat Stroke is
Hypothermia
.
Penalties
Science
-5
Machinery
-5
Cuisine
-5
Construction
-5
Duration: 180 seconds (0.3 cycle)
Tips
Avoid sending dupes into hot environments without proper equipment (
Atmo Suit
/
Jet Suit
).
Set up your
Triage Cots
to low thresholds to quickly remove athletics penalty.
The
Regenerative
trait will make duplicant regenerate health, which will remove the athletics penalty after some time (20hp/cycle).
