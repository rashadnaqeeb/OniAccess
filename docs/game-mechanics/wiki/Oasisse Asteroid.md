Oasisse Asteroid
A desolate location with a cache of life-sustaining water trapped beneath the surface.
A wide underground desert will pose temperature threats and strain the resources of a colony on Oasisse. However, surviving the challenge should present great industrial opportunities.

The
Oasisse Asteroid
is one of the
Asteroid Types
available to start on. It features lots of very hot
Sand
surrounding the starting
Forest Biome
and taking up most of the map; and, like the
Verdante Asteroid
, it's oil biome can be found in unexpected places, similar to the effects of the Irregular Oil
World Trait
. Due to the combination of early challenges, Oasisse can be considered the one of the more difficult starts in the game.
Map Layout
Example Oasisse world. Notice the huge amount of hot sand and scattered
Oil Biome
patches.
Strategy
While the early
Water
in the
Forest Biome
can be used for
Oxygen
production, it is far better allocated for research. Water is sparse close to the
Printing Pod
, making water geysers an ideal early source, although these can be tricky to tame early.
The Desert is hot, around
70 °C
/
158 °F
. This, along with the
Biome
border being made of
Granite
and not
Abyssalite
means that there is a fast-approaching heat threat that can very easily prevent crops from growing when ignored.
Wolframite
does not spawn anywhere on Oasisse, unless the map has the Glaciers or Frozen Core
World Traits
. The only source of this metal is from
Space Destinations
, making obtaining
Niobium
rather difficult, even in the late game.
Dasha Saltvines
,
Sleet Wheat
, and
Wheezeworts
are not obtainable on the asteroid itself and can only be obtained from the
Printing Pod
with
Care Packages
enabled or from
Space Destinations
.
Nosh Sprouts
are completely unobtainable.
