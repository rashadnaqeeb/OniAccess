Microbe Musher
is a
building
used to produce some starting and intermediary
Foods
. Recipes must be assigned in order for
duplicants
to work.
The Microbe Musher is served by the
cooking errand
, and utilizes
cuisine
attribute. A higher attribute level will reduce the time and overall energy required to complete a production cycle.
Food products fall to the ground after being produced and will
expire
if not
refrigerated
or placed in a sterile (oxygen/water-free) environment.

Tips
Mush Bars are disastrously inefficient for regular food production. They consume extreme amounts of both dirt and water, offer no
morale
benefit, draw precious early game energy, and require considerable duplicant time to produce. Research Basic Farming and grow
plants
ASAP to avoid ever using them.
However they can be useful as a temporary food source in an emergency, or when attempting the Locavore
Achievement
.
