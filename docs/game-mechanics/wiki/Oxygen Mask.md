Oxygen Mask
The
Oxygen Mask
is the simplest, cheapest craftable Exosuit produced by the
Crafting Station
. It protects Duplicants from hostile environments at the cost of
Athletics
. Dupes equip an Oxygen Mask if one is available at an
Oxygen Mask Dock
. Dupes also cannot pass an
Oxygen Mask Checkpoint
if they aren't wearing an Oxygen Mask.
Oxygen Masks
are crafted at a with 50 kg
Aluminum, Cobalt, Copper, or Iron Ore
or 1
Worn Oxygen Mask
.
Hazard Protection
Oxygen Masks protect their wearer against
Yucky Lungs
and inhaling
Germs
, but not
Eye Irritation
or
Popped Eardrums
.
Oxygen masks do not protect the wearer from
Gnit
stings.
Oxygen Masks provide their wearers with
Oxygen
, allowing them to work in non-oxygen environments without any worry. At 20 kg capacity, a dupe with average oxygen consumption may breathe for 200 seconds before needing to hold their breath. However, dupes will retreat to refill their Oxygen Mask at ?% capacity.
Drawbacks
Oxygen Masks apply -2 Athletics while worn. This penalty can be negated with the
Suit Sustainability Training skill
.
Oxygen Masks lose durability at a rate of 20% per cycle while worn, and will turn into a
Worn Oxygen Mask
when an Oxygen Mask with 0% durability has been unequipped.
Duplicants will still exhale
Carbon Dioxide
(
2 g/s
) into nonbreathable areas, which is problematic when trying to maintain a
Vacuum
or keeping an area free of Carbon Dioxide.
Notes
Oxygen Masks have a significantly smaller oxygen capacity compared to an
Atmo Suit
, lasting only 200 seconds. (⅓ cycle)
Oxygen Masks have a limited durability and must be repaired after their durability falls to zero.
