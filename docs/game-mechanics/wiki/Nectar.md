Nectar
is an organic liquid only produced by
Bonbon Trees
. It can be used in the production of
Plastic
,
Ethanol
and
Sucrose
.
Production
Bonbon Trees
produce up to 80 kg/cycle of Nectar when fully grown and exposed to at least 10000 Lux.
Nectar can be extracted by Duplicants doing harvesting jobs, into a connected pipe or by a feeding
Spigot Seal
.
Usage
Polymer Press
transforms Nectar into
Plastic
.
Spigot Seals
convert Nectar into
Ethanol
by drinking it directly from a Bonbon Tree, but not if it was extracted.
Nectar vaporizes into 77%
Sucrose
and 23%
Steam
at
160 °C
/
320 °F
and solidifies into 77%
Sucrose
and 23%
Ice
at
-82.5 °C
/
-116.5 °F
.
Tips
Its Specific Heat Capacity is similar to water, but its temperature range is significantly greater, making it an excellent early game coolant or for heat deletion in a
Polymer Press
.
Due to its wider liquid temperature range, Nectar also works well for
liquid airlocks
, especially for colder temperature ranges.  Nectar over
Ethanol
can form a stable liquid lock all the way down to
-83 °C
/
-117.4 °F
, significantly below the point where a
Petroleum
over
Crude Oil
or any form of Water-based liquid lock would freeze solid.
Nectar and
Salt Water
share the same density, meaning they can be stacked to create liquid airlocks of arbitrary height.
