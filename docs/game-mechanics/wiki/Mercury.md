Mercury
is a metallic
Liquid
. It is particularly useful as a thermal conductor.
Usage
It is consumed by
Mercury Ceiling Light
to produce a large amount of light.
Despite its low
Specific Heat Capacity
, Mercury's high
Thermal Conductivity
of 8.300 (more than four times that of
Petroleum
), as well as its low freezing point and high boiling point (
-38.8 °C
/
-37.84 °F
and
356.8 °C
/
674.24 °F
respectively), makes it an excellent thermal conductor in both freezing and boiling temperatures alike.
Counter-intuitively, it is also an excellent thermal insulator when used in very low mass (<40g) because of its very low specific capacity. At low mass amounts, heat exchange is largely dominated by
clamping
. A practical application using  Mercury's low specific heat capacity and low solidification point are accessible steam rooms - very little heat will leak out of a liquid lock entrance with only 10g of mercury.
Mercury's thermal range makes it an ideal liquid to use in the construction of
Liquid Airlocks
in freezing/boiling temperatures before
Nectar
or
Crude Oil
has been discovered.
The liquid walls can be made by building tempshift plates out of
Solid Mercury
, which quickly melt.  Other refined metal structures, such as Radiant liquid and gas pipes, also work well for this.
Mercury can be frozen into Solid Mercury, which is a
Refined Metal
that can be used to construct buildings in extremely low temperatures (below
-40 °C
/
-40 °F
). This also means that players must be careful not to accidentally select it as a building material in higher temperature applications.
Production
Mercury is found naturally in large amounts in the
Nectar Biome
.
Mercury can be refined from
Cinnabar Ore
via the
Rock Crusher
at a rate of 100kg Cinnabar Ore → 50kg Mercury + 50kg
Sand
.
Mercury can be refined from Cinnabar Ore via the
Metal Refinery
at a rate of 100kg Cinnabar Ore → 100kg Mercury.
Mercury can be harvested from the
Ceres Ore Field
Space POI
.
Tips
Solid mercury brought back into a base storage area will often quickly melt, so care should be taken when designating storage to avoid this.
Its low specific heat capacity means that it is not suited to being used as a coolant for the
Metal Refinery
. Depending on the metal being refined, even mercury starting at subzero temperatures may immediately vaporize into a gas.
