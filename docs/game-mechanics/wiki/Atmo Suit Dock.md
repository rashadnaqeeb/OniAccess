# Atmo Suit Dock

Atmo Suit Dock
Stores and recharges Atmo Suits. Build next to an Atmo Suit Checkpoint to make Duplicants change into suits when passing by.
An atmo suit dock will empty atmo suits of waste, but only one suit can charge at a time.

Atmo Suit Dock
is a
building
that stores one
Atmo Suit
and fills it with
Oxygen
. It must be built adjacent to either an
Atmo Suit Checkpoint
or another Dock that is adjacent to a Checkpoint. A row of Docks may be built for a single Checkpoint as long as all the buildings are adjacent.
Atmo Suit Docks require Oxygen piping and Power to work. Piping gases other than Oxygen will damage the Atmo Suit Dock.
Tips
When you inspect a dock that contains a suit, the Contents shows "Atmo Suit x 1: 200kg @
xx
°C". That is the mass of the
suit
, not its oxygen load. To see how much oxygen the suit contains, click on that line to select the suit itself.
Power is consumed only when oxygen is being loaded into atmo suits. The dock has a maximum throughput of 1000g/s.
Standard dupes breathe 100 g/s, therefore a suit needs to recharge 1s for each 18s it was worn. Docks draw 120 W, hence wearing a suit uses 6.67W.
Unsuiting suits at a dock exchanges surface germs with the oxygen tank within the suit, potentially contaminating them with infectious germs like
Slimelung
and
Zombie Spores
for subsequent suit wearers.
