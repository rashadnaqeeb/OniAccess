# Temporal Tear

The
Temporal Tear
is the farthest destination on the
Starmap
. It serves as the ending (or a major lategame milestone) of Oxygen Not Included, for both the Base Game and the
DLC. In lore, it is a gateway to an alternate timeline or universe, which is hopefully a paradise. This is never confirmed. The rocket sent to the Temporal Tear, along with any
Duplicant
on it, will never return to the colony. Once a rocket has breached the temporal tear, the
achievement
of "The Great Escape" will be earned.
In the
Spaced Out!
DLC:
The Temporal Tear can be opened with the
Temporal Tear Opener
, located on the
Tundra Asteroid
.
Once open, the Tear will cause
Fullerene
Meteor Showers
once a cycle on the Tundra Asteroid, until you save and reload the game.
Note: if a DLC's fragment planetoid replaces the Tundra Asteroid (e.g. the
Ceres Fragment
from
), the Temporal Tear Opener will still be present on it.
When a rocket arrive at the Temporal Tear for the first time, you will see a cutscene (similar to the
Spaced Out! Short
) before being given a view (centered on your
Printing Pods
) of each
Planetoid
with a Printing Pod.
