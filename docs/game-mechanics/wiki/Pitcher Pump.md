# Pitcher Pump

Pitcher Pump
Manually pumps Liquid into bottles for transport.
Duplicants can only carry liquids that are bottled.
Pitcher Pumps allow Duplicants to bottle and deliver liquids from place to place.

Pitcher Pump
pumps
Liquid
below it into bottles for transport.
Duplicants
can only carry bottled liquids. A Liquid Bottler is required to supply
buildings
that require liquids, but do not have
piping

The building itself is 2 tiles wide and 4 tiles tall. The building will automatically expand up to 4 tiles deep into the liquid below the pump. The bottler can't expand into foreground buildings, such as
Liquid Pumps
or
Ladders
.
Hovering over it with the mouse displays how much liquid is accessible. This includes only the tiles actually within the pump's reach (maximum 2 x 5 tiles), and only of liquids who's total mass exceeds
1 kg
. When the pump is in a larger pool, the actual available amount is obviously much larger, since liquid from the surrounding pool will keep flowing in.
When multiple liquids are located in Liquid Bottler's reach, the Duplicants will be able to separate them. The building will work with non-water liquids; it can be used to pump liquid
Chlorine
or
Phosphorus
.

Tips
Removing unwanted liquid from a pool can be done by using Pitcher Pump and auto-bottling
Bottle Emptier
.
For example, large amount of Polluted Water in Water pool that cannot be mopped or floating
Visco-Gel
and Oil.
Duplicants will try to deliver multiple supply errands together. Use Pitcher Pump and 4 side-by-side
Bottle Emptiers
to reach maximum manually transport efficiency from one pool to another. (800 kg per run)
Making auto-bottling
Bottle Emptier
suddenly unreachable to drop the bottle from duplicants is an efficient way to automate liquid bottling. This is useful for generating
Polluted Oxygen
from
Polluted Water
bottles.
Other ways for liquid bottling included emptying pipes and deconstructing full
Liquid Reservoir
, and
pumping liquid into a
Liquid Cargo Tank

or a
Spacefarer Module

The Pump only exchanges heat with the two tiles that make up the "platform" that the duplicant stands on. This allows for some particularly absurd feats, such as using it to collect bottles of
Magma
or even
Liquid Tungsten

Pitcher Pump cannot flood, and can be used even while completely submerged.

Cell of Interest
