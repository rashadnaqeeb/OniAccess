Usage
The
Rust Deoxidizer
can produce
Oxygen
,
Chlorine
,
Iron Ore
with Rust and
Salt
:
750 g/s Rust + 250 g/s Salt = 570 g/s Oxygen + 30 g/s Chlorine + 400 g/s Iron Ore.
Heating Rust over its melting point will turn it into
Molten Iron
with a 1:1 ratio, which can then be cooled into refined
Iron
. The conversion is 87.5% more efficient than what can be achieved with a using Rust Deoxidizer, but doesn't produce Oxygen or Chlorine. In case an
Iron Volcano
is available, it's more worthwhile to use the Rust Deoxidizer, as
Iron Ore
is more difficult to produce in other means.
Production
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Oxidized Asteroid Fields at a rate of 432 – 1296 kg per cycle.
In Radioactive Asteroid Fields at a rate of 18.2 – 72.8 kg per cycle.
It is produced by
Jawbos
.
Tips
Magma
spawns from
Volcanoes
with a high enough temperature to melt rust. That temperature can also be reached by using
Liquid Steel
as "coolant" in a
Metal Refinery
. In both cases, reusing the heat from the resulting iron to preheat more rust will make the process much more heat-efficient. A properly designed rust melter may later be used to refine most other
Metal Ores
faster than with a metal refinery or it can be converted into a
Regolith
melter.
