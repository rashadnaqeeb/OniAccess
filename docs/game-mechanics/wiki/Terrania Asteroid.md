Terrania Asteroid
A balanced variety of resources on a smaller sandstone world.
Terrania Asteroids have everything needed to start a colony in a compact area. With a short distance to the surface, inter-world travel can be achieved quickly.

The
Terrania Asteroid
is a small starting asteroid with a
Sandstone Biome
start. The starting area is surrounded by
Marsh
,
Jungle
,
Barren
,
Wasteland
and
Tundra
Biomes with the
Magma Biome
at the bottom.

Strategy
Despite the flavour text the distance to the surface is the same on all three starting asteroids.
Its infinite water source is a
Cool Steam Vent
or two. It slowly gets hotter over time, providing a familiar challenge.
Wasteland Biome
contains sulfur,
Sweetles
, and
Spindly Grubfruit Plants
.
The hot
Barren Biome
near the bottom which contains some free refined
Iron
. If you want to get any oil, you must go to the Rusty Oil Asteroid, although you can acquire plastic via glossy dreckos.
