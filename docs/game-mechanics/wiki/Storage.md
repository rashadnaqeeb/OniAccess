# Storage

Storage
Storage refers to item contents that is linked to a game object (typically buildings) and serves as an intermediate step in many in-game processes. Storage can typically be directly viewed in the
Contents
tab in the side screen.
For example, when
Salt Water
enters a
Desalinator
, the
Salt Water
is transferred from its input
Liquid Pipe
into the buildingâ€™s storage. After
10Â kg
accumulates, the stored
Salt Water
is converted into
Water
and
Salt
, which are then placed back into its storage.
Items in storage still interact with the Simulation Grid as if they were entities occupying that cell. They participate in germ simulation, exchange heat, and potentially undergo phase change. This can result in surprising behavior when using certain buildings in extreme temperatures for long periods of times, such as the
Critter Fountain
entombing itself with
Frozen Brackene
.
Storage Item Modifiers
Storages may have modifiers that affect item behavior.
Insulate
Items do not exchange heat with the Simulation Grid.
Seal
Items do not undergo
sublimation
or phase changes.
Preserve
Food items do not spoil. (Eggs still lose viability.)
Hide
Hides the storage contents from view. Note even if the storage does not have the Hide modifier, in several cases, items will still be hidden (e.g. Storage Bins, Critter Feeders).
