# Dewdrip

This article is related to
The Prehistoric Planet Pack
content.
Dewdrip
A crystallized blob of
Brackene
from the
Dew Dripper
.
Type
Industrial Ingredient
Dewdrip
are produced by
Dew Drippers
and can be crushed into
Brackene
.
Production
Dew Drippers
produce 1 unit of Dewdrip every 2 cycles or 8 cycles if wild.
Usage
Dewdrip
can be used to make Brackene and is the source of food for
Dartles
.
2 units of Dewdrip make 20 kg
Brackene
in a
Plant Pulverizer
.
Tips
Dewdrips make a great source of
Brackene
when harvested from wild
Dew Drippers
because no
Water
is required unlike other recipes at the
Plant Pulverizer
.
When domesticated, a Dew Dripper will produce a Dewdrip for every 20 kg of
Brine Ice
, which is still a more efficient use of a Water.
