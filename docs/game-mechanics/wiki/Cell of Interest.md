Cell of Interest
(COI, also known as Tile of Interest) is a location on a
building
which corresponds to some form of interaction between the building and the world. There are three types which are: material delivery, material drop-off, and Dupe interaction. In most cases, the three types of COI are located in the same cell.
Cell of interest type
Delivery cell
- The cell which accepts item deliveries to the building.
Material drop-off cell
- The cell where the building item output is dropped, e.g food from an electric grill.
Dupe interaction cell
- The cell that the Dupes must occupy in order to operate the building. Dupes exist in the bottom cell for most purposes.
