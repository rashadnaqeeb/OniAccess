Idylla Flower
Decorative Plant
Idylla Flowers are a rare species of everblooms that thrive with very little care, making them a perennial favorite among newbie gardeners.
Their springy blossoms can be 'bopped' gently for sensory entertainment, but hands should be washed immediately as the petal residue can permanently stain most textiles.
