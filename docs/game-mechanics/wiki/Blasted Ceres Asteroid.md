Blasted Ceres Asteroid
The heavily damaged remnants of a frosty location with sub-zero temperatures throughout.
Exploring the rubble of Blasted Ceres's sub-zero temperatures is particularly challenging, yet natural fuel sources provide a valuable payoff.
