Usage
The Flower Pot is available early on in the game and is an easy way of providing
Decor
. It costs very little to build and is mostly limited by available space and seeds. A single
Sandstone
Flower Pot with a
Bluff Briar
in it will provide +26
Decor
around it. Note that the decor is counted as "20+ decor item" for the condition of
Great Hall
.
Place the Flower Pot between 2 structures where your duplicants will stay for some time (
Cot
,
Research Station
,
Microbe Musher
...) to help with high
Decor
expectation Duplicants early on. Flower Pot by itself does not contribute to decor.
Flower Pots will eventually become outclassed by the
Sculpting Block
's wider range, as its only relevant advantage will be its 2 tile height and higher total decor value per object.

History
U57-700386
: Flower Pots and decor plants no longer count as two separate decor items that contribute to a Room's requirements. An empty Flower Pot will not count as a decor item. If a Flower Pot contains a healthy decor plant, this combination will count as one decor item. If the Flower Pot becomes non-operational (i.e. no foundation) or the plant wilts, it will not count as a decor item.
