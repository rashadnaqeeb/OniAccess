Wall Pot
can host a
decorative plant
which will increase
Decor
in the surrounding area. However, if the plant's needs are not met (and the plant becomes
Stifled
), it will lower decor instead.
Usage
Wall Pot
itself doesn't have decor bonus but the decor bonus rather comes from the plants that may be planted in the pot. Despite the pot itself having a size of only 1w x 1h, the plant that will be planted within the pot has a size of 1w x 1h making the actual useful size of the pot 1w x 2h. This means it is not possible to place a pot with a plant in it directly below
Crown Moulding
or a ceiling tile.
