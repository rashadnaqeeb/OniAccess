Usage
The Bottle Emptier empties any bottle of liquid brought to it by a
Duplicant
. This is useful for cleaning up any mopped up spills. The type of liquid(s) it empties can be limited by the player with the Element Filter menu of the Emptier.
The Bottle Emptier can be rotated in construction so that the liquid can fall to its left side or right side.
By default the only option in the Element Filter menu is
Water
, other
liquids
become available as the player discovers them.
If a duplicant empties a bottle of germ filled polluted water, both the surface of the bottle emptier and the duplicant will be covered in germs.
Enabling Auto Bottling option allows the Duplicants to specifically bottle liquid at a
Pitcher Pump
and bring it to the Bottle Emptier, provided there is a Pitcher Pump with liquid it accepts.
The Bottle Emptier can empty a bottle of 200 kg liquid at most at one time. The Emptier outputs 10 kg/s, so a 200 kg bottle of liquid takes 20 seconds of in-game time to empty. Duplicants will not deliver another bottle until the current one is completely discharged.
The Bottle Emptier can be completely submerged and still function without issue.
