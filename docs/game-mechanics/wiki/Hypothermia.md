# Hypothermia

The subject of this article was removed from the game.
* The in-game information in this article is kept purely for historical purposes.
Hypothermia
is a temporary status affliction that affects a
Duplicant
if their
Body Temperature
drops to
34 °C
/
93.2 °F
or below. There are no remedies, but resting on a
Triage Cot
slightly (~10%) speeds up recovery.
[
confirmation?
]
Hypothermia is most likely to afflict a
Duplicant
in an environment of elements with high
Thermal Conductivity
that are below
34 °C
/
93.2 °F
, such as
Water
(even just a tiny amount) or even
Hydrogen Gas
. It also tends to occur during prolonged stays in a
Frozen Biome
.
A similar status,
Heat Stroke
, afflicts a
Duplicant
when their
Body Temperature
is high rather than low.

.
