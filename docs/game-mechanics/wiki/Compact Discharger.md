Compact Discharger
is a building that produces energy if provided with
Power Banks
.
Compact Dischargers produce more heat and require more space relative to power production compared to the Large Discharger but they may be rotated and mounted onto walls and ceilings. They turn idle when no more buildings require power, removing the need for a
Smart Battery
.
Trivia
An old design of Eco Power Bank can be seen on Discharger's icon.
