# Niobium Biome

This article is related to
Spaced Out
content.
The
Niobium Biome
is an extremely hot
Biome
exclusively found on the
Superconductive Asteroid
and containing no plants or critters. It contains veins of
Niobium
encased in
Obsidian
.
Strategy
Because of the extreme heat of this biome moving through it is an extreme challenge as most common materials will either melt or worse, evaporate. Avoiding direct contact with hot materials is a necessity. Thankfully no gasses are present by default, so maintaining a vacuum and being mindful of any
Magma
that may have found its way inside should make getting to the
Niobium
significantly easier.

Database
Niobium Biome
The Niobium Biome features only two resources yet, because
Niobium
is an extremely rare and valuable element, it is worth making a special visit.
By itself,
Niobium
is not a particularly useful resource. But if processed through a
Molecular Forge
, it produces the extremely thermal conductive
Thermium
, which goes a long way in solving many extreme temperature issues in a colony.
The edges of this biome are filled with
Obsidian
so a Duplicant with the Super-Duperhard Digging skill will be required before my colony can explore here.
