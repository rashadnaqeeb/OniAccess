The Ice Liquefier turns
Ice
into
25 °C
/
77 °F
Water
, in batches of 100 kg every 5 seconds, at the cost of
Wood
. It is mostly useful for
Advanced Research
and
Wash Basins
in Frosty Planet starts where Wood and Ice are abundant but liquid water is hard to come by.
On the starting special asteroids added by The Frosty Planet Pack, it can be unlocked thanks to the
Research Portal
.
The rate of wood consumption is based on the temperature of the ice being melted.  The amount of wood consumed is equal to 51.25 grams per batch (ie. per 5 seconds) per degree difference in temperature between the ice input and the output temperature of
25 °C
/
77 °F
.  For ice at exactly
0 °C
/
32 °F
, this equates to 1.28125 kg of lumber per batch.  Despite not stating it in the description, the Ice Liquefier outputs
Carbon Dioxide
gas to its environment equal to 14.2% of the mass of wood consumes (or 181.9375 grams of CO2 per batch for the above
0 °C
/
32 °F
ice), at the same temperature as the wood that was consumed as fuel.
Any
Germs
on the ice will be retained in the melted water, which can be potentially hazardous if the ice was handled by a duplicant with
Food Poisoning
germs, and the water is subsequently used in a
Microbe Musher
or used to irrigate
Bristle Blossom
by hand.  Germs on the wood used as fuel are deleted.
The Ice Liquifier will stop consuming fuel and melting ice once it contains 500 kg of melted water.  The meter built into the side of the Liquifier shows the amount of water it contains, out of the maximum 500 kgs it can hold, incrementing each time a 100 kg batch of water is melted.
