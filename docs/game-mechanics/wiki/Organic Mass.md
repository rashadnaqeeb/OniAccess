Organic Mass
is a space destination with a distance of 40,000 km. It is the only source of
Gassy Moos
and
Gas Grass Seeds
, which together can be used to convert liquid
Chlorine
into
Natural Gas
at a 1:10 ratio. Uniquely, it is composed of elements in
Gas
,
Liquid
and
Solid
phases where other destinations are composed entirely of Solids.
Utilization
The Organic Mass is composed of the gases
Carbon Dioxide
and
Polluted Oxygen
, which are collected by the
Gas Cargo Canister
, the liquid
Polluted Water
, which is collected by the
Liquid Cargo Tank
, and the solids
Slime
and
Algae
, which are collected by the
Cargo Bay
. A
Biological Cargo Bay
can be used to collect Gassy Moos and Gas Grass Seeds. The most efficient
Rocket
configuration for missions to the Organic Mass consists of 4
Liquid Fuel Tanks
and 6 cargo modules, and will return 1.66 kilograms of cargo for every 1 kilogram of
Petroleum
fuel.
