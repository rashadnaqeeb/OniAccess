# Biome

Biomes
are regions on any
Asteroid
or
Planetoid
, with each containing anywhere from 2 to 11 Biomes.  Some biomes can only be found on specific worlds, and some biomes are completely missing from games generated with certain settings
General
The game has 22 different biomes, each biome containing different
resources
,
critters
, and
plants
. Biomes are mostly separated by veins of
Abyssalite
or
Granite
.
Although each biome has a color shading applied to it that stays after the biome is mined out, there are no special properties beyond the contents of the biomes.
(As an example, the
Tundra Biome
does not cool itself, only starting out cold and cooling through
Wheezeworts
, which can be removed).

Biome Descriptions
Base Game
The Sandstone Biome
Sandstone Biome
Main article:
Sandstone Biome
Commonly refered to as the
Temperate Biome
, it is one of the possible starting biomes. It contains helpful early-game materials such as
Water
,
Dirt
,
Algae
, and
Coal
. It also contains both early-game plant species;
Mealwood
and
Bristle Blossom
. The
Shine Bugs
and
Hatches
that are found here also also useful critters for a colony just starting out.
Forest Biome
Main article:
Forest Biome
One of the possible starting biomes. It is a more challenging biome to survive in compared to the
Sandstone Biome
given less
Water
and the complete lack of
Algae
and
Coal
. The
Oxyfern
plants generated here are capable of turning
Carbon Dioxide
to
Oxygen
; although this oxygen generation is often insufficient to sustain a colony permanently. The presence of both
Arbor Trees
and
Pips
make early food production with
Mealwood
far more sustainable
Given there are less resources that are immediately useful to the player; branching out to other parts of the Asteroid is encouraged.
Barren Biome
Main article:
Barren Biome
A great early source of
Refined Iron
and
Coal
, the biome is rather
barren
beyond this; completely lacking life of any sort.
In
; some variants are more similar to variants of the
Forest Biome
than the
Barren Biome
; generating with
Aluminum Ore
,
Pips
, and
Arbor Trees
.
Tundra Biome
The Tundra Biome
Main article:
Tundra Biome
A frigid biome made primarily of
Snow
,
Ice
, and
Polluted Ice
.
Sleet Wheat
and
Wolframite
are also found here; and are incredibly useful. Wolframite is a precursur for the highest melting point metal in the game, and the highest overheat temperature modifier in the game; while Sleet Wheat is an excellent source of both early and late-game foods.
The
Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifier
can also be found here; which comsumes
Hydrogen
and deletes heat while running.
Jungle Biome
Main article:
Jungle Biome
Commonly refered to as the
Caustic Biome
, this is the home of the
Drecko
and
Pincha Peppernut
. Drecko's are an excellent source of
Reed Fiber
and
Plastic
. Pincha Peppernuts are a required ingredient in many late-game food options; notably
Spicy Tofu
and
Pepper Bread
. The atmosphere here is a mix of
Chlorine
and
Hydrogen
; both potentially very useful; especially in deep freezers.
Magma Biome
Main article:
Magma Biome
Appears at the very bottom of several asteroids or when specific
World Traits
are present. It contains large amounts of
Magma
,
Obsidian
, and small amounts of
Neutronium
which cannot be destroyed.
Marsh Biome
Main article:
Marsh Biome
Filled with
Polluted Oxygen
,
Polluted Water
, and
Slimelung
-infested
Slime
, this biome is also an excellent source of
Gold Amalgam
.
This biome is filled with organic material. Plenty of
Algae
along with all the
Slime
; its also home to
Pacu
,
Pufts
, and
Dusk Caps
. The
Thimble Reed
plants are one of the primary ways of obtaining
Reed Fiber
.
Ocean Biome
Main article:
Ocean Biome
Commonly referred to as the
Tide Pool Biome
, this is the home of the
Pokeshell
.
Fossil
,
Sand
, and
Salt Water
are very common here. There is also
Sedimentary Rock
here; which can be used to increase the chances of
Hatches
laying a Stone Hatchling Egg.
Waterweed
can be found here; which is required in the production of
Frost Burgers
.
Oily Biome
Main article:
Oily Biome
Often located near the bottom of the map; this biome is the primary source of
Crude Oil
; which is a precursur for several useful materials which will greatly help with game progression.
Slicksters
are also found here; which turn
Carbon Dioxide
into Oil. This biome is dangerous; the heat can easily scald
Duplicants
and the germs released by the
Sporechids
here can cripple a duplicant for several cycles.
If the asteroid has Irregular Oil
World Trait
during world generation, the
Oily Biome
may appear anywhere in the map.
Rust Biome
Main article:
Rust Biome
A chilly biome full of
Rust
,
Iron
, and
Chlorine
. Depending on variant, a wide range of plants and critters can be found; notably
Nosh Sprouts
and
Dasha Saltvines
; which are exclusive to this biome. The
Rust
in this biome can be combined with salt to produce
Oxygen
; great for when other options are impractical or impossible.
Space Biome
The Space Biome
Main article:
Space Biome
The surface of every asteroid. In the base game; it contains
Mafic Rock
,
Regolith
, and
Granite
in the upper layers, and
Igneous Rock
can be found in the lower layers. In
the surface makeup of planetoids varies greatly between planetoids.
Meteor Showers
can randomly happen and bring several useful materials to the asteroid.
Gases and liquids are sucked into the vacuum of space through the open background, deleting them, and in the absence of gases
heat
can not dissipate from materials and objects. Adding
Drywalls
helps to construct rooms that can hold air and liquid.
Added by Spaced Out!
Swampy Biome
Main article:
Swampy Biome
One of the possible starting biomes; this biome is filled with
Polluted Water
and
Polluted Oxygen
. With the amount of
off-gassing
materials that make up this biome, like
Polluted Dirt
and
Polluted Mud
, keeping a breathable atmosphere for your
Duplicants
is not difficult. Its important to note that the off-gassing does consume the tile it emits from; slowly. This can cause pools of liquid to suddenly open up and spill into unwanted areas as natural tiles disappear.
With
Pacu
spawning here; its also relatively simple to set up food production; and get a considerable amount of
Lime
in the process.
Aquatic Biome
Main article:
Aquatic Biome
Found exclusively on the
Water Asteroid
, it is the only source of
Graphite
, a resource used in the production
Fullerene
, which itself is the precursor of
Super Coolant
. The Biome is drowing in
Water
making traversal difficult and
atmo Suits
a requirement to get to any reasonable depth.
Lime
is also found in large amounts here.
Metallic Biome
Main article:
Metallic Biome
A large source of metal ores; notably
Gold Amalgam
. Also contains useful amounts of
Dirt
,
Coal
, and
Oxylite
.
Moo Biome
Main article:
Moo Biome
Found exclusively on the
Moo Asteroid
, it is the only biome where
Gassy Moos
and
Gas Grass
can be found. The temperature is cold enough to freeze chlorine; and there is no naturally available breathable gas.
Niobium Biome
Main article:
Niobium Biome
Found exclusively on the
Superconductive Asteroid
, it contains the only source of
Niobium
.
Radioactive Biome
Main article:
Radioactive Biome
A frigidly cold biome able to freeze
Carbon Dioxide
and
Chlorine
and in some cases even cause Frostbite; it is the only source of
Beetas
and thus the best conversion ratio of
Uranium Ore
to
Enriched Uranium
. This biome is also a source of
Wolframite
.
Regolith Biome
Main article:
Regolith Biome
Found exclusively on the
Regolith Asteroid
. Outside of Classic Starts; this is the only place where
Shove Voles
can be found.
Wasteland Biome
Main article:
Wasteland Biome
The home of the
Divergents
and the symbiotic
Grubfruit Plant
. The wasteland features a breathable atmosphere; making exploration easy; and is also filled with
Sulfur
; making ranching and cultivating the plants and critters here simple; although sustaining this will require a
Sulfur Geyser
at some point. There is also a diverse set of minerals contained here; which is unusual for biomes with most only having just one; not including the biomes border.
Added by The Frosty Planet Pack
Ice Cave Biome
Main article:
Ice Cave Biome
The starting biome for every Ceres start. It contains large amounts of
Snow
and
Ice
, with plenty of
Oxylite
spread about. The temperature means
Water
for research is difficult to obtain without an
Ice Liquefier
as melting the biome itself isn't recommended due to
Liquefiable
materials making up a vast majority of the biomes tile count and mass. The biome also contains both
Pikeapple Bushes
and
Floxes
; useful for
Wood
and
Phosphorite
generation in the cold.
Cool Pool Biome
Main article:
Cool Pool Biome
The Ceres alternative to the
Jungle Biome
. With a source of
Iron Ore
and
Reed Fiber
; its essential to exploit this to progress in the bitter cold. It also contains
Sucrose
which can be useful when ranching
Spigot Seal
.
Nectar Biome
Main article:
Nectar Biome
The Ceres alternative to the
Oily Biome
. It is dominated by frigid cold temperatures and is the home of the
Spigot Seals
and
Bonbon Trees
.
Nectar
from the trees can be turned into plastic directly though a
Polymer Press
or into
Ethanol
through Seal ranching.
Added by The Prehistoric Planet Pack
Garden Biome
Main article:
Garden Biome
The starting biome for every Relica start. It contains a variety of basic resources, including
Water
,
Dirt
, and
Peat
. It also has several unique plants, including sprawling
Ovagro Vines
,
Sweatcorn
, the unusual
Mimika Bud
and their
Mimikas
, and the non-propogating
Snactus
, and is also home to the
Lumb
. It is warmer than most starting biomes.
Feather Biome
Main article:
Feather Biome
The Relica alternative to the
Jungle Biome
/
Tundra Biome
. It has caverns filled with
Chlorine
, which are inhabited by
Rhexes
and their prey
Dartles
, which eat from the
Dew Dripper
plants. Chlorine-consuming
Megafrond
plants also grow here. This biome has cold temperatures and large deposits of
Iron Ore
.
Wetlands Biome
Main article:
Wetlands Biome
The Relica alternative to the
Marsh Biome
/
Oily Biome
. It shares much in common with the
Marsh Biome
, including the lakes of
Polluted Water
and their resident
Pacus
, the warm temperatures, and deposits of
Gold Amalgam
, which are sparsely present in this biome. Unlike the Marsh Biome, there is an absence of
Slime
,
Thimble Reeds
, and
Pufts
, instead being home to the carnivorous
Jawbo
, irritating
Gnits
,
Amber
-producing
Luras
, and aquatic
Seakomb
, an important industrial ingredient.
