Deep Fryer
allows Duplicants to cook high-quality food using Tallow.
Duplicants
will not fabricate unless recipes are queued. It requires Grilling
skill
and to be built inside a
Kitchen
.
The Deep Fryer is served by the
cooking
errand
, and utilizes
cuisine
attribute. A higher attribute level will reduce the time and energy required to complete a production cycle.
Food products fall to the ground after being produced and will
expire
if not
refrigerated
and placed in a sterile (oxygen/liquid-free) environment.
Deep Fryers can kill all
Food Poisoning
germs
in the ingredients.
