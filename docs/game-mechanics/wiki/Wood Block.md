Wood Block is a decorative building that does not need a floor, ceiling or wall as its base, and only occupies 1x1 of space. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +4 for Crude, +8 for Quaint, +12 for Masterpiece.
When decorated by a
Duplicant
with Masterworks
Skill
, it provides +15 decor in a radius of 4 tiles. When made out of
Wood
, this increases to 22.5.
