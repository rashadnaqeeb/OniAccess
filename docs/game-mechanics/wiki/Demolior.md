Demolior
is a one-time gameplay event that starts on playthroughs originating on the
Relica Asteroid
or
RelicAAAAAAAGHH Asteroid
. When
is active, it starts on the clusters
Relica Cluster
,
Relica Minor Cluster
or the
RelicAAAAAAAGHH Cluster
.
Behavior
A large Impactor Asteroid spawns from the
Temporal Tear
at the start of a playthrough and is set to collide with the players' asteroid. The amount of time it takes to collide with the players' asteroid can be configured in the Difficulty menu at the start of the game.

Starting on
RelicAAAAAAAGHH
will set the collision time to 10 cycles regardless of difficulty settings, but grants the unique achievement to unlock:
The Lab: Life Found A Way
Handling the Demolior
In order to reveal the potential impact zone (should Demolior collide) as well as an on-screen countdown until collision, the player must analyze the
Research Portal
spawned close to the Printing Pod.
(Note: not activating the Research Portal has no bearing on when Demolior impacts.)
Doing so will also reveal a point of interest (POI) showing a prebuilt
Meteor Blaster
(rummaging the nearby Suit Lockers will yield 4
Intracosmic Blastshots
), as well as unlocking the necessary researches to deal with the Demolior: the
Blastshot Maker
and
Meteor Blaster
.
In order to avoid collision, the player must shoot 100
Intracosmic Blastshots
at Demolior with a
Meteor Blaster
.
Blast Line of Defense Success
If the player succeeds in shooting down
Demolior
, then they are rewarded with 3
Space Destinations
, or up to 3
Space POIs
at the location where
Demolior
was destroyed.

Space POI Reward
Immediately after shooting down
Demolior
, a one-time
Iridium Meteor Shower
occurs which reigns down damaging
Iridium
meteors onto the players' asteroid (around
2090 kg
,
3135 kg
,
3355 kg
of
Iridium
total for Relica Minor, Relica Classic, and Relica Asteroid, respectively). Shooting down
Demolior
also unlocks the Blast Line of Defense
Achievement
if available.
In
, the space background on the starting asteroid is replaced with an asteroid belt. This is purely cosmetic.
Blast Line of Defense Failure
Should the player choose to ignore the threat, then a cutscene plays of a large potato-like asteroid demolishing the impact zone (
Note:
Story Traits
won't be generated inside the impact zone
). Not all is lost however - the impact zone reveals large amounts of
Iridium
to mine, and several potentially valuable geysers (Two
Volcanos
,
Hydrogen Vent
, and a
Iron Volcano
) should the player trek through the molten wasteland.
Everything caught in the impact zone will be destroyed. This includes Critters, Duplicants, other POIs, and Geysers. Duplicants removed in this manner will not trigger
Mourning
, although will fail the unique achievement
The Lab: Life Found A Way
.
In addition to replacing the impact zone, all geysers on the affected world are uncovered and revealed. Natural tiles within a 4-cell radius of the geysers'
Cell of Interest
are excavated leaving the geysers free to erupt. Natural tiles outside the 4-cell radius but within a 6-cell radius has a 50% chance to be excavated. Geysers are set to active and immediately leave Dormancy.
