Usage
Although it consumes more power than the
Lamp
and provides the same amount of light radius when placed on a 4-tiles high ceiling, the Ceiling Light has the invaluable ability to be placed in areas that aren't usually filled with other structures.
Bristle Blossom
growth requires Light.
The working speed of machine operation can be increased by +15% when lit.
Combined with
Thermo Sensor
, it can be useful for heating up
Plants
due to its low heat effect and ease of placement.
Mechanics
Ceiling Light provides its largest horizontal light coverage to the 5th and 6th level below it (11 tiles coverage width).
