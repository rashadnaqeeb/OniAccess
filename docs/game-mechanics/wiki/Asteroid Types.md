World Seed
World seeds define the maps layout. They are randomly generated, but if you loved the map you just played on and want to retry that seed, or share that world for your friend to play too, you can take that alphanumerical seed, enter it to the seed menu and get the same asteroid layout.
World Traits
Main article:
World Trait
Depending on the asteroid type and world seed, an asteroid may have a number of random traits which affect the
biomes
and terrain features which can generate there.
Story Traits
Main article:
Story Trait
Additionally, upon world creation, players can selectively enable or disable the generation of story traits, which are
lore
-focused points of interest that can provide unusual benefits to colonies.
Base
Standard Asteroids which tend to be somewhat similar to one another.
Terra
Main article:
Terra Asteroid
A temperate asteroid.
Relica
Main article:
Relica Asteroid
An asteroid added by
The Prehistoric Planet Pack
DLC with new
Biomes
.
Ceres
Main article:
Ceres Asteroid
An asteroid added by
The Frosty Planet Pack
DLC with new cold
Biomes
.
Oceania
Main article:
Oceania Asteroid
Similar to
Terra
, but with more
Salt Water
.
Rime
Main article:
Rime Asteroid
A uniformly cold asteroid with more
Biomes
and additional
Volcanoes
.
Verdante
Main article:
Verdante Asteroid
A
Forest
asteroid that lacks a
Tundra Biome
.
Arboria
Main article:
Arboria Asteroid
A colder variant of
Verdante
, with
Tundra Biomes
, but without
Marsh Biomes
.
Volcanea
Main article:
Volcanea Asteroid
A variant of
Terra
with a guaranteed magma vents, which makes it hotter.
The Badlands
Main article:
The Badlands Asteroid
Features large swaths of generic rocks and lacks
Biomes
with liquid
Water
.
Aridio
Main article:
Aridio Asteroid
A uniformly hot variant of
Verdante
.
Oasisse
Main article:
Oasisse Asteroid
The starting area is besieged by hot
Sand
, and there are no cold
Biomes
.
The Lab
These are special asteroid types that are more unique compared to their standard counterparts. These asteroids are accessed through a different style of gameplay called
The Lab
and unique challenges not seen on other asteroids.
Skewed
Main article:
Skewed Asteroid
“
A tilted world, at once strange and...strangely familiar. Its singular terrain is consistent across all universes.
This resistance to the fluidity of the temporal shifts has the cosmos buzzing, as individual colonies compare notes on successes, failures and the bizarre redistribution of resources.
„
A unique asteroid similar to
Terra
with the
Geodes world trait
, but which is rotated 90°; from the standard orientation. The
Magma Biome
is at the left of the map, whereas the usual
Space Biome
is at the right of the map; however, the top of the map is still the space access point for rocketry and meteors, since the sides and bottom of the map are bordered by
Neutronium
as on a regular map. Unique to this map, almost all
Ruins
and their contents (including the Gravitas Lobby, but excluding
Story Traits
and
Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifiers
) are also rotated sideways. There are some other unusual features on this asteroid, including (but not limited to) a pair of uncovered
Gold Volcanoes
within a chlorine/hydrogen gas pocket at the intersection of several biomes.
The "alert" welcome message at the beginning of the game is different for this asteroid, and initially appears rotated by 90°; itself:
“
This asteroid is oddly tilted, as though a powerful external force once knocked it off its axis.
I'll need to recalibrate my approach to colony-building in order to make the most of this unusual distribution of resources.
„

Main article:
RelicAAAAAAAGHH Asteroid
This asteroid is very similar to
Relica
with guaranteed
Geoactive and Volcanic Activity world traits
. The

impact happens in 10 cycles and cannot be adjusted by world difficulty. When the impact does happen, all
Geysers
on the asteroid will be uncovered and erupt immediately.

Impactor asteroid collision in 10 cycles!
My scans indicate that the impact will trigger the eruption of all geysers that surround our landing site. There are...so many.
Initiate survival procedures immediately.
„
