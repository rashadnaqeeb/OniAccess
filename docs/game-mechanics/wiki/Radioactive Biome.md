Spaced Out

The
Radioactive Biome
is a cold
Biome
mainly composed of
Uranium Ore
,
Ice
and
Wolframite
. Also includes
Beetas
,
Wheezeworts
,
Saturn Critter Traps
, and
Tranquil Toes
.
Strategy
The
Radioactive Biome
is cold but rarely enough to cause frostbite damage to
Duplicants
. The bigger issue is
Beetas
; as they will sting any Duplicants if given the chance; even when they are wearing an
Atmo Suit
. The best way to avoid this is running your game on max speed; as the lag caused by this makes critters do specific actions significantly slower (This is dependant on hardware though, so it is not reliable). The second best way is making sure dupes keep as little contact with them as possible; pneumatic doors work great for this.
Going full extinction on the Beetas isn't ideal however, as they aren't available in care packages and they are the most efficient source of
Enriched Uranium
. Leaving the entire biome alone until you need the resources contained within is a good strategy; as Beetas are capable of removing
Uranium Ore
from natural tiles at 100% efficiency; as opposed to a duplicants 50% efficiency.

Database
Radioactive Biome
A highly volatile environment containing a highly useful resource, this biome is invaluable when venturing into nuclear technologies.
Lead Suits
are imperative if my Duplicants are going to start exploring this biome as
Radioactive Contaminants
are a constant danger here.
Beetas
pose a double threat as they are both highly radioactive and very aggressive. If they can be subdued,
Beeta Hives
provide a great service turning
Uranium Ore
into
Enriched Uranium
.
While the Radioactive Biome, and the Beetas contained within it, should be avoided at all costs if my Duplicants do not have the correct protection, my colony will need to trek into this dangerous biome if we are going to build any higher-tier nuclear technologies.
