Garden Biome
The Garden biome is a temperate environment with beneficial critters and plants well-suited for
Farming
.
This biome is home to
Lumbs
, which assist in harvesting edible plants, and pollinator
Mimikas
which boost plant growth speeds.
Power
systems built in this biome will benefit from
Nickel Ore
and
Peat
deposits, and with plenty of
Shale
available, there is no lack of construction materials.
Colonists can rely on
Snactus
plants for life-sustaining calories in their early days.
Ovagro Nodes
become efficient
Food
crops when permitted to grow to their full extent, as their
Water
requirements do not increase with the number of
Ovagro Fig
-bearing vines.
New colonies should be able to survive here with relative ease.
