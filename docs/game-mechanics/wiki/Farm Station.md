# Farm Station

Farm Station
Produces Micronutrient Fertilizer to increase Plant growth rates.
Assigned Duplicants must possess the Crop Tending trait.
This building is a necessary component of the Greenhouse room.
This station only has an effect on crops grown within the same room.

A

Micronutrient Fertilizer is manufactured at a Farm Station based on "pull logistics", if a plant in a Greenhouse requires Farmer's Touch, a Duplicant will manufacture Micronutrients Fertilizer at the nearest available Farm Station to their own position.
They will then deliver the Micronutrient Fertilizer to a plant in a Greenhouse that needs it, and apply Farmer's Touch as a separate errand.
A Farm Station does not need to be enabled to turn a room into a Greenhouse and merely disabling the Farm Station is inadequate to stop Duplicants applying Farmer's Touch if there are other Farm Stations which they can use elsewhere. The Farm Station must be removed entirely or the room otherwise rendered not a Greenhouse.
As Duplicants attempt to use the nearest available Farm Station, disregarding how far it is and actual pathing distance, this can lead to Duplicants needlessly running around potentially to far away Farm Stations, centralizing Greenhouses with all Farm Stations being readily accessible to all Greenhouses can improve efficiency, especially if using multiple Farmers.
Duplicants will still fertilize a plant even it is ready to harvest or not currently growing due to environmental conditions or missing irrigation/fertilization, so prioritize harvesting to avoid wasting fertilizer (and plant growth time, for that matter).
Harvesting a plant does not interrupt the
Farmer's Touch
modifier, so plants with growth cycles less than the duration of the modifier are still worth fertilizing.
Analysis
The output of a single
Fertilizer Synthesizer
provides enough Fertilizer to boost 14.4 plants continuously, increased by +10% for each level of Agriculture of the Farmer(s) applying Farmer's Touch.
Each Micronutrient Fertilizer requires 41.667s of Fertilizer Synthesizer time, and costs 2.708 kg of
Dirt
, 1.083 kg of
Phosphorite
, and 1.625 kg of
Polluted Water

The cost of creating the necessary 5kg of
Fertilizer
using a Fertilizer Synthesizer is almost always cheaper, in terms of resources consumed per plant harvest, than feeding the crop for another cycle.
The resources needed for the Fertilizer Synthesizer are relatively common and easily produced:
Dirt can be produced from
Pips

Ethanol Distiller
via
composting

/
Slime

125 °C
/
257 °F
to produce Dirt 1:1, which can then be turned back into more Fertilizer, at the cost of the other two resources.
Phosphorite is excreted by
Dreckos

ranching

Mealwood

Pincha Pepper

Polluted Water can sourced from
