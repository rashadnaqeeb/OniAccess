Production
The initial quantities of Niobium can be obtained from
Space Destinations
. The destination's content has to be revealed with
Research Module
first to uncover Niobium deposits there. After that Niobium can be recovered as any
solid
resource with a
Cargo Bay
. In each game there is only one planet (Superconductive Asteroid) which yields Niobium metal in the base game.
Niobium can be found as a low-mass (about 27-30 kg) mineable tile throughout the
Superconductive Asteroid
, surrounded by
Obsidian
and
Magma
, making its extraction challenging.  A single
Niobium Volcano
can also be found on this asteroid, providing an infinite supply of the metal.
After the first batches of
Thermium
are produced it becomes possible to refine more Niobium from Thermium:
Metal Refinery
can produce more Niobium from
Thermium
in 1:1 proportion.
100 kg Thermium = 100 kg Niobium.
Rock Crusher
can produce Niobium and
Sand
from Thermium.
100 kg Thermium = 50 kg Niobium + 50 kg Sand.

The main usage of Niobium is to produce

5 kg Niobium + 95 kg

Thermium

Tips
Thermium can be converted into Niobium at 1:1 rate in the Metal Refinery. This is effectively converting 95 kg of Tungsten into Niobium.  As such, only 5 kg of Niobium is required to bootstrap further conversion.  Once this cycle is started, unless Niobium and Thermium are allowed to run out, the supply of the two is effectively only limited by the supply of Tungsten.
