Research Portal
A functional research decrypter with one transmission remaining.
It was designed to support colony survival.
Dimensions
3×3 tiles
Category
Buildings
Decor
+5 (Radius: 1 tiles)
Effects
Light Range
: 5 tiles
Brightness
: 1800 Lux
On the starting asteroids added by
The Frosty Planet Pack
(Ceres and Ceres Minor) and
The Prehistoric Planet Pack
(Relica and Relica Minor), one
Research Portal
will be spawned close to the Printing Pod.
Usage
The building emits a bit of light, similar to a smaller
Ceiling Light
originating from the top-middle tile. Even if it visually appears to turn off once the researches are unlocked, the portal still emits light and counts for the Laboratory room.
Ceres and Ceres Minor
Interacting with the Research Portal will instantly unlock the following researches:
Wood Heater
Ice Liquefier
Wood Tile
Relica and Relica Minor
Interacting with the Research Portal will instantly unlock the following researches:
Blastshot Maker
Meteor Blaster
It also plays a cutscene that:
Reveals the potential impact zone should
Demolior
collide.
Reveals a point of interest (POI) showing a prebuilt
Meteor Blaster
. Rummaging the nearby Suit Lockers will yield 4
Intracosmic Blastshots
.
Displays an on-screen countdown until collision.
Note: not activating the Research Portal has no bearing on when
Demolior
impacts.
Trivia
Despite the light visually turning off after the Research Portal is used, it will continue to emit light indefinitely.
