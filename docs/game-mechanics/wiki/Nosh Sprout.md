Per Duplicant (domesticated)
Assuming each duplicant eats 1,000 kcal per day.
Per plant, every 21 cycle wil gain 12 beans. In the same time, duplicant will have required 21,000 kcal.
As Tofu:
Netting 2 Tofu, for a total of 7,200 kcal. Thus you'll need ~2.92 Nosh Sprout per dupe, assuming none of the beans are taken to plant new sprouts.
As Spicy Tofu:
Netting 2 Spicy Tofu, for a total of 8,000 kcal. Thus you'll need 2.625 Nosh Sprout per dupe, assuming none of the beans are taken to plant new sprouts.
As Curried Beans:
Netting 3 Curried Beans, for a total of 15,000 kcal. Thus you'll need 1.4 Nosh Sprout per dupe, assuming none of the beans are taken to plant new sprouts.
As Nosh Noms:
Netting 2 Nosh Noms, for a total of 10,000 kcal. Thus you'll need 2,1 Nosh Sprout per dupe, assuming none of the beans are taken to plant new sprouts.

Water usage
When compared to
Sleet Wheat
, Nosh Sprouts are actually more water efficient.
Sleet wheat uses 20 kilograms of water during 18 cycles to produce 7200 Calories if making frost buns. 7200/18 = 400 Calories/20 = 20 calories per kilogram of water.
Nosh sprout uses 8.333 kg of polluted water per cycle per plant through an
Arbor Tree
, to produce 7200 Calories. 7200/21 = 342 calories/8.333 = 41 calories per kilogram of water, when taking into account the water used when making tofu the water total comes to 342 calories/13.1 = 26.11.. calories per kilogram of water.
Tips
If Tofu is seen as the base line amount of Calories, 3600 / 6, then each Nosh Bean provides 600 kcals.
This means that the net caloric gain of Spicy Tofu is +400 kcals.
Nosh Sprouts with Tofu provide a baseline 343 Calories per day, if the plants drop instead of being harvested it decreases to 288.
With Spicy Tofu this increases to 381, and 320 respectively.
Nosh Beans don't spoil if planted. This allows you to preserve the Nosh Beans indefinitely. Therefore, after the plant has grown, you may disable whatever it is planted on to preserve the food without having to fertilize/irrigate the sprout.
Database
Nosh Bean Plant
Edible Plant
The Nosh Bean Plant produces a nutritious bean that can function as a delicious meat substitute provided it is properly processed.
Though the bean is a food source, it also functions as the seed for the Nosh Bean plant.
While using the Nosh Bean for nourishment would seem like the more practical application, doing so would deprive individuals of the immense gratification experienced by planting this bean and watching it flourish into maturity.
