# Wasteland Biome

This article is related to
Spaced Out
content.
The
Wasteland Biome
is a temperate
Biome
and one of the few with a diverse selection of
Raw Mineral
.
Divergents
and
Grubfruit Plants
are found here.
Strategy
The
Wasteland Biome
generates as completely habitable, so exploring shouldn't be an issue. The
Divergents
and
Grubfruit Plants
are best ranched/grown together, as Divergents increase the yields of Grubfruit significantly. The biome also comes stocked with a large amount of
Sulfur
to make this easier. Sulfur itself is not easily renewable; only obtainable in significant amounts from a
Sulfur Geyser
which, while very likely to generate on most clusters, is not always guaranteed.

Database
Wasteland Biome
While the Wasteland Biome does not look particularly interesting, a pragmatic colony can take advantage of its selection of construction resources.
The prevalance of
Copper
,
Sandstone
,
Igneous Rock
and its
Iron
-rich counterpart
Mafic Rock
, make this a fruitful biome to explore for construction material.
Sand
is also in abundance here which is useful as a filtering material.
While the wildlife is not in abundance in the Wasteland Biome, the Sweetle and Grubgrub make interesting creatures to domesticate as they co-exist with the
Grubfruit Plants
to produce a much higher quality food than the
Spindly Grubfruit Plant
found in the wild. Additionally, the
Sulfur
found here works both as food for the GrubGrubs and fertilizer for the Grubfruit Plants.
The abundance of
Oxygen
found in the Wasteland Biome makes for a low-risk area to send my Duplicants into to collect useful resources to continue with their construction projects.
