The
Botanical Analyzer
, as the name implies, analyzes
mutated Plant
Seeds
. It requires a Duplicant with the
Improved Farming II Skill
to operate it. Unidentified Seeds can not be used even if the mutation they have has been analyzed on another seed before.
While requiring
Improved Farming II
, using the
Botanical Analyzer
is a research errand, using the Science Skill.
Tips
As a
Science Building
, the Botanical Analyzer receives a +10% efficiency bonus when placed inside a
Laboratory
.
The
Cell of Interest
of this building, where identified Seed will drop, is the far left one.
Since mutated Seeds are a potentially infinite resource, the Botanical Analyzer is a great way to have
Duplicants
gain Science skill, which, in turn, allows them to level up faster.
Bugs
The Botanical Analyzer does not actually consume Power or produce Heat.
Trivia
Whenever a Seed is dropped from a plant it will pop up as, for example
Mealwood (Specialized)
, even if that mutation was not yet analyzed. The seed, however, will still be named as
Mealwood (Unidentified Subspecies)
.
Certain plant modifiers still appear in the Item Infobox of the seed even when unidentified, which usually maps to only 1 possible Plant mutation.
