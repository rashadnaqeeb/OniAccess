Mechanism
Temporal Tear Opener requires 3 tiles line of sight to the sky, matching its width. It also needs a total of 5 printing pods built and activated on all asteroids. If these conditions are satisfied, it will be able to accept radbolts from its radbolt input, storing up to 1000 internally. It will consume 5 radbolts per second during the startup phase. When 10,000 radbolts are consumed, the player can fire it up, opening the Temporal Tear and causing a
Meteor Shower
.
