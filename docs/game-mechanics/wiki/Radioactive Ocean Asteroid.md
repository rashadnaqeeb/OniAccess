The Radioactive Ocean is a small asteroid with limited resources. The start is a
Forest Biome
which has a pool of
Polluted Water
and a few pools of
Water
. Most of the asteroid is made up of the
Ocean Biome
and
Radioactive Biome
.
Strategy
This asteroid presents a challenge due to its small size and lack of resources such as
Reed Fiber
,
Gold Amalgam
, and
Crude Oil
. None of which can be accessed through the teleporter.
Oxygen generation is done mainly through a
Electrolyzer
. Alternatively
Rust
can be found in the
Radioactive Biome
which can be used in a
Rust Deoxidizer
for
Oxygen
.
Food plants are limited to
Mealwood
,
Waterweed
and
Saturn Critter Trap
. For ranching there is
Pokeshells
,
Pips
,
Shine Bugs
, and a single
Hatch
.
Many building materials are blocked behind rockets, with the exception of
Iron Ore
, which can obtained via
Rust
. Rockets will be necessary to access certain resources like
Plastic
, which will be needed to create
Steam Turbines
.
This asteroid presents a unique resource challenge but can have considerable payoffs due to its large amount of
Water Geysers
and
Uranium Ore
.
