# Unreleased Content

There is a large amount of unreleased content for Oxygen Not Included. Some of these can occasionally be reached in game (such as Refined Metals), but many of them are only hinted at in the files, art assets, or developer streams.
Critters
Two critters are currently in the files but not in-game:
Name
Picture
Description
Volgus
A skittish mammalian creature. It moves in herds for safety.
Shockworm
Shockworms are exceptionally aggressive and discharge electrical shocks to stun their prey.
Ionizing Bug (Spaced Out)
Ionizing Bugs emit a dangerously radioactive Light in hopes of attracting more of their kind for company.
