Bugs
the right section of affected area having abnormal decor value.
When Great Monument is built, right section of decor affected area is not related to the monument. Thus when the area or the monument are updated, that section's decor value is continuously added. This value is not canceled even when the monument became incomplete or deconstructed until the save file is re-loaded.
The unbugged area of decor is 512 tiles, which means the game is coded such that subtractions of decor can only occur to 2^9 tiles simuntaneously.
