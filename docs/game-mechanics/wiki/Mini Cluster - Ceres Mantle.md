The
Ceres Mantle Mini Cluster
is a
Planetoid Cluster
with
Ceres Mantle
as the starting asteroid. It is the only
non-Moonlet Spaced Out! style cluster
with three inner asteroids instead of two.
Strategy
Among other minor differences, this cluster distributes the biomes and generated features of the starting
Ceres Minor Asteroid
of the
Ceres Minor Cluster
across a smaller starting asteroid and two inner asteroids, and makes expanding to those two inner asteroids harder than in most other clusters.
The duplicant teleporter on the
Sweet Ceres Asteroid
starts in the
Tundra Biome
, which is unusually inhospitable as a starting teleport biome, and all other biomes on the asteroid are either normally cold or cold variants, lacking liquid water; producing oxygen and keeping duplicants warm will be challenging without extra preparation. However, access to the
Nectar Biome
, and therefore to
Bonbon Trees
and
Spigot Seals
, and to a frozen variant of the
Oily Biome
, is gated behind establishing a presence on Sweet Ceres. This shouldn't be delayed for long, as there are no
Plastic Monomers
available on the starting asteroid.
An accessible way to establish a presence on Sweet Ceres is to teleport a duplicant on short trips every 5 cycles to dig a path to the
Supply Teleporter Output
, and then carry an initial supply of
Wood
and
Alveo Vera
seeds shipped in from the starting asteroid to near the teleporter receiver. The duplicant doing this will need Superhard Digging, and ideally wear an Oxygen Mask or Atmo Suit from their home asteroid even on the first visit, as well as a Warm Coat or be Frost Proof if not in an Atmo Suit. This will allow the initial permanent residents on Sweet Ceres to produce oxygen by farming, warm themselves with
Wood Heaters
, and produce water for a washroom with an
Ice Liquefier
. You may want to hold off on defrosting the Frozen Friend in the
Cryotank 3000
until this preparation is done.
The
Geothermal Power Plant
is generated on the
Ceres Core Asteroid
, which has a
Magma Biome
surface (and is the only asteroid among the starting and 3 inner asteroids in the cluster to have the Magma Biome). Breaking through the surface to establish a base and utilize the Geothermal Power Plant therefore presents a very similar challenge to the
Superconductive Asteroid
. It may make more sense to colonize the
Irradiated Swampy Asteroid
first, as that's the only inner asteroid with Uranium for early
Applied Sciences Research
.
