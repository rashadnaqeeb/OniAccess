Database
Dasha Saltvine
Edible Spice Plant
The Dasha Saltvine is a unique plant that needs large amounts of salt to balance the levels of water in its body.
In order to keep a supply of salt on hand, the end of the vine is coated in microscopic formations which bind with sodium atoms, forming large crystals over time.
Trivia
The name "dasha saltvine" may be a reference to the phrase "dash of salt", similarly to the
Pincha Pepper
.
