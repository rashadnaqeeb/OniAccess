Beetiny
A Beetiny is the larval stage of a Beeta. This creature crawls near the hive, being unable to jump up or down one tile and unable to attack. Upon reaching age two, it turns into a Beeta if there is a nearby Beeta Hive. If instead are no accessible Beeta Hives on nightfall, and there is sufficient space for a hive, it turns into a
Beeta Hive
that matures in three cycles. A Beeta Hive can be formed at any time during the night hours (schedule blocks 22-24), and this means when a Beetiny is relocated to a place that satisfies the conditions during these hours, it will immediately form a Beeta Hive.
Unlike the mature Beeta, Beetinys are wranglable.
Printerceptor Story Trait
Beetiny is the only live creature that can be printed from the
Printerceptor
, and as such has unique considerations when printing.  Due to the low livable temperature range, the beetiny will die quickly after being printed if the area around the printer is not cooled to sufficient temperatures or wrangled immediately.  Additionally the beetiny must be in a location with enough unoccupied space to form a beeta hive, or it will transform into an adult Beeta instead.
Diet and Pacification
Beeta flies around gathering
Uranium Ore
to feed into its hive. The structure then converts Uranium into
Enriched Uranium
and Nuclear Waste.
When Carbon Dioxide gas is present, Beetas and Beetinys enter a sleeping state and consume it at 100 g/s. This allows for preventing stings when harvesting Enriched Uranium from a Beeta Hive. The critters can be prevented from sleeping by submerging them in a small amount of liquid, allowing them to grow up normally with constant sleeping, and concentrating of Beetas into a nearby sleeping spot for radiation harvesting.

Radioactivity
Beetas pose a significant radioactive hazard when sleeping, as each Beeta puts out 1440 rads/cycle, while each Beetiny puts out 480 rads/cycle. and
Lead Suits
are recommended for Duplicants to avoid radiation poisoning. The consumed rate of Carbon Dioxide and the radiation emitted are affected by critter
Metabolism
. For example, if a Beeta is under overcrowded state, the diet rate would decrease.
Database
Beeta
Aggressive Critter
Beetas are insectoid creatures that enjoy a symbiotic relationship with the radioactive environment they thrive in.
Much like the honey bee gathers nectar and processes it to honey, the Beeta turns
Uranium Ore
into
Enriched Uranium
through a complex process of isotope separation inside the Beeta Hive.
When first observing the Beeta's enrichment process, many scientists note with surprise just how much more efficient the cooperative combination of insect and hive is when compared to even the most advanced industrial processes.

Notes
↑
The nuclear waste drops as a liquid at the temperature of the Beeta at the time of death, which will typically be lower than the freezing point of nuclear waste. It may solidify into debris or natural tiles, and can persist as a supercooled liquid indefinitely if heat transfer is prevented, such as with neutronium or airflow tiles in a vacuum.
↑
Contrary to popular belief, Atmo Suits and Lead Suits do
not
prevent Duplicants from being stung by Beetas. While Beetas may frequently ignore dupes, this is usually because of game lag causing the Beetas to not notice fast moving Duplicants in time to path to them.
