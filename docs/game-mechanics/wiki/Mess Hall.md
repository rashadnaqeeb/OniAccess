Description
The
Mess Hall
is a special room that is good for relieving a bit of extra stress when used. It needs to be at least twelve tiles big in area and needs at least one mess table, but more mess tables work well too.
Tips
Creating a dedicated dining area or mess hall is highly recommended. Making this area much nicer than the rest of your colony is an easy way to reduce stress. Lavishing the area with lamps, art, and high decor items amplifies the de-stressing effect. Including a
Ration Box
or ideally a
Refrigerator
with the setup saves time and stores food efficiently. Making a mess hall close to food production areas saves Duplicants the hassle of having to transport food long distances.
