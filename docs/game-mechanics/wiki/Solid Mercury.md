Solid
Refined Metal

Usage
Solid Mercury can be used as a Refined Metal to construct
Buildings
.
Due to their especially low melting point, buildings made using Solid Mercury are highly prone to melting in habitable temperatures. Therefore, using Solid Mercury as a construction material outside of temperature-controlled environments is strongly discouraged.
Rocket Platforms
, as well as rockets themselves, do not exchange heat with their environment, making Solid Mercury a safe Refined Metal to use for these. Care must be taken to prevent the Solid Mercury from melting in transit, however.
Solid Mercury can also be fed to
Plug Slugs
at a rate of 60kg per cycle to produce 3kg of
Hydrogen
per cycle, or they can be fed to
Smog Slugs
and
Sponge Slugs
at a rate of 30kg per cycle to produce 1.5kg of Hydrogen per cycle.
