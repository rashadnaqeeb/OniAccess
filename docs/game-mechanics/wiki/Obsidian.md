Obsidian
is a
Mineral
found in the
Barren Biome
and
Volcanic Biome
, and infrequently in the
Frozen
and
Caustic
biomes. Maps with any of the Boulders
World Traits
have Obsidian boulders scattered randomly outside the starting area. It is also found in small amounts blocking
Geysers
. A
Duplicant
must learn the Superduperhard Digging
skill
to dig Obsidian, making it a significant impediment in the early game.
Obsidian can also be obtained by deconstructing
mosaic Tiles
found in
Ruins
, many of which are made of Obsidian. This does not require any Skills, and is thus a very easy way to obtain moderate amounts of Obsidian.
Usage
Obsidian abundance can vary significantly depending on world generation. It is most common on worlds with a Boulders
World Trait
and especially on
The Badlands Asteroid
. Despite this, it has limited applications.
It does not provide a decor bonus and is therefore inferior to
Sandstone
and
Granite
in regards to
decor
affecting buildings.
Most of its value as a construction material can be derived from its low thermal volume, high thermal conductivity, and Thermally Reactive trait, which means it will rapidly heat or cool - encouraging the conduction of
heat
between areas and substances in contact.
It also has a higher melting point than most other natural rocks and
metals
, making it possible to even build
ladders
and tiles into
magma
and contain
volcanoes
with it, or pipes that are near impossible to melt.
