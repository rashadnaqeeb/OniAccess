Power Bank Charger is used to charge
Empty Eco Power Banks
. The building consumes 480 W of
power
, but only delivers 400 W into the Eco Power Bank (a -80 W inefficiency). As Eco Power Bank has a capacity of 120 kJ, charging one will take 300 seconds, or half a Cycle.
A Bionic dupe uses 200 W, draining one Power Bank per cycle. If a Charger could run at a perfect 100% of the time, one Charger could therefore meet the power needs of two dupes. Because it takes a short amount of time to refill the Charger with empties, in practice you need one more Charger than the math indicates. In other words: Chargers needed = (bionic dupe count / 2) + 1
