Sink
is a
Medicine
Building
that cleanses
Duplicants
of
Germs
. Duplicants will use a Sink when passing by in the direction of the arrow, similar to a
Wash Basin
. However, Sinks do not have to be filled and emptied by a Duplicant and have a liquid input and output for
Pipes
instead.
Germs are removed from the Duplicant at around 20,000 germs per second while in use. This happens even if the action bar doesn't complete, which can be
abused
.
Sink has a Set Direction setting that determines which direction/s Duplicants will wash their hands when passing by.

per use and converts it to

Duplicants are cleaned of germs even if the input Water contains germs.
