Critter

Only Duplicants with the

It's designed for land critters, but flopping fish sometimes find their way in too.

Critter Trap
is a
building
that will catch non-flying
critters
that walk into the trap.
Duplicants
can then take the bagged creature to a nearby
Critter Drop-Off
.
A critter is captured only when it enters the bottom left tile of Critter Trap.
Gold Amalgam
,
Steel
or better should be used if one aims to capture
Slicksters
since their natural habitat may be well over the overheat temperature of Critter Traps made from lesser materials. Alternatively, collecting their
eggs
or building a
stable
downside might be a simpler option.
Critter traps are capable of catching flopping
Pacu
.
Tips
Critter Traps are reusable and can be deconstructed to refund their material cost, like most other buildings.
The older version of this building, created in versions of the game prior to the
Song of the Moo Update
, was single-use only and did not refund its material cost; it was also constructed from 200 kg of
Plastics
, which presented difficulties when used in hot environments.
