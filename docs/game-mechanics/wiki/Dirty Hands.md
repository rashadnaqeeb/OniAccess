# Dirty Hands

The subject of this article was removed from the game in update
OU-229531
.
* The in-game information in this article is kept purely for historical purposes.
Dirty Hands
was a status effect that duplicants gain after a certain amount of time in the colony. It increases the likelihood of catching
Food Poisoning
by a negligible amount (0.0000005% chance, added to an existing 10% chance), and doubles the chance of catching
Diarrhea
(which is currently bugged and can only be acquired by Duplicants with the Iron Gut trait). The effect can be removed by using a
Hand Sanitizer
. The Hand Sanitizer's flavor text claims that "the only thing filthy hands are good for is picking up disease".
