rocket

The stored fuel type is determined by the rocket engine it is build upon.
Storing additional fuel increases the distance a rocket can travel before returning.

Liquid Fuel Tank
is required for the rocket to launch and can be filled with 900 kilograms of Liquid Fuel each. It will come back empty regardless of where it is taken to land or how many other modules were attached besides it.
Liquid Fuel Tank is required by
Petroleum Engine
and
Hydrogen Engine
, and can store
Petroleum
and
Liquid Hydrogen
accordingly. You may adjust the storage capacity between 0 kg and 900 kg.
Duplicants will remove excessive fuel and drop bottles of fuel.
