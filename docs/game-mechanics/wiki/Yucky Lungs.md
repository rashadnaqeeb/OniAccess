The
Yucky Lungs
effect is given to
Duplicants
who breathe too much
Polluted Oxygen
. It gives Air Consumption Rate +30g/s, lasting for 0.2 cycle.
It also causes
Yucky Lungs Coughing
effect, which will throw
Duplicants
in coughing fits.
