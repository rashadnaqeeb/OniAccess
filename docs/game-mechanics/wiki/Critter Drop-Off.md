# Critter Drop-Off

Critter Drop-Off
Releases trapped Critters back into the world.
Can be used multiple times.
Duplicants automatically bring captured critters to these relocation points for release.

Critter Drop-Off
allows
duplicants
to move a captured
critter
to a different location. Creatures can be captured using a
Critter Trap
and a duplicant with Ranching skill or by being wrangled directly by a duplicant with Ranching
skill
.
It is possible to set a limit of Critters. Once the room is filled with that many critters no more will be dropped off. However, if new critters hatch from eggs, any excess of critters need to be handled by the
Critter Pick-Up
.
Critter Drop-Off is not required for the
Stable
and can be placed anywhere.
Tips
This building has a base overheat temperature of
75 °C
/
167 °F
and will overheat in
Oil Biome
if built with
Copper Ore
or
Iron Ore
. Choose
Gold Amalgam
as the building material instead.
The Drop-Off limit cannot be higher than 40 critters. To go higher, separate rooms with separate Critter Drop-Offs are required.
The Critter Drop-Off is considered "flooded" in any amount of liquid and will then cease to function; keep dry to avoid this.
