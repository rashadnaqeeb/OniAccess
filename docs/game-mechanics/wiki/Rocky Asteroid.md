Rocky Asteroid
is a space destination with a distance of 20,000 km. It is composed of roughly equal amounts of
Raw Metals
, in the form of
Copper Ore
and
Iron Ore
, and
Raw Minerals
, in the form of
Sedimentary Rock
and
Igneous Rock
.
Utilization
The Rocky Asteroid is a renewable source of Raw Metals and Raw Minerals. It is located at a distance of 20,000 kilometers. The optimal
Rocket
configuration for a space mission to the Rocky Asteroid consists of either 4 cargo modules and 2
Liquid Fuel Tanks
, or 6 cargo modules and 3 Liquid Fuel Tanks, both returning 2.22 kilograms of cargo (if using
Cargo Bays
) for every 1 kilogram of
Petroleum
fuel.
Using a
Biological Cargo Bay
will return
Stone Hatches
.
Tips
The similarly distant
Metallic Asteroid
provides a greater quantity of the same metals in their refined rather than raw form. As such, missions to the Rocky Asteroid to acquire Raw Metal to refine are counterproductive.
It is, however, the only renewable source of ore in the event that it is needed for repairs.
