# Plastic Ladder

Plastic Ladder
Increases Duplicant climbing speed.
Plastic ladders are mildly antiseptic and can help limit the spread of germs in a colony.

; They are used to grant access to the different floors of the colony, as well as functioning as scaffolding structures for various projects throughout the base.
Usage
Duplicants can move
vertically on ladders at full speed, and go on top of ladders as if it was a tile. They can also hang on to a ladder to go horizontally without tile access, and jump sideways between ladders
This allows Ladders to be used for vertical movement and gas/liquid-permeable scaffolding. However, Ladders take much longer to construct than
Tile
, making them a poor option for urgent situations.
Ladders slightly decrease
Decor
, disincentivizing overuse of them in environments where Decor matters.
From any given Ladder, Duplicants can
dig in their construction range
: This means it is possible to efficiently dig out large areas by placing Ladders in rows (skipping one tile between each) that are 7 tiles apart.
Duplicants cannot climb onto a ladder right under the ceiling, the ladder must be 1 tile away from the ceiling in order to allow passage.
Tips
Plastic melts at
159.9 °C
/
319.82 °F
, so avoid using Plastic Ladders in hot environments, or build them using
Plastium
.
Although the tooltip says plastic is antiseptic, it has no in-game effect.
While ladders cannot be built over rocket modules or engines, they can certainly be built after a rocket is launched, it's recommended to build a long line of ladders one tile apart from the end of your gantry platforms after a rocket launches in order to give duplicants full access to any rocket module. However, since your colony's need for rockets may change over time, it's best to use a widely available and heat resistant material like Igneous Rock which can survive up until Hydrogen Rocket's exhausts.
