Stinko Swamp Asteroid
A large-ish, polluted swamp world.
Stinko Swamp Asteroids are full of swampy and marshy areas, but also contain a variety of other biomes diverse enough to keep a determined colony supplied with essentials.

The inelegantly named
Stinko Swamp Asteroid
is relatively small but rich in resources. It contains pockets of
Polluted Water
, abundant
Slimelung
, several
Ruins
, and several plants and animals not found on cold Planetoids.

Example Radioactive Swamp Asteroid Layout
Strategy
None of the biomes that make up this asteroid contain significant amounts of easily accessible clean water. There is plenty of mud, polluted water, and polluted oxygen. Deodorizers are essentially a requirement, and it would be wise to find a vent which emits steam or clean water as soon as possible.
