Hydrogen Engine
Burns Liquid Hydrogen to propel rockets for space exploration.
The engine of a rocket must be built first before more rocket modules may be added.
Hydrogen engines can propel rockets further than steam or petroleum engines.

Tips
A rocket chimney spanning the entire height of the asteroid allows getting more water than is used to launch the rocket (after using an electrolyzer to generate fuel) and getting a consequent amount of power. Rough numbers estimate above 12 kW of power and above 1000 g/s of water per rocket when launching to the closest destination.
