Critter Feeder
automatically dispenses food for hungry
Critters
. Critters tend to stay close to their food source and will wander less when given a Feeder. It can store up to 2000 kg of critter food. Critters will prioritize eating from a Critter Feeder over debris.
Usage
The following critters (and their variants) can feed from critter feeder:
Hatch
Shine Bug
Pokeshell
Shove Vole
Divergent
For each critter type, you can choose which materials that critter can get from the critter feeder. The critter feeder will only store chosen materials and/or foods.
