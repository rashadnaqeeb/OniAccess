# Advanced Research

This article is about the type of research points. For the research node, see
Advanced Research (Research)
. For the skill, see
Skills
.
Advanced Research
is the second tier of
research
points used in
research
. Advanced Research is required for all tier 3 and higher
research
categories.
Advanced Research is obtained at a
Super Computer
. Each Advanced Research costs 50 kg of
water
. Only dupes with the skill
Advanced research
can conduct this type of research.
