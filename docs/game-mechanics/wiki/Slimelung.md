Slimelung
is a type of
Germ
that is contracted through the inhaled air, while it impedes
duplicant
productivity, it is not lethal. Generally, it is abundant in the
Swamp Biome
. Slimelung infestation serves as incentive to purify
Polluted Oxygen
, which is otherwise almost as harmless as clear
Oxygen
.
Sources
Slimelung germs can be found in the
Slime
and
Polluted Oxygen
of the
Swamp Biome
.
Slimelung multiplies quickly in
Slime
and
Polluted Oxygen
, but dies in/on most other substances. Large clouds of Polluted Oxygen may quickly become infected with dangerous amounts of this germ.
Slime
tiles are generally generated infected and quickly infect surrounding solid tiles, then infect the nearby gases with puffs of infected Polluted Oxygen once dug out.
Morbs
can periodically emit a cloud of 200 g of
Polluted Oxygen
infected with 200 germs of Slimelung.
Infection
When Duplicants breathe the
Polluted Oxygen
infected with Slimelung germs, they have a chance to be infected at the start of next cycle.
While inhalation and surface contact with heavily contaminated materials can quickly infect a
duplicant
, consuming items with Slimelung has no effect (beyond the exposure from handling it).
Symptoms
While ill, the duplicant will lose air at faster rate (-1% per second) than they passively regain it, forcing them to forcefully cough once in a while. This stacks with inability to breathe due to lack of
Oxygen
in air, causing affected duplicants to be particularly badly suited to operating in low-oxygen environments. Additionally, ill duplicants will cough occasionally, producing a cloud of polluted oxygen at their feet containing 1000 germs each time. This is rather inconsequential due to low number of germs, but the coughing may interrupt jobs or sleep and can infect other duplicants who breathe in the polluted oxygen.
Mitigation
Deodorizers
can purify
Polluted Oxygen
into
Oxygen
, and reduce the germ number. The deodorizer does not remove the germs directly but the clean air will kill the Slimelung over time.
Duplicants carrying Slime past an
Ore Scrubber
will stop and clean it, however slime often starts with up to 900,000 germs of Slimelung, and a single Scrubber kills 480,000 germs, meaning the Slime will need to be cleaned twice to be fully disinfected.
Duplicants wearing
Atmo Suits
or
Oxygen Masks
do not breathe the environment gas and are immune to germs.
Radiation Lamp
and
Wheezewort
can be used to create radiation, which kills Slimelung over time. In order to achieve a net decrease in germ count, most natural slime tiles need to be exposed to at least 16-20 rads/cycle, and objects with a higher number of germs need more radiation. Other sources of radiation that might be effective at dealing with Slimelung include Cosmic Radiation and Crashed Satellites, or even
Shine Bugs
if they stay close enough to objects infected with Slimelung.
Treatment
Slimelung can be treated with a
Medical Pack
crafted at an
Apothecary
, which must be administered by a duplicant with the Bedside Manner skill in a
Sick Bay
. A duplicant infected by
Slimelung
will go to the
Sick Bay
when both Medical Pack and a qualified doctor is available. The MedPack, once administered, will completely cure the dupe, who'll then have antibodies against the disease in their system for a while.
If left untreated, a duplicant will fight off the disease themselves within 4
cycles
.
