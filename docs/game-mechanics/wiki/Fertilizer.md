Fertilizer
is an Agriculture Resource. It is found in the
Temperate Biome
, in tiles of 50 kg around
Water
.
Properties
Fertilizer is a very soft material, having a hardness of 2. At
125 °C
/
257 °F
it melts into
Dirt
.
Usage
Duplicants
with Crop Tending Skill can use Fertilizer to increase
Plants
growth rate in
Farm Station
.
Sage Hatch
can eat up to 140 kg of Fertilizer per cycle and excrete
Coal
with a 1:1 ratio.
Fertilizer can be used as
solid oxidizer
for
rockets
. Although its efficiency is the least among oxidizers, Fertilizer is far easier to handle than the others.
However, rockets can consume hundreds of kilos of it on just one trip; those hundreds of kilos could have been used for farms across many cycles.

Tips
Fertilizer increases plants growth rate by +100% for one cycle. It can be useful on plants with long growth cycles or high resource requirements for normal growth, as the resource requirement doesn't double.
Fertilizer has no effect on
Mealwood
.
