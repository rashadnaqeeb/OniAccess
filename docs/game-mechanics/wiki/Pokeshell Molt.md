# Pokeshell Molt

Pokeshell Molt
Can be crushed to produce
Lime
Type
Industrial Ingredient
Pokeshell Molt
is an
Industrial Ingredient
item produced by
Pokeshells
. It is a renewable source of
Lime
for
Steel
production.
Usage
Rock Crusher
can produce
Lime
from Pokeshell Molt.
10 kg Pokeshell Molt = 10 kg Lime.
Production
Pokeshells can produce Pokeshell Molt. A young Pokeshell produces a 5 kg Pokeshell Molt and an adult Pokeshell produces 10 kg.
Notes
Pokeshell Molt is an item made of
Genetic Ooze
.
