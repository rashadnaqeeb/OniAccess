Behavior
A
Lumb
will travel to any plant where any of the plant body can intersect with its 2x2 body - It begins a stomping motion, harvesting
all
plants that are mature in its 2x2 vicinity. There is a 60 second delay before it can stomp again.  Lumbs can also harvest downward-hanging plants, such as
Pincha Peppernut
and
Dew Dripper
, provided the bottom cell of the plant is no more than 1 tile away from the ground.
A Lumb will periodically express themselves with a Roar emote. This is cosmetic and has no in-game effects.

Database
Lumb
Domesticable Critter
While other creatures with the Lumb's low forebrain neuron count rapidly become extinct, these docile creatures are uniquely positioned for survival.
Their large size and spiny protrusions make them unappealing to most predators. Anecdotal evidence also suggests that smaller species will sometimes adopt orphaned Lumbs.
Some experts theorize that this may be motivated by a desire to benefit from the Lumb's genetic muscular condition (akin to restless leg syndrome), which causes them to regularly pound the earth in such a way as to cause nearby plants to drop desirable foods.
