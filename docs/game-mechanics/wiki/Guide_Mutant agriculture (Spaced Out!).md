# Guide/Mutant agriculture (Spaced Out!)

Plant
mutation is a mechanic introduced in the
Spaced Out!
DLC. Plants exposed to
Radiation
have a chance of dropping mutated seeds, which alter the plant's yield and requirements.
Mutation Efficiency
Mutation stats are presented as additive modifiers, which can be deceptive when determining efficiency. A non-mutated plant produces yield
Y
after it consumes
F
units of fertilizer per cycle for
L
cycles. In other words, a plant produces
Y
/
FL
units of output per unit fertilizer consumed - in particular, this means that decreasing plant life cycle essentially decreases fertilizer consumption, as it consumes for fewer cycles.
A
mutated
plant has a modifier applied to each of these values: for instance, a Wildish plant has the yield remain the same, but increases life cycle by 350% (
L
m
=4.5
L
) and decreases fertilizer by 90% (
F
m
=0.1
F

Y
/(4.5
L

F

Y
/(0.45
LF
) = 2.22
Y
/
LF
