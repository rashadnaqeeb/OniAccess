Sunburn is a
disease
caused by prolonged exposure to light brighter than 72000
Lux
. Once contracted, it takes 1.7
cycles
(1020 seconds) to recover. The effect is a 20%/cycle increase in
stress
for the duration, as well as the duplicant pausing every 3-5 seconds to emote about their affliction.  This temporarily interrupts any work task action they were taking.  This occurs even in
Transit Tubes
, with the duplicant abruptly halting in the middle of the tube to emote, then just as suddenly resuming their journey.  This regular interruption makes them substantially less efficient at working and commuting around for the duration of the effect.
It has a weaker preceding status, "Intensely Bright", that provides a 5%/cycle decrease in stress.  The presence of the "Intensely Bright" status also indicates that the duplicant is accruing exposure for sunburn.  After 120 seconds of exposure, this status is replaced with Sunburn.  Exposure time is cumulative, and will only decrease when in an entirely unlit area, at a rate of 1 second of exposure per 4 seconds in an unlit area.  Time spent in a lit area below 72000 Lux will neither increase nor decrease the current level of exposure.  While a duplicant has a sunburn, further exposure has no effect, and the duplicants accrued exposure time is reset to 0 upon recovering from sunburn.
It may be caused by other sources of light besides the sun, if they are sufficiently bright.
