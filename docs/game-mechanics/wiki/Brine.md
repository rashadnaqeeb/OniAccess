Brine
Brine is a natural, highly concentrated solution of Salt dissolved in Water.
It can be used in desalination processes, separating out usable salt.

Tips
A tile of
Brine Ice
can be created by freezing brine. To form a tile of brine ice by freezing, the mass of the brine must exceed 800 kg. If the tile is 800 kg or less it will fall as debris.
Brine Ice is the best ice to use if you wish to create a thermal ice block.  Unlike other waters it does not get its specific heat capacity halved when frozen so has the best specific heat capacity of all waters and it also can fit up to 1200 kg in a single tile.
