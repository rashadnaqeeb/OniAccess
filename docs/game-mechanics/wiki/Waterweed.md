Wild
A wild Waterweed takes 48 cycles to grow to maturity and produces an averaged 100 kcals per cycle when harvested, meaning you need 10 plants per duplicant.
Domestic
A domestic Waterweed takes 12 cycles to grow to maturity. Producing 400 kcals per cycle when harvested meaning you need 2.5 plants per duplicant.
