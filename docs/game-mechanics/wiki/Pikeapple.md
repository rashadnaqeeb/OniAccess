Pikeapples

Pikeapple Bushes

Critters
.
Usage
Duplicants
can eat raw Pikeapples to restore Calories.
Pikeapples can also be fed to Critters.
Floxes
eat Pikeapples at a rate of 160kcal/cycle and convert them into 5kg/cycle of
Dirt
.
Hatches
eat Pikeapples at a rate of 700kcal/cycle and convert them into 656.3g/cycle of
Coal
.
Sage Hatches
eat Pikeapples at a rate of 700kcal/cycle and convert them into 875g/cycle of Coal.
