# Tile (Ruins)

This article is about the non-buildable tile found in
ruins
. For the buildable tile, see
Tile
. For a list of other solid tiles, see
Category:Tiles
.
Tile
Used to build the walls and floor of rooms.
Dimensions
1×1 tiles
Category
Ruins
Decor
+10 (Radius: 2 tiles)
Deconstructible
Yes
Material
100
kg
Raw Mineral
Tiles
(sometimes called
mosaic tiles
) are a type of
Building
only found in
Ruins
, unlike normal
Tiles
, and are not buildable. They are made of 100 kg of
Raw Mineral
, usually
Obsidian
but sometimes other minerals, and can be deconstructed to reclaim their materials. Unlike regular Tiles, they
do not
increase running speed at all; they also provide twice the
Decor
as regular Tiles.
Deconstructing these Tiles is a good source of
Obsidian
, which can be difficult to obtain in large quantities in many biomes, especially in the earlygame.
