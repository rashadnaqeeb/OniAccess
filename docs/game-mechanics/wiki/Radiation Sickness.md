A Duplicant suffering from major or extreme Radiation sickness will not go to minor or major Radiation Sickness if they fall under 300 absorbed Rads, but either severity of Radiation Sickness gets healed once the Absorbed Rad Dose in a Duplicant sinks below 100 rads.
Exploits:
If a Duplicant emerges from an Escape Pod or Trialblazer Pod their absorbed rads (along with many other properties) will be reset. "Exploding rocket therapy" is an effective way to heal a duplicant with severe radiation sickness.
Radiation sickness status is not saved, so saving and loading the game will recalculate a Duplicant's radiation sickness based on their absorbed rads dose, which can heal severe or major radiation sickness faster than waiting for it to drop all the way to 100 rads.
