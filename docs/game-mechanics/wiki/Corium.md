Corium is only made in small amounts during
Reactor meltdowns
. The Corium is ejected from the Reactor and impacts with things, forming rock tiles.
Usage
Corium
has no use in the current game except for its unique thermal properties, which can be utilized for solid based cooling system.
However, since Corium cannot be used as a building material, the Corium must be used on a
Conveyor Rail
, or the cooling system has to be made close to the site of the reactor meltdown.
