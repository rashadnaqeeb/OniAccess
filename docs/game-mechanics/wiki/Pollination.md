Pollination
is a symbiotic interaction between
plants
and certain
critters
species, which benefits plant growth.
Pollination
of each pollinator is mutually exclusive. Only one
Pollination
effect can be active on a
plant
before it expires. A
pollination
effect with a greater effectiveness, such as those given by
Grubgrubs
, will not overwrite those with a lesser effectiveness, such as those given by
Sweetles
, and vice versa.
Some plants rely on pollinators in order to grow at all, while others receive a valuable acceleration to their natural growth speed.
Pollinators
Mimika
(+25%)
Divergents
(both Sweetles +5%  and Grubgrubs +50%)
