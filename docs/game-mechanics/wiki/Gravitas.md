# Gravitas

Gravitas was an Earth technology company that conducted experimental and often controversial projects with great success. However, it was destroyed along with the rest of the Earth during the events instigated at Gravitas Facility and leading up to the game.
Company
The logo of Gravitas was a stylized strand of DNA, shaped to look like an hourglass. The
Gravitas Facility lobby
reception desk has this logo prominently displayed. Products Gravitas sold include the
Thermo Nullifier
, the
Printing Pod

errands

critters
and
plants
are also bio-engineered by Gravitas.
The Temporal Bow
The Temporal Bow was the brainchild of Director Jackie Stern, a method of obtaining infinite energy through the fourth dimension. It works by vibrating sections of the forth dimension backwards and forwards in time. Unfortunately, what was meant to be a means of saving humanity during a time of political and economic strife ended up destroying it. While Stern claimed publicly that the Temporal Bow produced virtually no waste, in reality, it was generating hundreds of pounds of
Neutronium
every few hours, believed by Stern to be an autoimmune reaction of the universe to prevent contamination between timelines. This greatly disturbed Dr. Olivia Broussard, who warned Stern that the research they were conducting had the potential to destroy the world, but she was ignored.
Eventually, due to issues with the massive power consumption of the
Printing Pod
, Stern expressed a desire to build Temporal Bow technology into the Pod itself. Broussard was once again disturbed by this notion, but Stern insisted, stating that the world was doomed regardless. Some time later, Dr. Nikola Techna, who had designed the safety systems designed to prevent the Temporal Bow from destroying the world, had a breakdown over the use of the Temporal Bow in printing pods, informing Ruby that the world was doomed if anything went wrong and that the entire universe and time itself had the potential to be destroyed. He was right, and the utilization of the Temporal Bow technology in printing pods eventually tore the Earth apart.

Dr. Olivia Broussard was the head of the Gravitas Bioengineering Department, and college friend of Director Stern. When she was onboarded, a copy of her genetic makeup was taken. At first she was deferential to the Director's orders, but later on she was increasingly uncomfortable with the ethical situation, and eventually quit from the colony project.
