# Metallic Biome

This article is related to
Spaced Out
content.
The
Metallic Biome
is a
Biome
that provides an abundance of
Metal Ores
; notably including
Gold Amalgam
.
Strategy
The
Metallic Biome
isn't particularly difficult to get through. The complete lack of gas making oxygenating this a breeze; and the temperature is
Duplicant
friendly. The existance of
Oxylite
only makes things easier.
Gold Amalgam
is great due to the increased overheat temperature modifier; making it great when steel is lacking.

Database
Metallic Biome
A plethora of metals pervade the Metallic Biome making it the go-to destination for a colony ramping up production for technological advancement.
Gold Amalgam
,
Aluminum Ore
and
Cobalt Ore
are in abundant supply throughout this entire biome. Refining these metals with a
Metal Refinery
will make them available for building advanced technologies.
Though
Chlorine Gas
and
Hydrogen Gas
are the prevailing gasses in this biome,
Oxylite
exists in rock form and can provide
Oxygen
for Duplicants once they uncover it.
Dirt
,
Coal
and
Igneous Rock
round out the rest of this biome, making it a great deposit of resources for a budding industrialized colony.
Trivia
Despite what the example image of the
Metallic Biome
in the in-game database is showing, the actual Biome do not contain any
Copper Ore
or
Wolframite
, nor large pockets of
Oxygen
in the biome's unexcavated form.
