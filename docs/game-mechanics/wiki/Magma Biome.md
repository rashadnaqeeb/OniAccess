# Magma Biome

The
Magma Biome
is an extremely hot biome often filled with
Magma
, as the name would imply. It is often found in an asteroid's core at the bottom of the map; but may also spawn elsewhere due to
World Traits
like Magma Channels and Volcanic Activity, or as part of an Asteroids gimmick like
Flipped Asteroid
or
Blasted Ceres Asteroid
.
Strategy
There is very little practical reason to explore this biome, however the extreme heat does make an excellent power source when combined with
Steam Turbines
. A full core on a base-game map can easily power a colony for thousands of cycles with little maintenance if set up well. (
Spaced Out!
maps will have less power potential due to being smaller; if they have a magma-core at all)

Magma Biome
Temperatures in the Magma Biome can reach upwards of 1526 degrees, making it a reliable source of extreme heat that can be exploited for the purposes of producing
Power
and fuel.
Magma
is source of extreme
Heat
which can be used to transform
Water
in to
Steam
or
Crude Oil
into
Petroleum
. In order to prevent the extreme temperatures of this biome invading other parts of my base, suitable insulation must be constructed using materials with high melting points like
Ceramic
or
Obsidian
.
Though exosuits such as
Atmo Suits
and
Jet Suits
