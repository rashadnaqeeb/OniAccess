Coal
is a
Consumable Ore
found in the
Temperate
and
Caustic
Biomes. It can be used to create power using a
Coal Generator
.
Hatches
produce Coal after consuming enough resources.
Usage
Coal can be used to generate Power in the
Coal Generator
.
1 kg/s Coal = 600 W Power + 20 g/s
Carbon Dioxide
.
Coal can be used in
Kiln
to produce
Refined Carbon
(which is further used in
Steel
production) and
Ceramic
.
125 kg Coal = 100 kg Refined Carbon.
100 kg
Clay
+ 25 kg Coal = 100 kg Ceramic.
Apothecary
uses coal in some medicine recipes:
Vitamin Chews
, that slightly increase
germ
resistance for 1
cycle
1 kg Coal = 1 unit Vitamin Chews.
Curative Tablet
, which cures
Food Poisoning
.
1 kg Coal + 1 kg
Water
= 1 unit Curative Tablet.
Basic Rad Pill
, which reduces
Radiation Absorption
(-100 Rads/Cycle for 1
cycle
).
1 kg Coal = 1 unit Basic Rad Pill.
Production
Hatches
, Sage Hatches and Stone Hatches excrete Coal from various Resources. Note, that Smooth Hatches do not excrete Coal.
Coal can also be harvested from
Carbon Asteroids
, although the energy value gained from burning that Coal is way less than the energy value of the
Petroleum
spent on the
Rocket
travel.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Carbon Asteroid Fields at a rate of 235.8 – 707.4 kg per cycle.
In Glimmering Asteroid Fields at a rate of 30 – 90 kg per cycle.
Note
Coal is a renewable resource if produced via
Hatch
ranching, as Hatches can be fed with renewable resources, or if gathered on Carbon Asteroids, as they replenish themselves renewably.
