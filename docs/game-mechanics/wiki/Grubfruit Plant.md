Grubfruit

Grubfruit
Farming
Grubfruit Plants
requires to have at least one
Divergent critter
in the farm as well. Under ideal circumstances, a single Divergent can tend to 8 plants, but in practice this number can never be reached due to time spent traveling or otherwise busy, such as when being groomed.
Since a healthy
Grubfruit Plant
drops 8 kg or 2000kcal of Grubfruit every 8 cycles, they grant an average 250kcal per cycle. This theoretically requires 4 plants per duplicant. However, 4 plants which were planted simultaneously will only grant food every 8 cycles, then feed a dupe for 2 days and then the Grubfruit would spoil, leaving the Duplicant to starve if the plants are not carefully planted.
This makes it all the more important to cook the 2000kcal
Grubfruit
with 4000g
Sucrose
into 2400kcal
Grubfruit Preserve

Food Quality
to Good [+3]).
At 2400 kcal / 8 cycles, it gives 300 kcal per cycle on average, and only 3.33
Grubfruit Plants
are needed per Duplicant.
Spindly Grubfruit

Spindly Grubfruit Plants

A
Spindly Grubfruit

Spindly Grubfruit

Database
Grubfruit Plant
Edible Plant
The Grubfruit Plant exhibits a coevolutionary relationship with the
Divergent
species.
Though capable of producing fruit without the help of the Divergent, the
Spindly Grubfruit
is a substandard version of the Grubfruit in both taste and caloric value per cycle.
The mechanism for how the Divergent inspires Grubfruit Plant growth is not entirely known but is thought to be somehow tied to the infrasonic 'songs' these insects lovingly purr to their plants.
