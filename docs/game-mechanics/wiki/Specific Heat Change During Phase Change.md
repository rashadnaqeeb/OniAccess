# Specific Heat Change During Phase Change

For most elements, there is a no increase or decrease in
Specific Heat Capacity
; however, some have a change in this number enabling it to be exploited for heat gain or deletion.
Usages
Heat gain
You can do efficient heat exchanger that cause small amount of heat from another source.
Examples
Petroleum Boiler
,
Regolith
Smelter,
Sour Gas
Boiler,
Salt
Boiler, and
Mud
Boiler
Heat loss
You can do efficient heat deletion method at the cost of little to no power draw.
Examples
Ethanol
Cool
Steam Turbine
and
Nuclear Fallout

Trivia
While most real-life materials do have specific heat capacity changes during a phase change, they do not create or destroy heat (unlike in the game) because real-life phase changes do not have a 3°C hysteresis.
