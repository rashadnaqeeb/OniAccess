Ore Scrubber
is a
Utility
building that uses piped
Chlorine
gas to kill
Germs
on items carried by
Duplicants
, consuming the gas in the process.
Mechanics
When a
Duplicant
passes by an operational Ore Scrubber while carrying
Debris
or bottled fluids that contain germs, they will stop at it, similar to a
Sink
, and scrub the item, removing up to 480,000 germs in the process. This will consume 70g of
Chlorine
, which must be supplied to the Ore Scrubber through
gas pipes
. Germs are only removed from the object being scrubbed, and not from the Duplicant operating it. The Ore Scrubber does not require power, nor does it generate heat, but it is possible for it to
overheat
if placed in an area of sufficiently high temperature. Only one Duplicant can use a single Ore Scrubber at a time, and they will only use it a single time when passing by it.
Tips
Slime
and germy
Polluted Dirt
are two examples of solid debris that frequently are found or created contaminated with germs. Ore Scrubbers are a relatively simple way of disinfecting these objects before being brought into the colony, as long as you have a source of
Chlorine
to supply them.
Germ
count on solid debris or bottled fluids can compound if more of the same object is dropped onto the same tile, such as from a
Water Sieve
dropping
Polluted Dirt
, or freshly-mined
Slime
from adjacent tiles falling and merging. This can result in debris containing more than 480,000 germs, which requires two or more uses of an Ore Scrubber to fully disinfect.
Placing multiple Ore Scrubbers sequentially can help reduce the chance of contaminated debris slipping through, as well as helping prevent Duplicants from bypassing the scrubbers entirely due to them all being in use.
Duplicants will only stop at Ore Scrubbers if they are carrying a germy object. With access to
Atmo Suits
to negate the
downsides
of exposure to the caustic gas, placing Ore Scrubbers directly within a
Chlorine
atmosphere allows the scrubbed material to benefit from both the operation of the Ore Scrubber itself, as well as the natural disinfection provided by being surrounded by Chlorine, which can considerably increase its effectiveness at removing germs. This will also help to clean Duplicants and their suits of germs spread to them from picking up contaminated debris.
The
Decontamination Shower
is an effective alternative to the Ore Scrubber, requiring
water
instead of
Chlorine
, and converting it into
Polluted Water
. It will remove up to 1,000,000 germs from both the Duplicant as well as anything they are carrying at the same time, but requires higher-tier
Research
as well as access to
Lead
.
Trivia
Despite being called an Ore Scrubber, no type of
Metal Ore
naturally spawns contaminated with germs.
Slime
is the only natural solid tile that can spawn contaminated, but it is possible for these
Slimelung
germs to spread to adjacent tiles if the tile's germ count exceeds 1,000,000.
