Tips
The
Thermal Conductivity
of Amber is extremely low (0.17), making it a bit involved to melt Amber at a reasonable rate.
Amber debris can be submerged in a hot liquid or gas with a Thermal Conductivity that is at least 0.17 (see
Liquid
and
Gas
