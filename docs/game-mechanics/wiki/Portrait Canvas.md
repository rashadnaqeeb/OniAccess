Portrait Canvas
is a
decorative
building in the
Furniture category
that provides a
Decor
bonus to its surroundings and can
be painted on
to provide extra.
There is 1 kind of crude, 1 kind of quaint and 12 kinds of masterpiece paintings; 8 of the masterpieces are locked behind
Blueprints
. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +5 for Crude, +10 for Quaint, +15 for Masterpiece.
Tips
Reed Fiber
, a required construction material, gives +10% Decor bonus to the total Decor value of the painting, and
Gold Amalgam
or
Copper Ore
can be used for another 10%.
Niobium
gives +50%, but is harder to obtain as a space material.
