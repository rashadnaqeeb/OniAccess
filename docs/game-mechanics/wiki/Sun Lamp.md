Sun Lamp
is a recreational building that provides sunlight level Lux. It can be paired with the
Beach Chair
to improve the morale bonus from the beach chair.
Mechanics
The Ceiling Light provides its largest horizontal light coverage to the 11th level below it (23 tiles coverage width).
Tips
Since Lux drop-off follows an inverse square relationship to distance, the lamp has to be placed very close to the
Beach Chairs
to get any benefits.
Gas Grass
can be grown under Sun Lamps, however net energy gain is dubious.
Duplicant Motion Sensor
or other
automation
can help minimize the massive continuous power drain of Sun Lamp.
