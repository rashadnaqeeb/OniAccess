The Badlands Asteroid
A barren location with an overabundance of mineral resources.
The rocky terrain of The Badlands poses no immediate threats, but sparse resource availability will make expansion and technological progress challenging.

The Badlands Asteroid
is one of the
Asteroid Types
available to start on. It is a dry Asteroid with little natural
Liquids
and less useful materials due to
Barren Biomes
taking up so much space. Due to a lack of
Marsh Biomes
,
Gold Amalgam
is only available from meteors, and hot liquid handling and oil processing will require
Steel
early on.
Map Layout
Example Badlands world. Notice the
Barren Biomes
.
Strategy
Pips
,
Pokeshells
, and
Pacus
are not available in The Badlands. The player has to wait for the
Printing Pod
to
print
them, or source them from
Space Destinations
.
Dusk Caps
,
Thimble Reeds
,
Arbor Trees
, and
Oxyferns
are also not available on the asteroid and will have to be printed or found in space destinations.
Waterweeds
are completely unobtainable.
There is no
Sedimentary Rock
available in early game which is required for
Stone Hatch
breeding. The player has to break
Fossil
into
Sedimentary Rock
with a
Rock Crusher
instead.
Gold Amalgam
is only available via
Meteor Showers
in
The Badlands Asteroid
. As a result there may be some difficulty in production of
Oxylite
and
Super Coolant
if a
Gold Volcano
is not rolled in worldgen.
