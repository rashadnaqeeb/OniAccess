Transit Tube Crossing
allows a Tube System to go through walls. The Transit Tube Crossing takes the place of a wall tile and then connects to
Transit Tubes
on both of its sides.
Tips
Similarly to
Transit Tubes
, Transit Tube Crossings are made of
Plastic
and have the same low melting point. Do not run them in areas over
159.9 °C
/
319.82 °F
or else they will melt into
Naphtha
.
Tube Crossing cannot serve as an exit from tube-network, it has to be connected to properly directed segments of Transit Tubes.
Using two tiles of Transit Tube Crossing connected together will stabilize their temperature against each other and counteract their property of seemingly absorbing heat from space vacuum until eventually melting.
Transit Tube Crossing acts as a solid tile and do
not
leak liquids or gases - as shown in the screenshots below.  They can withstand up to 1400 kg of pressure.
