# Atomic Research

The subject of this article was removed from the game.
* The in-game information in this article is kept purely for historical purposes.
Atomic Research
was the fourth tier of
Research
points before it was reworked into
Materials Science Research
. It was necessary to unlock more advanced structures.
Atomic Research Points were obtainable by a Duplicant with an Atomic Research
Skill
to research at the
Atomic Collider
, which consumed 10
Radbolts
per
Research Point
