# Fire Pole

Fire Pole
Allows rapid
Duplicant
descent. Significantly slows upward climbing.
Fire poles provide quick downward travel, while ladders provide better upward movement.

Fire Pole
functions similarly to
ladders
, the only difference being that downward movement is 400% as fast (for a
duplicant
with 0
Athletics
) while upward movement is 75% slower. If a ladder and fire pole are both available to reach an area, a duplicant will prioritize using the pole to descend and prioritize the ladder to ascend.
Unlike climbing, descent speed on fire poles is not affected by the Athletics attribute, making them less attractive for high Athletics duplicants.
Tips
Placing Fire Poles beside Ladders (or even better, plastic ladders) can greatly increase movement efficiency throughout your base.
