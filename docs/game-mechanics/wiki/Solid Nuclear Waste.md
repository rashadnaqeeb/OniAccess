Solid Nuclear Waste
is a highly toxic
solid
full of
radioactive contaminants
. It melts into
Nuclear Waste
at
26.9 °C
/
80.42 °F
.
Production
Produced by
Research Reactor
as liquid.
Formed in a
reactor Meltdown
: this is a very costly and messy way to get Nuclear Waste.
Radbolt Engines
emit gaseous
Nuclear Fallout
, which can condense into liquid Nuclear Waste afterwards.
On death, a
Beeta
turns into 1000 g of solid
Nuclear Waste
.
Usage
Radiation Generation
Solid Nuclear Waste depending on mass can emit considerable radiation that can be absorbed by
Radbolt Generators
as natural tiles, same as liquid. However as debris it doesn't emit any radiation at all.
As "natural" tiles for Pip farms.
Because of the radiation it naturally emits, "natural" tiles from Solid Nuclear Waste are ideal for
Pip farms
that take advantage of
plant mutations
.
Tip
To create a natural tile of Solid Nuclear Waste, the Liquid Nuclear waste either needs to be compressed to 1473 kg of pressure and then cooled down until it freezes OR it could be frozen while in a bottled state OR it could be frozen while in a building that allows phase changes (since liquids in buildings are considered to be in bottles).
