# Refined Metal

Refined Metal
is one of the types of
Resources
in the game
Oxygen Not Included
. It was introduced in the
Automation Upgrade
. Every type of
Metal Ore
in game has a corresponding refined version of itself. The refined version of any Metal Ore has slightly better properties compared to its unrefined version.
Usage
Refined Metal is used in the construction of all
Automation
wires, gates and sensors; it is also used to make the highest tier wires, and many of the most advanced buildings.
Conductive Wire
and
Conductive Wire Bridges
can only be built out of refined metals. They have a maximum capacity of 2 kW instead of the 1 kW maximum capacity of normal
Wire
. Additionally there is
Heavi-Watt Conductive Wire
and the
Heavi-Watt Conductive Joint Plate
which increases the max power to 50kW from the 20kW in the
Heavi-Watt Wire
and
Heavi-Watt Joint Plate
but they require refined metal and have a significantly smaller
Decor
penalty.
It is also used to build
Metal Tiles
which provide the largest Decor bonus of any tile other than
Carpeted Tile
,
Metal Blocks
(+20 in a 8 tile radius) and may be used to build
Tempshift Plates
.
Plug Slug
consume refined metals and produce 5% of the consumed volume as
Hydrogen
in exchange.
Production
Metal Ore can be refined using either the
Rock Crusher
or the
Metal Refinery
. The Rock Crusher is only 50% efficient at converting raw ore into refined metal (converting the other 50% of the raw ore into sand) whereas the Metal Refinery is 100% efficient. Alternatively,
Smooth Hatches
can eat any unrefined metal and convert it into its refined variant at 75% efficiency (it will not produce any side product like the
Rock Crusher
does).
Steel
is the exception and needs a multitude of ingredients to produce, needing
Lime
,
Refined Carbon
and
Iron
to put inside the Metal Refinery.
Niobium
is obtained from space missions. After one or two space missions, a Niobium-
Thermium
cycle can be estabilished.
Iridium
is primarily obtained from
Demolior
.
Metal volcanoes
can provide an infinite source of Refined Metal, but care must be taken not to let them warm up their environment too much. The hot debris can be used for building immediately, and using it in such a way will reset its temperature and effectively delete the heat, but your dupes may become injured when handling it. When done right, you may never need to refine most metals yourself. Ore is also not easily available in unlimited quantities and still used for some important buildings, so counterintuitively, ore could be more valuable than its refined metal.
