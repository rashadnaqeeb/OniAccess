Polluted Water
is the most common naturally occurring
liquid
on most asteroids, especially in the
Swamp Biome
. It also fills caves with
Polluted Oxygen
.
Polluted Ice
in the
Frozen Biomes
will also start melting into cold puddles of Polluted Water once they are breached.
Polluted Water, like any other liquid, creates negative effects if a
Duplicant
walks or is submerged in it by adding onto their stress. It can be created by Duplicants not reaching a toilet in time, or by
stress
-vomiting. It can also be created as a by-product of the
Algae Distiller
, or through certain amenities such as
Lavatories
and
Showers
.
Polluted water can be dropped in clean water tanks without worry of the clean water becoming polluted. However, if the polluted liquid contains Germs, the clean water will also become contaminated. If the drop of polluted water inside your tank is less than 150 kg it can be mopped up, even submerged at the bottom of the tank.
Despite being dirty, Polluted Water is more useful than it seems. It is a better coolant than pure
Water
due to its 40 degree wider temperature range. Polluted Oxygen
emission
is not strictly negative, since it can be filtered into pure
Oxygen
. Polluted Water is also required for irrigating certain
Plants
. When piped into a
Water Sieve
, it can be purified into regular water, although this does not remove any germs present.
Emissions
Polluted Water emits Polluted Oxygen, even in a vacuum. Like
Slime
and
Polluted Dirt
, it will stop emitting when the pressure above it reaches 1.8 kg. Specifically, five times a second it has a 1% chance of converting 0.1% of the surface cell's mass, up to 1 kg;
[
1
]
so with a full (1000 kg) surface cell, it will produce approximately 30 kg per cycle. However, because of the low probability, the production is not consistent.
The emission of Polluted Oxygen is calculated on the first 1000 kg of polluted water. Having less will reduce emission, having more has no effect. So for production of
Polluted Oxygen
it is better to spread the polluted water tiles out rather then stacking them up.
Polluted Water only emits Polluted Oxygen to the tile above it, not to any other adjecent tiles.
Polluted Water in
Liquid Reservoirs
does not emit any Polluted Oxygen; this may be useful to players who want to store this resource for later use.

Production
Duplicants stress-vomiting or not reaching a toilet in time.
Lavatories
,
Showers
,
Wash Basins
, and
Sinks
convert Water into Polluted Water. Lavatories produce 11.7 kg of Polluted Water and consumes 5 kg of clean Water, making them water positive. All others are water neutral.
There are two types of
Geysers
that produce Polluted Water – Polluted Water Vents and Cool Slush Geysers.
Carbon Skimmers
capture
Carbon Dioxide
into Water to produce Polluted Water. They are water neutral.

Algae

produce
Oxygen
and remove a small amount of Carbon Dioxide while producing Polluted Water as a by-product at the rate of 290.33 g/s. They are water negative.
Natural Gas Generators

Petroleum Generators
produce Polluted Water as a by-product at the rate of 750 g/s, or 450 kg/cycle, or 2.(66) W/g
Peat Burners

Tips
Due to its high specific heat capacity and a boiling and freezing range larger than
Water
,
Brine
and
Salt Water
, it’s a great coolant that can be obtained in the early game.
As with Salt Water and Brine, the larger temperature range is due to the combination of two real-life phenomena:
boiling-point elevation
and
freezing-point depression
.
Polluted Water can be pumped via
Liquid Pump
into a
Water Sieve
and be purified with a filtration medium, turning into pure
Water
that can be deposited from a
Liquid Vent
.
Filtering Polluted Water does not remove any
Germs
that may be present. The germs will remain in the clean water unless additional steps are taken.
Most naturally occurring and produced Polluted Water contain no germs and should be kept separated from infected water.
A
Lavatory
produces around six times more Polluted Water than by a Duplicant not reaching a toilet in time.
Even if Polluted Water has Food poisoning germs present in it, if a Duplicant becomes submerged in it, they wont have any food poisoning germs present on them.
A tile of
polluted ice
can be created by freezing polluted water.  To form a tile of polluted ice by freezing, the mass of the polluted water must exceed 400 kg.  If the tile is 400 kg or less it will fall as debris.
This is unlike other ices which require more than 800kg liquid to form a frozen tile.
