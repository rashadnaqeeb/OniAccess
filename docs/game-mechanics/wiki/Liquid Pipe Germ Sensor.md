# Liquid Pipe Germ Sensor

Liquid Pipe Germ Sensor
Sends a Green Signal or a Red Signal based on the internal Germ count of the pipe.
Germ sensors can help control automation behavior in the presence of germs.

Liquid Pipe Germ Sensor
is an automation sensor to detect the
germ
count in a
Liquid Pipe
.
It has a count threshold and can be configured as either below or above this threshold.
It is useful for keeping contaminated water separated from your clean
water
.
If the pipe segment is empty, the sensor retains the previous value.
Sensor will perform while entombed or submerged.
Tips
The germs in liquid pipes cannot be killed by surrounding
Chlorine
gas or
Radiation
. Therefore you cannot simply put a Liquid Pipe Germ Sensor on the output pipe of a
Liquid Reservoir
surrounded by Chlorine and configure it as below 1 germ. The germs in that segment will never be killed and the sensor would always send a Red signal. Those segments have to be constantly recirculated to work properly.
Note that contaminated water is not the same as polluted water.  Contaminated water contains germs and may be polluted.  Polluted water might not be contaminated.
Melting this sensor will result in 75kg of
Refined Metal
, effectively
converting 50kg of Plastic into 50kg of Refined Metal
.
