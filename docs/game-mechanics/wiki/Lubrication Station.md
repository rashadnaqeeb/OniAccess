A Lubrication Station is used by
Bionic Duplicants
to fully replenish their
Gear Oil
.
Using the Lubrication Station also applies a status of "Fresh Oil" which gives a buff depending on the type of oil used: +2 morale and -5% stress per cycle from Phyto Oil, or +3 morale and -10% stress per cycle from Crude Oil. The buff lasts for 8 cycles.
Content
To be usable, a Lubrication Station needs to contain at least 200 kg of Oil. Even if a duplicant needs Gear Oil and the Lubrication Station is at 190 kg, it won't use it. It can store up to 400 kg.
When destroyed, the Lubrication Station will drop its liquid content as bottles on the ground.
Germs
If the
Phyto Oil
provided contains
Slimelung
germs, these germs will be rapidly destroyed by the building in its reservoir, even in a polluted oxygen environment. Moreover, if a duplicant uses a Lubrication Station that has germs in its reservoir, these germs will be destroyed before arriving in the duplicant's body.
