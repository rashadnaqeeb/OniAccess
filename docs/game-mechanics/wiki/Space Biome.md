# Space Biome

The
Space Biome
is at the top of every map where
Liquids
and
Gases
are quickly destroyed due to space exposure at a rate of
100 kg/s
for liquids and
100 g/s
for gases.
Strategy
The
Space Biome
is the gate behind
Rocketry
; so conquering it is vital.
Atmo Suits
and an airlock is the simplest way of doing this; as it deals with both the potentially freezing or scalding temperature, and the lack of air.
When setting up permanent structures here that require some sort of atmosphere,
Drywall
can prevent materials from being deleted by space exposure. Setting up farms and ranches here can be quite beneficial; as
Vacuum
acts as a perfect insulator making temperature control far simpler.
Found On
The
Space Biome
is found on every Asteroid/Planetoid.

Space Biome
The Space Biome is located on the scenic surface of an asteroid. Watch for dazzling meteorites to shower elements down from the sky.
Setting up
Solar Panels
on the surface will provide a source of renewable energy. However, much care must be taken to ensure
Liquids
or
Gases
are not sucked out into the
Vacuum
of space.
Shove Voles
are native to this biome, and need to be wrangled or contained or they will infest the colony.
