Auto-Sweeper
can move items to and from buildings within a 9x9 zone centered on the machine (4 blocks out in each direction). Sweepers can load materials into a building, such as delivering raw food to an
Electric Grill
then pick up the finished product from the floor and place it in a container such as a
Refrigerator
in range.
The list of items that can be picked up by an
Auto-Sweeper
are:
Any food items
Interplanetary launcher payloads
Any item that can be placed in a
Storage Bin
, which includes most solids
Any bottle/canister with the Refined Metal or Metal tag (e.g.
Mercury
,
Molten Aluminum
,
Mercury Gas
)
Sweepers obey the
priority
system, so will move items from low priority containers to high priority containers. This can be somewhat confusing with
Conveyor Loaders
and
Storage Bins
since the sweeper will not place anything from the Bin into a Loader unless the Loader has a higher priority setting. The same is true when moving from a
Conveyor Receptacle
to a Bin, items will be left in the Receptacle causing the conveyor line to back up unless the receptacle has a lower priority than the Storage Bin.
Sweepers are the primary tool for building truly automated
shipping
systems that do not require any
Duplicant
labor to carry things around.
Tips
Auto-sweepers can supply diagonally, but only at specific angles
Auto-Sweepers consume power only when moving.
Auto-Sweepers obey
sweep
designations on items and containers.
Auto-Sweepers do not exchange
heat
with transported items.
Auto-Sweepers can transport up to 1 tonne per swing.
Auto-Sweepers need to be continuously enabled for approximately 2 seconds per item they attempt to pick up in a single "sweep", and then an additional 2 seconds to store what they're holding. They'll drop their contents from their center tile if disabled after picking up but before storing items.
Auto-Sweepers can reach through diagonal openings and closed
Pneumatic Doors
. This means that sweepers can transfer items between sealed rooms. However this diagonal reach only works at specific angles. Compare this to
Duplicant Reach
.
When
Automatic Dispensers
are placed between Sweepers it can daisy-chain them into the single sweeping system, where throughput is now limited by Sweeper, which exceeds single
Conveyor Rail
line in at least x25 times.
Which tile of a building an Auto-Sweeper needs to cover to be able to deliver to it depends on that particular building, see
Tile of interest
.
Auto-Sweepers works immersed in
liquids
.
Auto-Sweepers can plant seeds in
Planter Boxes
or
Farm Tiles
(not in regular
Tiles
) if they are in range.
Auto-Sweeper covering the 2nd left-most tile on the bottom row.
