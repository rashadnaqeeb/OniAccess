Tips
Pemmican uses Tallow, essentially converting 1000 g of not edible material into an extra 1000 kcal. It makes the
Carnivore
achievement a lot easier: killing 3
Spigot Seals
gives 150 kg Tallow, enough to make 150 Pemmican (390,000 kcal), each of them adding 1000 kcal to the 1600 kcal Meat (in comparison, a Barbeque only adds 400 kcal per 1600 kcal of Meat).
