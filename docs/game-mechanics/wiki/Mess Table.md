Mess Table
is a furniture item to which
Duplicants
can be assigned to consume their
food
in a determined position. Mess Tables reduce
stress
from eating off the floor and are the main component for the
Mess Hall
Room
and
Great Hall
room. It can store up to 5 g
Table Salt
and both consumes 1 g Table Salt and grant the duplicant +1 morale per use.
Tips
Creating a dedicated
Mess Hall
or
Great Hall
is highly recommended. Making this area much nicer than the rest of your colony is an easy way to reduce stress. Lavishing the area with lamps, art, and high decor items amplifies the de-stressing effect. Including a
Ration Box
or ideally a
Refrigerator
with the setup saves time and stores food efficiently.
Making a Mess Hall close to food production areas saves Duplicants the hassle of having to transport food long distances. Duplicants will be automatically assigned to any unassigned table; they will refuse to eat until they reach it, unless unassigned manually.  Duplicants will only eat at a table assigned to them, and can only be assigned to one table.  This can result in Duplicants starving to death while holding food, or repeatedly dropping food and making a full return trip to get new food from the original source instead of picking up the food item they dropped.
