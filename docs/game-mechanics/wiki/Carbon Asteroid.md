Carbon Asteroid
is a space destination with a distance of 10,000 km.
Usage
The Carbon Asteroid is composed entirely of solid allotropes of carbon and has no biological resources. It is generally composed of roughly equal amounts of
Refined Carbon
,
Coal
and
Diamond
, and is the only renewable source of Diamond. At 10,000 kilometers it is the closest destination for a space mission. The most efficient
Rocket
configuration for a mission to the Carbon Asteroid consists of 1
Liquid Fuel Tank
and 3
Cargo Bays
, returning 3.33 kilograms of cargo for every 1 kilogram of
Petroleum
.
Tips
The amount of energy that can be obtained by burning the returned Coal is, assuming average resource composition and optimal rocket design, 33% less than the energy value of the spent Petroleum. If your goal is to acquire fuel to power your base, the
Ice Planet
is a better destination as it has plentiful
Methane
.
