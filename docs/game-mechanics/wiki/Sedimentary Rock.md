Usage
Sedimentary rock lacks unique or exceptional characteristics regarding decor and thermal properties, and has a modest availability. It can be used in construction, but is superseded by most other minerals/materials for specific applications, and is not particularly abundant. It is best utilized for building simple structures such as
ladders

tile
