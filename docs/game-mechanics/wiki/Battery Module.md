Battery Module
Stores the excess Power generated by a Rocket Engine or
Rocket Platform
. Provides stored power to Interior Rocket Outlets. Loses charge over time.
Charging a battery module before takeoff makes it easier to power buildings during flight.

The
Battery Module
stores 100 kJ of power, and leaks 400J per cycle, while producing no heat. Because of this it acts as a superior version of the
Jumbo Battery
(2.5x the storage, 5x less leakage, and without the heat). It has very little burden or height, and may be preferable to other options (i.e. batteries taking up space in the crew modules).
Other Uses
This is also usable as a battery for colonies, but of course it requires a landing platform; the overall space that they take up can be considerable. Also, they lack any automation ports.
