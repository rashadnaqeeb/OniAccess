A fragment of a frosty starting world with sub-zero temperatures throughout.
Duplicants who work in sub-zero temperatures will need to wear or build warming equipment. Abundant natural fuel sources could lead to a well-powered colony.

When enabled, the Ceres Fragment asteroid will replace one of four outer asteroids that are otherwise guaranteed across most cluster configurations: Marshy, Moo, Water, and Tundra. The Ceres Fragment Asteroid attempts to place 4 generic geysers alongside the Ceres-related biomes,
Cool Pool Biome
,
Nectar Biome
, and
Ice Cave Biome
.
The Ceres Fragment is a fixed size of 64x128, and may result in a substantial increase in the total amount of space the asteroid would otherwise take.

Strategy
The Ceres Fragment is a chilly asteroid, although contains exclusive resources like the
Carved Lumen Quartz
. Plentiful
Ice
can be melted down for
Water
, and
Bammoths
provide ample amounts of food. Note that the Ceres Fragment does not contain the
Geothermal Power Plant
.
