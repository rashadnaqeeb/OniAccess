The Magma in the outer ring often leaks heat into the surrounding biome and the inner geode through

Strategy
If you haven't dug out the area and no crucial buildings nearby you can open the geode to let the heat drip into the surrounding natural tiles. Digging a natural tile destroys 50 % of its mass, which also destroys 50 % of its stored Heat energy.
Alternativly you can use diagonal building to destroy the magma or sieve the heat into a
Steamroom
with a
Tempshift Plate
.
