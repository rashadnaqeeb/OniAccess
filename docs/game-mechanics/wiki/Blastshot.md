Blastshot
is a resource produced in the
Blastshot Maker
. It is used by
Meteor Blasters
to destroy
Meteors
.
Each individual Blastshot has a mass of 10kg. However, the game groups Blastshots into bundles of 5, therefore when clicking on a Blastshot, the properties card will show a mass of 50kg. This is an important detail to consider when automating the Blastshot Maker with a weight plate. When a bundle is dropped from the building, it will have a mass of 50kg on the weight plate.
Once a blastshot obliterates a meteor, it will drop 2500g of the refined metal used in making it, aswell as some of the material the meteor consists of as items.
Each shot destroys one meteor and costs one Blastshot. Meteors destroyed by Blastshot return 25% of the resources they would have dropped on collision with a tile.
