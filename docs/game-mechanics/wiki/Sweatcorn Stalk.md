Sweatcorn Stalk
Edible Plant
The Sweatcorn Stalk is one of the oldest living members of the Zea genus.
This robust monopodial plant features eye-catching vegetable cobs prized for their color and intense sweetness.
Farmers gift the best of their harvest to their most valued neighbors. Some etymologists theorize that the term "corny" was coined to describe the heartfelt sentiments expressed in the accompanying card.
