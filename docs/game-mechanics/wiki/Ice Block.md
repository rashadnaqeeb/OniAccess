Ice Block
is a
decorative
building in the
Furniture category
that provides a
Decor
bonus to its surroundings and can
be sculpted
to provide a further bonus. The quality of the sculpture influences this bonus with higher quality sculptures from highly skilled artistic
Duplicants
producing the best Decor bonuses.
Ice Blocks will melt if not kept cold. They are most efficient in low-temperature, high-traffic areas; since they are twice as wide as other similar sculptures (e.g. Metal Block), they are a less space-efficient Decor provider than most.
There is 1 kind of crude and 3 kinds of quaint sculptures; 2 of the quaint sculptures are locked behind
Blueprints
. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +5 for Crude, +10 for Quaint.
Notes
As opposed to other types of
Artwork
, there are no masterpiece-quality type Ice Block sculptures. Thus, the
Masterworks skill
is not necessary to create ice blocks with the greatest possible decor bonus.
Tips
Ice sculptures melt above
-0.6 °C
/
30.92 °F
, so they're only useful as a constant source of decor in subzero temperatures or in
space
, as long as they're protected from the heat of
comets
,
regolith
and
rockets
.
Two
Large Sculpting Blocks
provide as much decor as a single Ice Block, although they cost twice as much in total and have a
slightly
diminished range owing to their size (1x3 tiles).
Similarly, two
Metal Blocks
provide
twice
as much decor as a single Ice Block (or even more, depending on the materials used).
Since Ice Blocks lack any masterworks, their skill-dictated decor bonus caps at a lower amount (+10) than other artwork types (+15 for masterworks). This makes Ice Blocks even less efficient of a decor provider in comparison.
Building Ice Blocks on
Mesh
top of the colony's supply of cold fresh
water
is a good way to make it last a bit longer, absorb quite a bit of ambient
heat
, and train
artists
without needing to deconstruct the statues. Liquid and any gases with high thermal conductivity will exchange heat with the ice block more quickly in case one intends to turn it into cold water.
Ice Blocks cannot be placed in
Sandbox Mode
without instantly melting, since it places all buildings at
20 °C
/
68 °F
, but when in
Debug Mode
the building can be cooled down to subzero temperatures if the game is paused.
