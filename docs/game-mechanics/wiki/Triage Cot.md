# Triage Cot

Triage Cot
Accelerates Health restoration and the healing of physical injuries.
Revives incapacitated Duplicants.
Duplicants use triage cots to recover from physical injuries and receive aid from peers.

The
Triage Cot
restores 50 points of a
Duplicant
's health per cycle, 1% of their breath per second and 10% stress per cycle.  While a Duplicant lays on the Triage Cot  they are considered to be
sleeping
and another Duplicant can treat the patient to give them the "Receiving treatment" buff, which improves Triage Cot's effects to 75 health and 20% stress per cycle.
A Duplicant who reached 0 hp is incapacitated and will need to be rescued to a Triage Cot by other Duplicants. A Triage Cot assigned to a wounded Duplicant will become unassigned if said Duplicant reaches full health.
