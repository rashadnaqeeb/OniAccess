Water Cooler
is a recreational building. A single Duplicant can use Water Cooler during
downtime
to slightly improve
morale
. Despite being a recreational building, it does not need to be in a
Recreation Room
.
Duplicants who use the Water Cooler to cool down will receive the Heat Resistant buff (immunity against Toasty Surroundings) for 0.2 cycles.
Tips
It requires bottled Water (through Mopping or deconstructing
Liquid Reservoir
, etc.), or a
Pitcher Pump
over a Water body to fill it.
Water Coolers can give duplicants
Food Poisoning
if water carries its germs.
Water Coolers are outperformed by most other drink-dispensing machines, e.g.
Espresso Machines
.
Water Coolers have the advantage of having the quickest animations among these machines.
Water Coolers can fill in as the required recreational building in an early
Great Hall
. Disabling the water cooler will still fulfill the room requirement and save on water.
Despite its name, the Water Cooler does not actually cool water. It simply stores whatever water is delivered to it, without changing its temperature. The temperature of this water has no effect on duplicants or the building’s operation.
Using the water cooler requires interacting with another duplicant to gain the Socialized (+1 Morale) effect for
Water
. Socializing is not needed to get the +2 morale bonus with
Brackene
.
