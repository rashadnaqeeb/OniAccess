Wall Toilet
is a
building
used by
Duplicants
to empty their bladders. It requires
Water
intake and produces
Polluted Water
and
Food Poisoning
Germs
. Some of these germs are left on each
Duplicant
that uses it. It is a smaller alternative to both the
Lavatory
and the
Outhouse
but requires a wall to attach to and is a great option for
Rocketry
.
Usage
The Wall Toilet is a hygienic way to provide a place for Duplicants to relieve themselves, and thus reduce
Stress
and the spread of
Diseases
.
It also fulfills the 'Toilet' criteria for the
Latrine
in the
Room Overlay
, which provides a +1 morale bonus to Stress reduction. Furthermore, it fulfills the 'Flush Toilet' criteria for the
Washroom
in the Room Overlay, which provides +2 morale bonus.
It also fulfills the 'Toilet' criteria for the
Hospital
room.
The Wall Toilet outputs more fluid than it takes in, so it can be used to recover minuscule amount of Water or Dirt when paired with a
Water Sieve
. Each duplicant with normal bladder will produce at least ~11.16 g/s Water, which, for example, can recoup a ninth of their daily oxygen demands down the line. However, the amount of excess water added is small enough that to keep an
Electrolyzer
running non-stop, it would require 90 Duplicants to use the bathroom each cycle.
If adding the water produced to your supply, you would have to worry about disinfecting the Polluted Water, which will have
millions
of germs in them.
Mechanics
If the Wall Toilet is attached to the outer wall of a
Spacefarer Module
or a
Solo Spacefarer Nosecone
the output of the Wall Toilet will be deleted by being dumped into space.  This is true always, even when the spacecraft is on the ground.
The Wall Toilet uses 2.5 kg of Water supplied by piping and produces 9.2 kg of Polluted Water, which is deposited on the other side of the wall. The resulting Polluted Water is the combination of the 2.5 kg of input Water temperature and 6.7 kg of
37 °C
/
98.6 °F
newly created Polluted Water.
One use of the Wall Toilet produces 120,000 Food Poisoning germs. These germs are divided among the Duplicant, the Polluted Water, and the Wall Toilet itself as follows:
Duplicant: +10,000 germs
Wall Toilet: +10,000 germs
Polluted Water: +100,000 germs (deleted usually)
Tips
Best used in spacecraft and venting the output into space.  Impractical to use on the ground compared to the
Lavatory
.
Running excess water piping around the interior of your spacecraft is sufficient to supply long voyages without the need to use buildings like a
reservoir
to contain water.
