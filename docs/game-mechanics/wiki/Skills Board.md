Skills Board
allows
Duplicants
to be given specialized
Skills
. The Skills screen will be locked until a
Skills Board
is built. It provides a variety of career paths for Duplicants to follow, which provides boosts to stats and abilities, but also makes the Duplicants
expect
nicer surroundings and food. The
Skills Board
was introduced as the
Jobs Board
in the Occupational Upgrade Update, supplementing the earlier and more rudimentary job assignment system. It was further changed into the
Skills Board
in The Quality of Life MK3 Update.
Usage
When a Duplicant is assigned to a job
After giving a skill to a duplicant through the Skills screen, the duplicant will be able to be assigned a specialized hat related to their new skill.
