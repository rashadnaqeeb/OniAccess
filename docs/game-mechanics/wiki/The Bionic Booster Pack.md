# The Bionic Booster Pack

Keep your colony current with new recruits who are on a big power trip!
The Bionic Booster Pack
revolutionizes the workforce with new
Bionic Duplicants
, skill-enhancing bionic
Booster
modules, portable power storage, and remote-work buildings—everything you need to create a colony of cosmic proportions.
Harvest resources in low-breathability environments safely! Bionic Duplicants have internal oxygen stores that allow them to work for several cycles between breaths.
Build-a-Bionic! Mix and match boosters to customize a bionic Duplicant's skillset, and remix as needed to adapt to your colony's ever-evolving needs.
Just make sure to keep these new Duplicants dry, and don't skimp on the bionic service stations. Traditional toilets were
not
designed for this...
Go off the grid! The new portable power banks that keep bionic Duplicants online can also supply power to buildings in isolated parts of your base when connected to one of the new electrical discharge buildings.
Cancel your commute! Rockets can now be outfitted for pilot-less flight, and new remote-work buildings enable your Duplicants to operate machinery anywhere on the asteroid.
Less time lost in transit, more time for what really matters: work.
Personalize your planetoid! New cosmetic skins transform automation components and electrical conduits, among other things.
New bionic recruits, remote-work options, and portable power solutions—supercharge your interplanetary exploration with
The Bionic Booster Pack!
