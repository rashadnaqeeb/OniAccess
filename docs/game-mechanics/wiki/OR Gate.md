OR Gate
is an
Automation
Building
that takes in two inputs and outputs Green when one or both inputs are receiving Green, and outputs Red when both inputs are receiving Red. It is connected to other buildings via
Automation Wire
. It is effectively redundant at present as connecting two automation outputs together automatically creates a "wired or" connection - unless you need to separate two logic circuits.

Like the
