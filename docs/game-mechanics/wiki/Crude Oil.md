Occurrences
Crude oil can be found in the
Oil Biome
, which is located above the
Magma Biome
on the bottom of the map. It can also be extracted from an untapped
Oil Reservoir
by constructing an
Oil Well
over it. It stacks up to 870 kg/tile when not pressurized.
For Spaced Out-style
Planetoid Clusters
,
Oil Biome
is found on the
teleporter
target asteroid.
For Moonlet Clusters,
Oil Biome
is found on
The Desolands Asteroid
, and
for Ceres Mantle Cluster, it is found on
Sweet Ceres Asteroid
.

Oil Reservoir

Usage
Oil Refinery
can produce
Petroleum
from Crude Oil with 50 % efficiency. It requires Duplicant operation.
Alternatively, Crude Oil can be heated to above
402.85 °C
/
757.13 °F
(turning point + 3 °C) and below
541.85 °C
/
1007.33 °F
(vaporization point + 3 °C) to get Petroleum with 100% efficiency. It can be done by venting
heat
from nearby
magma
, or by slightly heating oil from a
Leaky Oil Fissure
.
Despite Petroleum also being a liquid, Crude Oil that converts into Petroleum while in a pipe will still burst the pipe.
Hence, using
Metal Refining
with
Liquid Reservoirs
to convert them would be a bad idea.
On the other hand
, this method can be used to preheat Crude Oil. A
Thermo Sensor
confined in a one-tile insulated cavity that is heated by a one-tile segment of
Radiant Liquid Pipe
can be used to control the process.
Outside of recipes, crude oil is useful in of itself.
Crude Oil's great temperature range
-40.15 °C ↔ 399.85 °C
/
-40.27 °F ↔ 751.73 °F
makes it resilient to freezing/boiling when used for
liquid airlocks
Crude Oil can be heated to steam temperatures, making it a good choice as a
Metal Refinery
coolant when used in conjunction with
Steam Turbines
Crude Oil can be be chilled to fairly low temperatures through a
Thermo Aquatuner
which can be used to maintain
Deep Freeze
to prevent food spoilage, keep a farm chilly (.e.
Bonbon Trees
), or to condense
Chlorine
. However, It is not advised to use as a general coolant for aquatuners due to its low Specific Heat Capacity compared to
Water
and
Super Coolant
.
Crude Oil
is a
Gear Oil
that can be used to lubricate
Bionic Duplicants
or
Remote Workers
.
Trivia
Before the
Automation Upgrade
, Crude Oil could not freeze. This made it an excellent tool for cooling.
