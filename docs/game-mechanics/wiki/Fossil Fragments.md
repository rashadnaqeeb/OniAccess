Fossil Fragments
are
terrain features
found near fossil dig sites as part of the
Ancient Specimen
story trait
. They can be excavated by a Duplicant with the
Masterworks
skill
, which destroys them but drops their mass in
Fossil
.
Fossil Fragments come in two sizes:
Large
(2x2), which consists of 2000 kg of Fossil, and
Small
(1x2), which is comprised of 1500 kg of Fossil.
To be excavated, fossil must not be entombed and must fully stand on a floor.
Tips
If a fossil isn't being excavated after a floor is rebuilt underneath it, you may need to cancel and restart the excavation task, or reload the game.
