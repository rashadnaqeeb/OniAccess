# ONI Geyser Calculator

Geysers cycle between active and inactive states, making it difficult for players to understand the quantity of resources made available to them by the geyser. There are fan-produced Geyser Calculators that players can use to calculate the average output of a
geyser
given information about the geyser, and will also show how many machines or plants can be sustained by that geyser:
