Vertical Wind Tunnel
is used during
downtime
to improve a duplicants'
morale
and reduce
stress

Each use of the Vertical Wind Tunnel takes 90 seconds.  Using the Vertical Wind Tunnel may cause Popped Eardrums in areas with poor circulation if gas pressure exceeds 4 kg.
Trivia
When using the Vertical Wind Tunnel, if the 7th center tile is non-solid and the 8th center tile is solid, that solid tile loses mass constantly.
