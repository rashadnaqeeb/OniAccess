Radioactive Contaminants are a "
Germ
" designed to represent the effects of nuclear processes on materials. Contaminates can only be created by a
Research Reactor
, the
Radbolt Engine
, a
Radbolt
passing through a medium, or an
Atomic Power Bank
being destroyed.
Contaminated materials will release
Radiation
at the rate of 1 Rad per Cycle per 1000 Contaminants.
Contaminants have a half life of 1 Cycle which cannot be affected by environmental factors such as
Temparature
or the material they're attached to.
Contaminants do not cause any disease directly but the ongoing radiation exposure can cause
Radiation Sickness
.
