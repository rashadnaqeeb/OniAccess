Water Sieve
uses a
Filtration Medium
to purify
Polluted Water
. It needs to be supplied a Filtration Medium and receive Polluted Water in a
Liquid Pipe
on its Input port to function. It will output
Water
to the
Liquid Pipe
on its output slot. It will drop
Polluted Dirt
on the floor when its internal storage is full (
20 kg
).
The Water Sieve does not change the water temperature.
Usage
The Water Sieve is a very helpful building as
Water
is a very important resource. It allows a colony to operate structures like the
Carbon Skimmer
or the
Lavatory
without consuming clean water by instead consuming
Filtration Medium
which is often far more abundant. Water Sieve is required to make use of the
Polluted Water
found in the
Marsh Biome
,
Swampy Biome
, and
Wetlands Biome
.
Ratios
1 constantly running Water Sieve can process up to 3 tons of water per
cycle
. This can supply 300 combined
Lavatory
and
Sink
uses per cycle, or 100
Shower
uses.
2 Water Sieves are needed to filter a full pipe of
Polluted Water
.
1 Sieve can filter 5 active
Carbon Skimmers
.
2
Compost
piles, 2
Pokeshells
or 1
Sage Hatch
are needed to consume the
Polluted Dirt
from 1 Water Sieve that is always active.
Tips
If a Water Sieve is out of
Filtration Medium
, it will stop receiving any liquid from its Intake Pipe.
Conversely, when it is in the Pipe Blocked status, it will stop demanding
Filtration Medium
, which is a commmon cause of
Duplicants
dropping
Filtration Medium
.
A Water Sieve will not be damaged by clean
Water
,
Salt Water
, or
Brine
. It will simply pass it along and does not change its temperature.
Filtering Water does not remove any
germs
contained in it. Be sure to filter your main water supply and waste water using separate Sieves. Germs in a Water Sieve will proliferate and spread to any future water filtered.
This is only relevant if the Water is being used to prepare
food
at a
Microbe Musher
, or used to fill the
Water Cooler
,
Espresso Machine
,
Juicer
, or
Soda Fountain
.
Polluted Water
contaminated with
Slimelung
will override
Food Poisoning
when combined in the same storage. This can be achieved using an Algae Distiller with Slime, or by having a Duplicant covered in Slimelung use a
Sink
.
