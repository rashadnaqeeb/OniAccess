Gold Amalgam
is a
Metal Ore
found in the
Marsh Biome
in small to modest abundance. It is used as a specialist construction material and can be refined into pure
Gold
.
Gold Amalgam can also be brought by
Meteor Showers
in
Space Biomes
.
Usage
Gold Amalgam can be used in similar fashion to other Metal Ores, and is typically found in small to medium sized nodes in marsh biomes. Gold Amalgam shares the 10% decor bonus with
Copper Ore
, and provides anything created from it with a 50 degree higher overheat temperature. The latter property is useful for allowing machinery that would normally overheat in very hot areas such as steam vents. However, it is fairly poor at general temperature regulation, as it has extremely low Specific Heat Capacity and the lowest Thermal Conductivity among metal ores. Meaning that machines made from Gold heat up quickly, and dissipate their heat slowly.
Iron Ore
has triple the Heat Capacity, and
Aluminum Ore
has six times the Heat Capacity which allow those metals to absorb significantly more heat even with the lower overheat temperature.
It is beat out by
Wolframite
when used as material for structures intended for
heat
exchange, but thanks to heat exchange mechanics this is usually a trivial difference.
It can be refined into
Gold
in the
Metal Refinery
.
100 kg Gold Amalgam = 100 kg Gold.
Rock Crusher
can break Gold Amalgam into Gold and
Sand
.
100 kg Gold Amalgam = 50 kg Gold + 50 kg Sand.
Stone Hatches
can eat Gold Amalgam and excrete
Coal
. It will increase the chance of laying Smooth Hatch eggs.
Smooth Hatches can eat Gold Amalgam and excrete Gold.
