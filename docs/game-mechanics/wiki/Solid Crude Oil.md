is the Solid state of
Crude Oil
.
It can be found in oil biomes on the
Rime Asteroid
and
Sweet Ceres Asteroid
. Due to the fact that half of the mass is destroyed when mined, generally it is a better idea to melt it on location via a
Liquid Tepidizer
or warmer oil from an
Oil Well
.
