# Briar Seed

Briar Seed
Type
Seed
The
Briar Seed
can be obtained by uprooting
Bluff Briars
, by digging up a
Buried Object
within the
Temperate Biome
, or as a random outcome of the
Printing Pod
. It grows into a Bluff Briar when planted.
Usage
Briar Seeds can be planted in
Flower Pots
to increase decor.
