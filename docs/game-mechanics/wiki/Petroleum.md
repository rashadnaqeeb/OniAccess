Petroleum
is a late game, powerful fuel for
Power
generation and
Rocket
engines. It stacks up to 740 kg/tile when not pressurized.
Occurrences
Petroleum can appear naturally if some map regions are sufficiently overheated, which leads to
Crude Oil
transformation or Molten Slicksters births.
Production
Molten Slicksters
produce 10 kg of Petroleum by consuming 20 kg of
Carbon Dioxide
per cycle.
Petroleum can be obtained from Crude Oil using an
Oil Refinery
with 50 % efficiency:
10 kg/s
Crude Oil
= 5 kg/s Petroleum + 90 g/s
Natural Gas
.
Alternatively, Crude Oil can be heated above
402.85 °C
/
757.13 °F
(turning point + 3 °C) and below
541.85 °C
/
1007.33 °F
(vaporization point + 3 °C), converting it into Petroleum with 100 % efficiency.
Gunk
may also be heated in order to convert 92% of its mass into Petroleum and 8% into
Sulfur
respectively.
Usage
Petroleum can be heated to
541.85 °C
/
1007.33 °F
(vaporization point + 3 °C) and converted to
Sour Gas
, which in turn can be cooled to
-164.5 °C
/
-264.1 °F
(turning point - 3 °C), turning it into 66.7% liquid
Methane
and 33.3% solid
Sulfur
.
Polymer Press
transforms Petroleum into
Plastic
.
Petroleum Generator
can burn Petroleum to generate
Power
.
Petroleum Engine
burns Petroleum as a rocket fuel.
Blastshot Maker
consumes Petroleum to make
Blastshot
.
Exosuit Forge
uses Petroleum to produce
Jet Suits
.
Jet Suit Docks
consume it to refill exosuit's jetpacks.
Emulsifier
consumes Petroleum with:
Isosap
to produce
Visco-Gel
.
Fullerene
and
Gold
to produce
Super Coolant
.
Outside of recipes, petroleum is useful in of itself.
Petroleum's great temperature range
-57.15 °C ↔ 538.85 °C
/
-70.87 °F ↔ 1001.93 °F
makes it resilient to freezing/boiling when used for
liquid airlocks
Petroleum can be heated to steam temperatures, making it a good choice as a
Metal Refinery
coolant when used in conjunction with
Steam Turbines
Petroleum can be be chilled to fairly low temperatures through a
Thermo Aquatuner
which can be used to maintain
Deep Freeze
to prevent food spoilage, keep a farm chilly (.e.
Bonbon Trees
), or to condense
Chlorine
. However, It is not advised to use as a general coolant for aquatuners due to its low Specific Heat Capacity compared to
Water
and
Super Coolant
