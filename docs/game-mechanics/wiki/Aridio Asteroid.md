Aridio Asteroid
is an
Asteroid Type
in the game of
Oxygen Not Included
. It is a hotter variant of
Verdante
— in particular, the starting
Forest Biome
is a little hotter than
30 °C
/
86 °F
from the very start, compared to other asteroids (where it is approximately
22 °C
/
71.6 °F
), making food production a challenge early on.
Map Layout
Example Aridio world. Notice the complete lack of
Tundra Biomes
.
Strategy
There are some challenge in the early game as the
Forest Biome
contains little
Water
that can be
electrolyzed
into
Oxygen
. However, there is plenty of
Polluted Water
in the
Marsh Biomes
which should last more than long enough to exploit a water
Geyser
.
Wolframite
does not spawn anywhere on Aridio, unless the map has the Glaciers or Frozen Core
World Traits
. The only source of this metal is from
Space Destinations
, making obtaining
Niobium
rather difficult, even in the late game.
While
Hatches
do not normally generate anywhere on Aridio, one can be found buried to the left of the
Printing Pod
.
Bristle Blossoms
,
Wheezeworts
, and
Sleet Wheat
are not obtainable on the asteroid itself and can only be obtained from the
Printing Pod
with
Care Packages
enabled or from
Space Destinations
.
