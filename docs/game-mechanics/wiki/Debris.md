# Debris

Debris are one of the two main forms of substances in Oxygen Not Included.
Debris do not collide with each other and can be infinitely stacked in any location. Bottles made normally have a maximum mass of
25 tons
, though it is possible to exceed this limit
[
1
]
. They fall, making a noise when they hit a
Solid
or
Liquid
.
Items on
Conveyor Rails
and in
Building
inventories are actually modelled as debris at the
Tile of Interest
.
The
Canister Filler
creates gasses in debris form and the
Bottle Filler
creates liquids in debris form. Duplicant mining will create solid debris.
Main article:
Solid
Phase transitions of gasses and liquids will create solid debris as long as they are less than 80% of the solid's default mass. Phase transitions of solids will always create solid natural tiles.
Main article:
Auto-Sweeper
Most solid debris can be picked up by an
Auto-Sweeper
. Liquid bottles and gas canisters cannot be picked up by Auto-Sweepers unless they are a
Refined Metal
.
Main article:
Thermal Conductivity
Debris transfer heat only to the
Natural Tile
that it is on top of (if it is a
Solid
) and the one that it is in. This is true even if it is on a
Conveyor Rail
or in midair and is not visually resting on the tile below it.
Main article:
Element Emission
Some debris emit other elements when the tile it is in has sufficiently low mass and is not a solid.
