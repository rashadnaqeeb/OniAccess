Mini-Pod
A portable Bioprinter that produces new Duplicants or Care Packages containing resources.
Only one Printing Pod or Mini-Pod is permitted per
Planetoid
.
A quick and easy substitute, though it'll never live up to the original.

The
Mini-Pod
follows the same rules as the
Printing Pod
. All the Mini-Pods share one cooldown timer with the Printing Pod. This means printing a
Duplicant
or a
Care Package
will reset the cooldown timer on the main Printing Pod and other Mini-Pods.
Once built, a Mini-Pod cannot be deconstructed, and no other Mini-Pod can be constructed on the same planetoid. It can still be melted according to its building material melting temperature.
