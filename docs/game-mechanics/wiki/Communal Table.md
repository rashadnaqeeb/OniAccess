Communal Table
is a public furniture item to which
Duplicants
can use to consume their
food
. Communal Tables reduce
stress
from eating off the floor and is the main component for the
Banquet Hall
Room
. They can also satisfy the
Dining Table
room requirement for both a
Mess Hall
and a
Great Hall
. It can store up to
15 g
Table Salt
and both consumes
1 g
Table Salt and grants the duplicant +1 morale per use. Eating food alongside one or more duplicants grants an additional +1 morale bonus.
Tips
Creating a dedicated
Mess Hall
,
Great Hall
, or
Banquet Hall
is highly recommended. Making this area much nicer than the rest of your colony is an easy way to reduce stress. Lavishing the area with lamps, art, and high decor items amplifies the de-stressing effect. Including a
Ration Box
or ideally a
Refrigerator
with the setup saves time and stores food efficiently.
Rather than building multiple tables, having Downtime schedules offset from one another is a good way to have a single Communal Table handle dozens of Duplicants.
Wood
may not be readily available on all asteroid starts. Instead,
Plywood
can be created at a
Plywood Press
after salvaging
Plant Husk
from plants, and cracking
Shine Bug
eggs for
Resin
. Resin can also be processed from
Amber
which drops in large amounts from
Lura Plants
.
Making a Dining Hall close to food production areas saves Duplicants the hassle of having to transport food long distances. Duplicants will automatically use an assigned dining table, and otherwise use a public dining table.
