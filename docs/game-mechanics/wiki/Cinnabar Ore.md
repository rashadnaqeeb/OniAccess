Cinnabar Ore
is a
Metal Ore
found in the
Ice Cave Biome
. It is exclusive to the
Frosty Planet
DLC. It can be used to construct buildings that require Metal Ore, or it can be refined into
Mercury
.
Usage
Most of Cinnabar Ore's characteristics are identical to that of
Copper Ore
, which makes it ideal as a general-use Metal Ore. However, its melting point is quite low in comparison to that of most Metal Ores (
583.5 °C
/
1082.3 °F
). Therefore, its use in extreme-temperature applications should be avoided.
100kg of Cinnabar Ore can be crushed into 50kg of
Mercury
and 50kg of
Sand
at a
Rock Crusher
.
100kg of Cinnabar Ore can be refined into 100kg of Mercury at a
Metal Refinery
.
Cinnabar Ore can be fed to some
Critters
:
Plug Slugs
consume 60kg/cycle of Cinnabar Ore and convert it into 3kg/cycle of
Hydrogen
.
Smog Slugs
and
Sponge Slugs
consume 30kg/cycle of Cinnabar Ore and convert it into 1.5kg/cycle of Hydrogen.
Production
Cinnabar Ore can be mined from the Ice Cave Biome located within the
Ceres Asteroid
.
Cinnabar Ore can be extracted from the
Ceres Ore Field
Space POI
.
