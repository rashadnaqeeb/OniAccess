The
Critter Cargo Bay
is a rocket module that allows interplanetary transport of
Critters
outside the Spacefarer Module. It can store one single critter.
Once you have selected the type of critter in the menu, a dupe will deliver a captured critter, similar to a
Critter Drop-Off
. Critters can be captured using a
Critter Trap
or through being wrangled by a duplicant with Ranching
skill
. Freshly hatched critters inside an
Incubator
also count as wrangled.
To release the critter from the module you can click the "Remove" button. This drops the critter already wrangled until it hits solid ground.
