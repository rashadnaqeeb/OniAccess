Behavior
Not much is known about the Gassy Moo due to its alien origin and nature. They are capable of flight and can survive in any atmosphere, or even lack thereof.
They do not technically reproduce. However, allowing a Gassy Moo to feed on
Gas Grass
or
Plant Husk
will cause the Moo to gain the "Welcoming Moo'd" buff, which will increase both "Accu-moo-lation" at a rate of 6.25% per cycle, as well as increasing "Brackene Supply" at a rate of 25% per cycle. The duration of Welcoming Moo is proportional to
1
0
9
the amount of calories it consumes. In other words, if a Moo is well-fed for 9 cycles, it will have 1 cycle of Welcoming Moo leftover on the 9th cycle.
When a Gassy Moo reaches 100% Accu-Moo-Lation and find themselves with a view into space, they will call out for another, untamed Gassy Moo to join them on the asteroid via
Gassy Mooteor
, effectively reproducing at a rate of once every 16 cycles. If a Gassy Moo is not directly within sunlight, then they will receive the "Moo-ted" Debuff. This only serves as a signifier to the player that the Moo cannot reproduce in those conditions, and does not provide additional debuffs.
When a Moo finishes singing, a mooteor spawns aimed at the location of the singing Moo within a 5-cell radius (picked randomly). The mooteor normally falls down from left to right at a 15 degree angle at the top of the world, but if that spawn point falls outside the world, it will instead come from the opposite direction.
When a Gassy Moo reaches 100% Brackene Supply, they can be milked at the
Milking Station
. This will produce
200 kg
of
Brackene
, meaning one Moo will produce roughly
50 kg
of
Brackene
every cycle.
Moos eat
Gas Grass
directly from the farm tiles, much like Dreckos. A single Moo can eat 2
Gas Grass
plants at constant growth, producing
10 kg
of
Natural Gas
/cycle. 11 Moos can sustain two
Natural Gas Generators
running at all times.
Gassy Moo is a peaceful critter and does not fight back when attacked by Duplicants.

Ranch construction
Gassy moo are affected differently by Happiness compared to other critters. Their unique reproduction is tied closer to its critter metabolism rather than the actual reproduction rate, so a satisfied gassy moo is just as effective as a happy gassy moo. This allows adding an additional 4 gassy moo per stable (i.e. 5 gassy moos can fit in a 16 tile stable)
Natural Gas
absorbs the most
light
out of the elements that are gases at the required temperatures, followed by
Chlorine
,
Carbon Dioxide

Polluted Oxygen

Gas Grass

As Gassy Moo are 2x2 Flying critters, they can not travel to the right-most cell of a room. Avoid placing Husbandry buildings like

Little is currently known of the Moo due to its alien nature and origin.
It is capable of surviving in zero gravity conditions and no atmosphere, and its method of reproduction has yet to be discovered.
The Moo has an even temperament and cohabits well with others in a farm setting.
