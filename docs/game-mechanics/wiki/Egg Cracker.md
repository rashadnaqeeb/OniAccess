# Egg Cracker

Egg Cracker
Converts viable Critter eggs into cooking ingredients.
Cracked Eggs cannot hatch.
Duplicants will not crack eggs unless tasks are queued.
Raw eggs are an ingredient in certain high quality food recipes.
