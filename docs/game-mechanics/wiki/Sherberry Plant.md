Sherberry Plant
Edible Plant
The semi-parasitic Sherberry plant leeches moisture and trace minerals from the primordial ice formations in which it grows.
The fruit of this varietal contains low levels of stomach-upsetting phoratoxins which, while not fatal, do serve as strong motivation for foragers to seek out additional sources of nutrition.
