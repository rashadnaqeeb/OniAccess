The
Artifact Transport Module
is a Module that lets
Rockets
acquire
Artifacts
from points of interest on the
Starmap
. Upon reaching a point of interest, the artifact is automatically added to the
Artifact Transport Module
.
Recovered Artifacts must be manually removed from the
Artifact Transport Module
once the Rocket is landed.
Recovering Artifacts is instantly done upon reaching the point of interest, although it must be the destination of the rocket as opposed to a tile the rocket passes through. (This previously required a
Drillcone
, but with more recent updates is no longer the case. Only an
Artifact Transport Module
is needed now.)
Multiple Artifact Transport Modules can be installed on a rocket, but only one artifact will be retrieved from a point of interest at a time; however it can be useful to have extra modules so that the rocket can collect artifacts from several points of interest in one mission.
Unlike all other space resources a rocket does not need to stop on the tile to collect the artifact, it can just pass through it.
Tips
Due to their small height and comparatively large burden, Artifact Transport Modules significantly slows down the speed of rockets. This can be intentionally used to slow down rockets to train the Piloting attribute of duplicants, or increase the uptime of engines that produce power, such as
Sugar Engine
and
Steam Engine
.
