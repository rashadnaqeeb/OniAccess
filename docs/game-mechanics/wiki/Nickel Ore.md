Nickel Ore

Resources

Sources
Nickel ore tiles only exist in the Garden Biome.
Usage
Nickel ore is the first metal ore you are likely to encounter when starting a game in the Garden Biome.
Metal Refinery
can refine Nickel Ore into
Nickel
.
100 kg Nickel Ore = 100 kg Nickel.
Rock Crusher
can break Nickel Ore into Nickel and
Sand
.
100 kg Nickel Ore = 50 kg Nickel + 50 kg Sand.
Stone Hatches
can eat Nickel Ore and excrete
Coal
. It will increase the chance of laying Smooth Hatch eggs.
Smooth Hatches
can eat Nickel Ore and excrete Nickel.
Crafting Station
can manufacture
Oxygen Masks
and
Metal Power Banks
from Nickel Ore.
50 kg Nickel Ore = 1 Oxygen Mask.
200 kg Nickel Ore = 1 Metal Power Bank.
