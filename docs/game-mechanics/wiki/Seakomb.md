Seakomb
is a plant grown underwater. It's a source of
Pacu
food and can be processed into
Phyto Oil
. The
Phyto Oil
can in turn be processed into
Biodiesel
.
Seed
The
Seakomb Seed
can be obtained by harvesting and uprooting
Seakomb
or by digging up a Buried Object within the Wetlands Biome.
Usage
Seakomb can be harvested to yield
Seakomb Leaves
and
Seakomb Seeds
. Seakomb Leaves can be fed to
Pacus
, used in
Allergy Medication
or processed into
Phyto Oil
.
Feeding Pacu
A happy pacu produces 1.5 egg/cycle, so 666.7 kcal/cycle pacu fillet, or 1066.7 kcal/cycle cooked seafood.
1 dupe need 1000 kcal per cycle, approximately 1 pacu's production.
Pacus
will eat 40% growth/cycle which is equivalent to 20 kg/cycle of Seakomb Leaves. 1 pacu requires 2 seakomb plants, so 20 kg/cycle polluted dirt.
Considering pacu's poop, this means 10 kg/cycle of polluted dirt as input per dupe.
Growth
A Seakomb requires the temperature range of
-10 °C ↔ 85 °C
/
14 °F ↔ 185 °F
, and atmosphere of
Water
,
Polluted Water
,
Salt Water
,
Brine
,
Phyto Oil
or
Resin
.
Wild
A wild Seakomb takes 20 cycles to grow.
Domestic
A domestic Seakomb can be planted in
Farm Tiles
or
Hydroponic Farms
with a downwards orientation. It requires 10 kg/cycle of
Polluted Dirt
.
A domestic Seakomb takes 5 cycles to grow.
Notes
Pips can't plant the seakomb in liquid, but they can plant it like other hanging plants. This causes the seakomb to stiffen until its atmosphere is satisfied.
Seakombs and
Waterweeds
are the only plants that require to be submerged in liquids in order to grow.
