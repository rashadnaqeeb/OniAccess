Plug Slugs are dynamic creatures that generate electrical Power during the night.
Their power can be harnessed by leaving an exposed wire near areas where they like to sleep.
Smog Slugs are porous creatures that draw in unbreathable Gases during the day.
At night, they sleep near exposed Gas Pipes, where they deposit their cache.
Sponge Slugs are thirsty creatures that soak up Liquids during the day.
They deposit their stored Liquids into the exposed Liquid Pipes they sleep next to at night.

Plug Slug
is a
Critter
found in the
Swampy Biome (Spaced Out)
. They produce
Hydrogen
,
Eggs
,
Meat
, and generate
Power
. They can be domesticated. The Morphs act as biological pumps for non breathable gasses and liquids.
Behavior
Similar to
Dreckos
and
Pips
, Plug Slugs are able to scale walls and walk along the ceiling. During the night Plug Slugs will nest on a ceiling and hang down, creating a socket two tiles below the ceiling (the lower tile of the slug) from which power can be drained. They will prefer to plug themselves onto exposed wires. Two Slugs cannot sleep on the same tile, and their socket cannot overlap buildings such as
Storage Bins
.
They emit up to 400 Watts if wild, or up to 1600 Watts when tamed. The output for slugs will be lower if they are hungry, down to 40 W when starving.
Since
Nighttime
lasts 75 seconds on a 600s cycle, this means they generate on average up to 50W when wild or 200W when tame (up to 3
basic batteries
or 3/4 of
Jumbo Battery
per wild slug per night). For reference, this puts a fully fed domesticated Plug Slug (or four wild) on par with a
Manual Generator
.
Plug Slugs can eat all kinds of unrefined
Metal Ores
and
Refined Metal
, and excrete
Hydrogen
when they do so.
Cracking a Plug Slug Egg yields 1000g of Raw Egg and 100g of Egg Shell.
It drops 3200 kcal (2 kg) of
Meat
on death.
Location
A number of Plug Slugs can be found on the Swampy Asteroid in the
Spaced Out
DLC. This number varies, but at minimum is 5 fully mature Slugs. More will spawn when starting on the
Quagmiris Asteroid
, although the generation rules seem to be similar between the Terra & Swampy starts. Additional Plug Slugs can be printed or found in Swampy pockets on other Asteroids.
Critter Morphs
The Slug has three variants, each with the same diet preferences. Each variant has it own unique ability to manage power, liquid, or gas.
Plug Slugs which spend time in unbreathable gases increases the odds of laying a Smog Slug egg. Plug Slugs which spend time in liquids increases the odds of laying a Sponge Slug egg. See
Critter Morphs
for exact details on how egg chances work.

Well-fed
Hatches

Coal Generator
uses 1kg Coal per second to produce 600W. Effectively generating 0.6*70 = 42 kJ of energy per critter per day.
Well-fed Plug Slugs produce 1600 W of power per

Well-fed Plug Slugs also produce 3kg of
Hydrogen

This combines to a total of 144 kJ per critter per day.
If the Plug Slugs are tame but glum, then food requirement and hydrogen output is reduced by 80%, but the direct power production remains the same. Per unit of food tame-glum Plug Slugs are 5x more effecient at producing power than tame-happy Plug Slugs, and 4x more efficient than Wild Plug Slugs.
Semi-starvation exploit
Starving Plug Slugs can be fed just before night to generate 800 W instead of 40 W at night, while starving for the rest of the day. This is much more efficient per-food than feeding the Slug all day. To take this exploit even further, Plug Slugs decide how much power they will generate at the moment they become drowsy and it doesn't matter if they subsequently cross a threshold that should cause them to produce less power, this means they only need to be non-starving for a few seconds when they become non-drowsy and so only a little food need to be given right before they become drowsy (allocating enough time for the Slug to react to the food and eat it). Uses
Plug Slugs consume metals and metal ores, both of which are valuable resources that are needed in large quantities. Considering the resource consumption, Plug Slugs are hard to maintain, and are thus a suboptimal source or power. However, they do still have some niched uses, especially when taken wild ranching into account.
The other morphs of the Slugs' family, however, are not as useful as their mechanical counterparts, in addition to being hard to set-up. A better choice would be to use the power-producing morph, and pair with batteries and pumps.
Remote Colony
Wild Plug Slugs produce power for free, enabling easy access to power wherever they are brought to.
They are much easier to set up compared to some other forms of input-free power sources, such as
wild arbor
ethanol
power. This characteristic makes Slug power useful if you don't want to spend too much time on a planetoid, or don't want to bring dupes with
Mechatronics Engineering skill
In addition, it does not require access to the surface, making it a valid alternative to solar. This characteristic is useful when colonising the
Regolith Asteroid
.
Super Sustainable
The use of Plug Slugs does not disable the
Super Sustainable Achievement
, and the electricity they produce do count towards the progress.
Non-Oxygen Gas Filter
Smog Slugs only breath in gases that aren't breathable, making it useful to filter out carbon dioxide and other miscellaneous gases without relying on a
Carbon Skimmer
or filter.
Database
Plug Slug
Domesticable Critter
Plug Slugs are fuzzy gastropoda that are able to cling to walls and ceilings thanks to an extreme triboelectric effect caused by friction between their fur and various surfaces.
This same phenomomen allows the Plug Slug to generate a significant amount of static electricity that can be converted into power.
The increased amount of static electricity a Plug Slug can generate when domesticated is due to the internal vibration, or contented 'humming', they demonstrate when all their needs are met.
