Lead Suits
are a type of exosuit like the
Atmo Suit
or
Jet Suit
, crafted at the
Exosuit Forge
.
While still providing breathable
Oxygen
and protection from temperature extremes, they also protect
Duplicants
when working in high
Radiation
environments, at the cost of a further -2
Athletics
reduction compared to Atmo Suits.
Wearing a Lead Suit will protect the wearer from
Soggy Feet
and Sopping Wet debuffs,
Eye Irritation
, slipping,
Chilly and Toasty Surroundings
and
Gnit
stings.
Lead Suits do not provide immunity to Scalding, but  they do increase the Scalding temperature to
1071.85 °C
/
1961.33 °F
.
Notes
The Lead Suit contains an internal Suit battery (visualized by a Yellow Bar) that lasts for 200 seconds in temperatures above
60 °C
/
140 °F
. After the battery depletes, the battery stops working and the insulation thickness bonus falls to
0 cm
. Recharging to full capacity at a
Lead Suit Dock
takes 60 seconds, and despite its name, consumes no power.
Insulation thickness operates independently of the suit’s Scalding Threshold and Frostbite Threshold bonuses. Since Lead Suit wearers are already immune to
Toasty Surroundings
and
Chilly Surroundings
, battery depletion only becomes relevant during prolonged exposure to extreme temperatures, and only after undocking.
Perhaps more impactful to gameplay is that the suit wearer will return to dock their suits once the battery charge falls to 25%, often well before their oxygen supply is depleted.
Bugs
The In-game tooltip incorrectly list the scalding and frostbite thresholds when Temperature Units are set to Celsius, but not in Kelvin. Both have 273.15 subtracted from their real values.
