Liquid Oxidizer Tank
is a
rocket
module that stores the oxygen needed for the engines to work in the vacuum of space in the form of
Liquid Oxygen
. It can store up to 2700 kg.
Oxidizer Tanks (either Solid or Liquid) are required by a
Petroleum Engine
or a
Hydrogen Engine
. You can adjust the storage capacity between 0 kg and 2700 kg.
Liquid Oxygen is a more powerful oxidizer than
Oxylite
, and can increase the rocket range by 33%, before the weight penalty is taken into account.
Duplicants will remove excessive liquid oxidizer as bottles of
Liquid Oxygen
.
