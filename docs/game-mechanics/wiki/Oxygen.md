Oxygen
is one of the
Resources
in the game
Oxygen Not Included
. It is a
Breathable Gas
and required by
Duplicants
to survive. It can be produced by machines (
Oxygen Diffuser
,
Electrolyzer
,
Rust Deoxidizer
), filtered from
Polluted Oxygen
by
Deodorizers
or cooling to liquid form, or a small amount created by the
Algae Terrarium

Mechanics
Oxygen is one of the lightest gases. Oxygen can be breathed in by
Duplicants
and exhaled as
Carbon Dioxide
. Due to the fact that Oxygen is lighter than Carbon Dioxide it will rise above it.
Although Duplicants require Oxygen to live, Oxygen is only breathable in high enough quantities (anything below 50 g/tile is unbreathable while anything between 50 and 300 g/tile is breathable but will
stress
Duplicants).
Duplicants typically use 100 grams of Oxygen per second (200 g/s with
Mouth Breather
trait, 75 g/s with
Diver's Lungs
, 50 g/s with
Deeper Diver's Lungs
, and 25 g/s with both). If the pressure is high enough they breathe normally, if it is non-breathable they will hold their breath until breath meter reaches 50%, then stop any task and run to closest spot to refill their lungs. If their breath meter reaches zero, they suffocate to death.
Usage
Atmo Suit
,
Jet Suit
,
Oxygen Mask
, and
Lead Suit Docks
require Oxygen to (re)fill their respective suits.
The
Telescope
requires Oxygen to work.
The
Oxylite Refinery
produces
Oxylite
from Oxygen and
Gold
. 600 g/s Oxygen + 3 g/s Gold = 600 g/s Oxylite.
Dense Pufts
and Puft Princes can breathe Oxygen and excrete
Oxylite
.
Longhair Slicksters
consume 30 kg/cycle. and provide some mobile
Decor
.
When cooled down to liquid state,
Liquid Oxygen
is a better rocket oxidizer that can be used by
Petroleum Engine
and
Hydrogen Engine
.
Although Oxygen has properties that are subpar for fast heat exchanges, applications requiring slow heat exchanges are best performed with Oxygen flowing through a circuit, with
Radiant piping
at the heat source and the heat sink. For example, maintaining the temperature of the Hydrogen in the Ranch for
Dreckos
can be done by channelling a dedicated circuit through the Ranch with the heat sink being a cold biome; thus, the ranch will not need cooling via objects that may take up foreground space, e.g.
Wheezeworts
.
The main advantage of using Oxygen is that the Oxygen can just mix with any Oxygen that is outside the circuit if there is any maintenance or modification that has to be done to the piping while Oxygen is still in it.
Oxygen is also much more available than other gases.
Production
Natural
Oxylite
emits Oxygen. 5 tiles of Oxylite are guaranteed near the
Printing Pod
to provide Oxygen for the first few cycles.
Oxygen Diffusers
produce Oxygen from
Algae
. Although this may seem efficient at the start, as the game progresses there is not as much Algae to use.
Electrolyzers
produce Oxygen from
Water
. 1 kg/s Water = 888 g/s Oxygen + 112 g/s
Hydrogen
. The Hydrogen needs proper handling otherwise it will make the air unbreathable.
Rust Deoxidizer
can produce Oxygen,
Chlorine
and
Iron Ore
from
Rust
and
Salt
. 750 g/s Rust + 250 g/s Salt = 570 g/s Oxygen + 30 g/s Chlorine + 400 g/s Iron Ore.
Algae Terrariums
produce Oxygen from Water and Algae. They also consume a tiny amount of nearby
Carbon Dioxide
, if present. They do not require power, but can be made more efficient when near a light source.
Oxyferns
and
Alveo Veras
produce Oxygen from Carbon Dioxide and water/ice.
The
Deodorizer
purifies
Polluted Oxygen
into Oxygen using
Sand
or
Regolith
as a filtration medium.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Exploded Ice Giants at a rate of 81 – 243 kg per cycle, as a gas.
In Ice Asteroid Fields at a rate of 27 – 81 kg per cycle, as a solid.
