Connects to an Automation grid to automatically turn Gas flow off when the specified amount has passed through it.

Limit
A gas meter has a limit setting:
the minimum limit is 0 kg;
the maximum limit is 500 kg;
the minimum, nonzero limit is 0.001 kg (1 g);
