Lumen Quartz
Lumen quartz emits a soft glowing
Light
that makes everything look and feel a little rosier.
It's even brighter and more beautiful once it's been carved.
Dimensions
1×1 tiles
Category
Special
Decor
+5 (Radius: 1 tile)
Effects
Light
: 1000 lux
Light range: 2 tiles
Lumen Quartz
are naturally-occurring structures that emit a small amount of
Light
and
Decor
in their vicinity.
They can be chiseled into
Carved Lumen Quartz
by a
Duplicant
to make them movable, increasing their Light and Decor values in the process.
Production
Small chunks of non-renewable Lumen Quartz can be found naturally occurring in the
Cool Pool Biome
.
