The
Hydrogen Engine
is the 7th and final engine in
Spaced Out!
, having the longest range and power out of all the engines. It is unlocked along with the
Liquid Oxidizer Tank
in
Cryofuel Combustion
.
Like most other engines, the
Hydrogen Engine
cannot
store its own fuel, and needs to be stored in
Large Liquid Fuel Tanks
, similar to the
Petroleum Engine
. Like with the Petroleum Engine, it needs oxidizer to function, with
Liquid Oxygen
being the most efficient. Liquid oxygen will need to be stored in a
Liquid Oxidizer Tank
.
Exhaust
In addition to the dry exhaust present in all rocket engines, the
Hydrogen Engine
produces
1726.85 °C
/
3140.33 °F
Steam
at 50kg/s. This creates tremendous amounts of heat and
Steam
that can be harvested and is tremendously mass-positive depending on the length the rocket travels.
However, not only does the heat risk damaging liquid pipes, but it is also hot enough to melt most
Refined Metal
. While the rocket and the
Rocket Platform
cannot melt, it can still melt rocket loaders/unloaders, power and automation wires, and overheat machinery like robominers, bunker doors, and rocket extensions. It is advised to use
Tungsten
for any buildings with little mass such as wires to prevent melting. It will also boil most common liquids, making it harder to transfer heat away from exposed components or operate liquid airlocks without an active cooling system to move heat away between launches.
While the easiest means to handle exhaust is to simply allow it to disperse into
The Void
and clean up the aftermath, recapturing this steam provides enormous amounts of resources. To capture rocket exhaust, surround the launch path with
Drywall
or
Tempshift Plates
and
Insulated Tiles
, ideally made of
Ceramic
or
Insulite
, and use a set of
Mechanized Airlocks
which shut as soon as possible when a rocket has launched or landed.
Trivia
The
Hydrogen Engine
is currently the most powerful engine in
Spaced Out!
, being good for long range journeys and carrying large amounts of modules.
Its current description and flavor text is a word for word copy from the base game
Hydrogen Engine
.
It has no visual difference to the base game
Hydrogen Engine
.
Like other high-level engines, it produces 600W of power, which can be used with a rocket electrical port.
