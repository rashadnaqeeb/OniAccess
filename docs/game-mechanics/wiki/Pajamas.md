Pajamas
are a
clothing item
obtained from the
Pajama Cubby
as part of the
Somnium Synthesizer
story trait
. Once dispensed, Pajamas can be assigned to
Duplicants
. Unlike other clothes, they are not constructed, but they can be obtained in infinite quantity by operating the Pajama Cubby with a Duplicant. The main benefit of Pajamas is that they allow Duplicants to produce
Dream Journals
while they sleep (1 every 300 s of sleep).
They increase
Decor
by 30 and increase the insulation thickness by 0.25 cm, the same as default clothing. (This is in addition to the 0.2 cm of insulation afforded by the stunningly thick skin of Duplicants.) Pajamas also greatly reduce Duplicants'
Athletics
(lowering their running speed correspondingly) and increases their
Stamina
loss by 10%/cycle. The increased Stamina loss means that Pajama-wearing Duplicants will tire sooner each cycle, so changes to their
Schedule
may be necessary to prevent them from passing out from exhaustion.
Pajamas are a free, infinitely-obtainable item composed of 4 kg of
Coal
. If heated past
276.9 °C
/
530.42 °F
, they will turn into 4 kg of
Refined Carbon
. Note that although they are an infinite resource, Pajamas will
disappear
when a Duplicant wearing them un-equips them.
