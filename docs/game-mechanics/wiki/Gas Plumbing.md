Gas plumbing is the action of transporting
Gas
and changing its properties, such as
Temperature
and
Pressure
. Most of the properties are altered via
Gas Pipes
or
Insulated Gas Pipes
. It is needed in order to distribute gas though your base, filter gases and manage
Atmospheric Pressure
and heat. The research Gas Piping is needed in order to start plumbing gas.

Tips
If you have researched Gas Piping and Advanced Filtration, you can easily distribute oxygen to your entire base and remove/filter another gasses on the area by connecting a gas filter and selecting oxygen and the gas of your choice.
Be aware that Gas Pumps, Gas Pipes and Gas Vents can be over-pressurized and stop working as well, the better way to avoid this is by installing Gas Permeable Tiles over a room or putting a mechanized airlock on someplace and connect a lever on it so the atmosphere will change its pressure and composition.
