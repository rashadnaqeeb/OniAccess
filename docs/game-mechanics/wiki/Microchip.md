# Microchip

Microchip
A specialized Microchip created by a professional engineer.
Tunes up Generators to increase their
Power
output.
Type
Industrial Product
Effects
Applies Engie's Tune-Up effect
Cost
Refined Metal
: 5 kg
Microchip
is created by a
Duplicant
with the Electrical Engineering
Skill
at a
Power Control Station
. It can only be used to increase the
power
output of generators.
It grants 50% power output increase without increasing fuel consumption or byproduct production for any generator except
Solar Panels
for 3
cycles
. To do so, an Electrical Engineer will pick up a single Microchip and perform the Tinkering errand on a viable generator; in other words, one in a
Power Plant room
.
Tips
Lead
is the easiest to come by Refined Metal and, due to its low melting point and overheat temperature penalty, not of much use for building while being abundant in the
Oil Biome
, so it is a good idea to make Microchip exclusively from Lead.
Trivia
Although it is made from Refined Metal, the Microchip item's primary element is
Genetic Ooze
. This allows it to be used on power generators that are in very hot places, for whatever reason.
The Bionic Booster Pack
In
The Bionic Booster Pack
, microchips are generated during
Bionic Duplicant
defragmentation.
They are used at the
Crafting Station
and
Soldering Station
to craft Boosters.
