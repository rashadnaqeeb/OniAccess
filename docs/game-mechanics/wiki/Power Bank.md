# Power Bank

This article is related to
The Bionic Booster Pack
content.
A
Power Bank
holds a small amount of energy and can power
Bionic Duplicants
,
Flydos
,
Compact Discharger
or
Large Discharger
.
All Power Banks have a capacity of 120 kJ.
Any Power Bank kept wet will be damaged and explode. This occurs when the Power Bank is exposed to Water-Based liquids
Water
,
Polluted Water
,
Salt Water
and
Brine
).
Note that this does not occur in other watery liquids (such as
Brackene
or
Nectar
) or to conductive liquids (such as
Mercury
or
Molten Salt
).
Water damage does not occur to Power Banks that are inside storage, such as when it's on a
Conveyor Rail
or when inside a
Storage Bin
.
Water damage does not occur to Power Banks entombed in tiles.
Water damage does not occur to empty
Eco Power Banks
.
When
Eco Power Banks
,
Metal Power Banks
or
Uranium Ore Power Banks
explode, the energy left over in the power bank is converted into heat and added into the cell it is in at a conversion rate of 1 kJ → 1mDTU, up to a maximum of
9725.85 °C
/
17538.53 °F
.
Eco Power Banks
will also leave a
Power Bank Scrap
behind, while
Metal Power Banks
and
Uranium Ore Power Banks
do not leave anything behind.
When
Atomic Power Banks
explode either from water damage or expiration,
10 kg
of
2726.85 °C
/
4940.33 °F
Nuclear Waste
with
5,000,000
germs of
Radioactive Contaminant
spawns at the cell the battery exploded at. The object containing the
Atomic Power Bank
takes 500 damage. Nearby objects in a 6x6 area are flung around randomly.
