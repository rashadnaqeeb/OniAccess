Outhouse
is a
building
used by
Duplicants
to empty their bladders. It requires no plumbing, and instead uses
Dirt
and requires periodic emptying by a Duplicant. The Outhouse produces
Polluted Dirt
as well as
Food Poisoning
Germs
, some of which are left on each Duplicant that uses it.
Usage
The Outhouse is a simple way to provide a place for Duplicants to relieve themselves, and thus reduce
Stress
and the spread of
Diseases
.
It fulfills the 'Toilet' criteria for the
Latrine
in the
Room Overlay
, which provides a +1
Morale
bonus. Note the
Washroom
does not allow having an Outhouse in it.
It fulfills the 'Toilet' criteria for the
Hospital
room.
A filled outhouse will spawn a
Morb
if not emptied quickly enough.
Mechanics
The Outhouse outputs 19.7 kg of Polluted Dirt at a stable
37 °C
/
98.6 °F
with each use, which is stored within the building. After 15 uses however, the Polluted Dirt must be manually emptied by a Duplicant and the 200 kg of Dirt is consumed. This falls under the
Tidy
errand. When the Outhouse is full or being emptied, it will be considered 'Out of Order' and cannot be used by Duplicants.
One use of the Outhouse produces 200,000 Food Poisoning germs. These germs are divided among the Duplicant, the Polluted Dirt, and the Outhouse itself as follows:
Duplicant: +68,500 germs
Polluted Dirt: +95,000 germs
Outhouse: +36,500 germs
Outhouse being cleaned by a Duplicant.
Tips
Because the Outhouse must be emptied periodically, it is beneficial to have several Outhouses for your Duplicants to use.
Likewise, it is recommended to replace Outhouses with
Lavatories
, as they do not require Duplicant maintenance.
Place a
Compost
nearby to keep Duplicants from carrying Polluted Dirt all over the colony.
One Compost can reduce 60 Kg compostables per cycle, so theoretically it can serve three or fewer normal Duplicants using the same Outhouse.
A
Bionic Duplicant
still consumes
13 kg
of
Dirt

19.7 kg
of
Polluted Dirt
. However, bionic duplicants instantly clog outhouses, making them unavailable. After cleaning, whatever remaining dirt and polluted dirt in
Storage
is dropped.
