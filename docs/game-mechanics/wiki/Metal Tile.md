Heat travels much more quickly through metal tiles than other types of flooring.

Metal Tile
is a type of
tile
that is constructed from
Refined Metal
. Metal tiles provide a +50% runspeed bonus to
Duplicants
moving across them.
Usage
Metal tile prevent liquids and gases from moving through them.
Metal tiles have the second highest
Decor
value of any tile (second to
Carpeted Tile
) and apply their Decor over 3 tiles. Metal tiles, particularly those made of
Gold
or
Niobium
, can be used to boost decor without taking up space inside a room.
Shove Vole
can not dig through refined metals. Stables containing Shove Voles can use Metal Tiles to prevent them from escaping.
Due to refined metals having a high
thermal conductivity
, metal tiles can be used to conduct heat. Out of the list of refined metals, metal tiles made from
Tungsten
have the highest melting point and metal tiles made from
Thermium
have the highest thermal conductivity.
Tiles made from
Niobium
,
Steel
,
Thermium
, or
Tungsten
will not melt if exposed to
magma
. This makes them useful for dealing with
Volcanoes
.

Trivia

Meteor Shower
, despite using the same ingredients as a Bunker Tile.
