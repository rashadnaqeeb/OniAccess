Fossil
is a
Mineral
that can be found at the bottom of the map in the
Oil Biome
in moderate quantities and in small amounts in the
Ocean Biome
.  It can be used as a general construction material.
Usage
Although general buildings can be made out of Fossil, its main purpose is to be used in the
Rock Crusher
to convert into
Lime
for
Steel
production as an alternative to
Egg Shells
.  Unlike most other items used in the Rock Crusher, Fossils do not break down into
Sand
; instead, they produce Lime and
Sedimentary Rock
.
100 kg
Fossil
= 5 kg
Lime
+ 95 kg
Sedimentary Rock
Fossils are required to construct
Fossil Block

Oil Biome

Ocean Biome

Diamond

The

which generate near the points of interest for the Ancient Specimen story trait can be excavated, producing 1500 - 2000 kg of Fossil each (depending on size).
