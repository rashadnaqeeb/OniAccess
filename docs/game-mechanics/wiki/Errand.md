List of Duplicants
Duplicants
can perform
Errands
around the
colony
. Errands can be prioritized via the
priority panel and sub-priority overlay
.  High priority errands are always performed before low priority errands. However, a busy Duplicant will continue to work on their current errand until it's complete, even if a higher priority errand becomes available.
Some
duplicants
are unable to perform certain errands. For example, duplicants with the Uncultured trait cannot perform
Decorate
errands.
Some errands require a minimal level of a certain
skill
to perform, either always or for certain tasks. For example, the Decorate errand always needs the Art Fundamentals skill (quality getting better with Aesthetic Design and Masterworks), and while every duplicant can do cooking errands on a
Microbe Musher
, to do so on an
Electric Grill
, the Grilling skill is required.
! Most errands are exclusive to duplicants, i.e. they cannot be perfomed by machinery. !
