# Guide/Relica

This article is related to
The Prehistoric Planet Pack
content.
This guide is for new people.
Starting
Duplicants
: A researcher, a rancher+builder, and a builder+digger.
Grow
Sweatcorn Stalk
and turn the resulting
Sweatcorn
into
Veggie Poppers
in
Smoker
for the first 30 cycles.
After finishing the Sweatcorn farm, construct the following resource cycle. Meanwhile, print the personnel (1 farmer 2 ranchers at most, or 1 rancher minimum) needed to expand the resource cycle.
Water (can also be found naturally) can make
Oxygen
in
Electrolyzer
and can grow
Ovagro Nodes
.
Ovagro Nodes produces
Ovagro Figs
and can be fed to
Lumbs
, which will produce
Peat
and
Tough Meat
.
Tough Meat turns into
Tender Brisket
in Smoker.
Mine
Peat
. Peat turns into power and
Polluted Water
in
Peat Burner
.
Polluted Water turn into
Water
in
Water Sieve
.
This resource cycle requires an input of resources, and it produces all three survival needs. The Ovagro plants can be
Pollinated
using
Mimika
, which can turn this cycle resource positive (minus
Filtration Medium
, which actually can also be covered in this cycle, albeit require advanced setups). In addition, everything can be found in the starting
Garden Biome
. See the page on Lumbs for more info about this cycle.
Next 100 cycles: Prepare resources and personnel to make
Intracosmic Blastshots
.
More personnel for exploration and expansion. Namely diggers.
Dig down for
Oily Biome
.
A total of 5t of
Refined Metal
, 10t of
Peat
, and 20t of
Petroleum
is needed. The Peat require 1 fig-fed Lumb 50 cycles to poop. Petroleum requires 40t of
Crude Oil
if using
Oil Refinery
,which would require 12t Water pumped into
Oil Well
.
At least 1 Duplicant with high
Machinery
Attributes
. (Not required)
Automated delivery of the resource to one place. (Not required)
It takes A LOT of time (60+ cycles, data acquired from guessing) for one
Blastshot Maker
to make enough Intracosmic Blastshots if no optimisation is made, i.e., you just build it in your base, and queue the tasks up, and let a dupe with 0 Machinery Attributes to do the crafting.
The time requirement is significantly reduced if resource delivery is automated, and further if a duplicant with high Machinery Attribute is used. It is thus recommended to automate the delivery, or at least start crafting Intracosmic Blastshots while the resource is being collected.
Multiple Blastshot Makers can be used.
The diggers from the previous phase can be reallocated to these tasks.
Shoot the
Demolior
. Finish it off when it is close for closer
Space POI
generation. Optimally less that 5 space grids from the starting planetoid to allow resource harvesting using rockets with
Steam Engine (Spaced Out)
.
