Hydrogen Gas
is one of the
Resources
in the game
Oxygen Not Included
. It is an unbreathable
Gas
that is extremely light and will rise above all other gases. This means that it will settle at the top of rooms and other open areas.
Hydrogen can easily be
density-separated
with a reverse funnel at the top of your base, and collected with a
pump
when levels get too high.
Production
Hydrogen can be found naturally in small quantities in the
Jungle
and
Ocean Biome
.
It is produced alongside
Oxygen
in an
Electrolyzer
using
water
and
electricity
Hydrogen Vents
produce Hydrogen at various rates during their active states.
Plug Slugs
will produce Hydrogen after consuming
metal ores
(3kg/cycle/critter).
Saturn Critter Trap
produces 41.75 g/s (25kg/cycle) of Hydrogen while in the growing state having consumed a critter.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Exploded Gas Giants at a rate of 105 – 280 kg per cycle.
In Helium Clouds at a rate of 60 – 180 kg per cycle.
Usage
In the current build of the game, Hydrogen has several uses:
It can be burned in the
Hydrogen Generator
(100g/s) to produce 800 W of power. See
Hydrogen Generator#Practicality
.
It can be condensed into
Liquid Hydrogen
to serve as a rocket fuel for
Hydrogen Engine
, the most powerful type of rocket-engine. Condensation of Hydrogen typically requires a dedicated cooling setup using advanced materials such as
Super Coolant
.
It can be stockpiled and utilized as
heat
transfer agent due to its high thermal parameters.
Dreckos
and
Glossy Dreckos
require spending time in Hydrogen atmosphere to grow their scales of
Reed Fiber
or
Plastic
. They do not consume Hydrogen.
It is one of the best gasses to use with the
Thermo Regulator
because of its high Specific Heat Capacity, high Thermal Conductivity and the fact that remains in the gas state until extremely low temperatures. That said, for most heat transfer applications the
Thermo Aquatuner
is more practical.
Extreme temperatures, where most liquids would boil, would make the use of Hydrogen as a working fluid practical - as do low temperatures where most liquids would freeze. In particular, it can be used instead of
Super Coolant
in the processing of
Sour Gas
or production of
Liquid Oxygen
.
With the use of
Radiant Gas Piping
and how
Thermal Conductivity
works, Hydrogen's low thermal conductivity compared to liquids is not as much of an issue as it appears - however, the solid materials involved still heavily favour
Radiant Liquid Piping
, as even
Aluminum Ore
has only a fraction of the thermal conductivity of any
Refined Metal
. The exception is
Steel
,
Niobium
and
Thermium
gas pipes, which have the same thermal conductivity as their liquid counterparts but use half as much metal to build.
Cooling
Hydrogen can be used to power the
Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifier
, which produces -80 kDTU/s at the cost of only 10 g/s of Hydrogen. Venting extra hydrogen into the cold biome and then back, or using natural convection, will quickly cool your base.
Locking
Wheezeworts
in a room with a Hydrogen atmosphere maximizes their cooling efficiency (approx. -12 kDTU/s per domestic Wheezewort).
Thermo Regulators
can be put in the same room, it will prevent them from
overheating
, while they are used to cool other gases in your base (like your oxygen).
More power-efficient setup, that generates consistent, controlled temperature, will include
Mechanized Airlock
paired with
Thermo Sensor
and oil/metal buffer.
