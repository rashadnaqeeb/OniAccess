Fish Trap
is a trap used to capture fish (i.e.
Pacus
and their fry). Once captured, a
Duplicant
will bring it to a
Fish Release
.
Tips
Fish Traps are reusable and can be deconstructed to refund their material cost, like most other buildings.
The older version of this building, created in versions of the game prior to the
Song of the Moo Update
, was single-use only and did not refund its material cost; it was also constructed from 200 kg of
Plastics
, which presented difficulties when used in hot environments.
While the tooltip only mentions swimming Pacu, Fish Traps can also capture Pacus in their flopping state on land (e.g. after they have been printed in the
Printing Pod
).
Critter Traps
can also capture flopping Pacu.
