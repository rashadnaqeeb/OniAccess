Biodiesel Engine
Burns Biodiesel to propel rockets for space exploration.
Biodiesel Engines have generous height restrictions, ideal for hauling a larger number of modules.
Once the engine has been built, more rocket modules can be added.
Rockets can be used to send Duplicants into space and retrieve rare resources.

rocket

Biodiesel

Oxylite

The Biodiesel Engine does not store the fuel by itself. Additional
Liquid Fuel Tanks

Solid Oxidizer Tanks

Liquid Oxidizer Tanks

Liquid Oxygen
