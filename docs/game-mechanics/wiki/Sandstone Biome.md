# Sandstone Biome

The
Sandstone Biome
is the starting
Biome
on several
Asteroids
and
Planetoid Clusters
. It contains rich resources to help Duplicants survive in the early game.
Copper Ore
and
Sandstone
are the primary metal and mineral in this biome, respectively.
Algae
,
Dirt
, and
Coal
are also common here; all of which are essential for early oxygen, food, and power production.
Hatches
and
Shine Bugs
are plentiful in this biome; along with
Mealwood
and
Bristle Blossom
plants.
Strategy
The
Sandstone Biome
is not a difficult biome to conquer compared to other biomes in the game.
As a starting biome; it has all the essential components for a early colony to thrive and expand; although it is unlikely a colony will survive long-term without branching out past the
Granite
and
Abyssalite
into other biomes.
As a non-starting biome; it likely won't offer anything the player doesn't already have; except in
where having the
Sandstone Biome
can be a great option to get a new colony on another Planetoid started.

Sandstone Biome
The Sandstone Biome is a temperate oasis with few inherent dangers. It's the perfect spot to get your colony up and running.
Oxylite
and
Buried Muckroot
are in sufficient supply to sustain your colony while you gather more resources.
Dirt
,
Algae
,
Copper
and, of course,
Sandstone
provide all the materials to get basic colony essentials built.
Random
Shine Bugs
provide
Morale
boosts for my Duplicants but are not a reliable light source.
Hatches
can be domesticated for food or
Coal
, which will be useful if using a
Coal Generator
for power.
All in all, this biome is the perfect starting spot for my colony to establish a base full of essentials, from which they can then venture out and explore.
