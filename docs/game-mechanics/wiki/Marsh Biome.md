# Marsh Biome

The
Marsh Biome
, also known as the
Swamp Biome
(not to be confused with the
Swampy Biome
from
Spaced Out!
) is a warm and volatile
Biome
filled with
Polluted Water
,
Polluted Oxygen
, and
Slime
. Most of the slime is infested with
Slimelung
.
Strategy
Of everything that can be found in the
Marsh Biome
,
Polluted Water
and
Slime
are both the most plentiful and potentially harmful.
At the stage of the game where you would have access to this biome;
Polluted Water
should be a solved issue. If not, covering a pool of it with a small layer of water will prevent any and all off-gassing.
Slime
is the more problematic of the two. It only off-gasses after it is mined; making it far easier to control; however because it is often infested with
Slimelung
germs, off-gassing in any capacity is not ideal. Creating a pool of liquid underneath a vein of Slime (Polluted Water works great for this!) and mining the vein from the bottom so the slime drops into the liquid is an easy, if not tedious means of preventing slimelung exposure. The Use of
Oxygen Masks
or
Atmo Suits
also prevent
Duplicant
exposure to the germs.
The
Pacu
found here are an excellent source of
Lime
and
Food
; and the
Thimble Reeds
are a primary source of
Reed Fiber
.

-30 °C

Database
Marsh Biome
Polluted Oxygen
dominates the atmosphere of the Marsh Biome as it escapes from the
Slime
this biome is known for.
Marsh Biomes contain large amounts of
Slime
which can be converted into
Algae
and provide a valuable resource for growing
Dusk Caps
as well as feeding to
Pacus
for producing some higher tier
food
.
Because of the high degree of probability that this biome will infect my Duplicants with
Slimelung
, it may be prudent to limit access to this area to essential activities only until my Duplicants are able to set up suitable protection.
