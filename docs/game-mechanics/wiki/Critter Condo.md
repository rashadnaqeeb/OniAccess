critters
inside a
Stable
with access to a Critter Condo will regularly relax in it and get the Cozy buff, a temporary +1 happiness bonus for 1 cycle.
A tame, ungroomed, cozy critter will have a total happiness of 0 and therefore use their base reproduction and metabolism. This allows you to ranch critters for their resource output without needing dupe labor to groom them, unless you also want the extra eggs.
In a full
Stable
with an active
Grooming Station
, the
Critter Condo
allows for an extra critter to be present as the happiness buff counteracts the debuff caused by overcrowding.
Wild critters will also use the Critter Condo, but this doesn't change their reproduction speed or their metabolism rate.
Trivia
Critter Condo has an overheat temperature, and when it overheats and autorepair is enabled, it generates a Repair errand, but it can still be used by critters properly when broken. This means the player needs to disable autorepair on Critter Condo when ranching
Molten Slicksters
, unless it is built using
Ceramic
.
