Mechanics
The canister emptier has an element filter, allowing the player to specify the types of canistered gases to be delivered to it. Gas canisters can be most easily obtained by creating them with a
Canister Filler
or by removing gases directly from a gas pipe via a duplicant possessing the
plumber
trait.
There is no maximum pressure limit to release the gas, which together with a
Liquid Airlock
, can be used to store large amount of gases in very small areas. However, this method requires manual duplicant labor.
The Emptier outputs at a rate of 250 g/s, so a 200 kg canister of gas will take 800 in-game seconds (1.25 cycles) to empty.  Duplicants will not deliver another canister until the current one is completely discharged.
