# Heavi-Watt Conductive Joint Plate

Heavi-Watt Conductive Joint Plate
Carries more Wattage than a regular Heavi-Watt Joint Plate without overloading.
Allows Heavi-Watt Conductive Wire to be run through wall and floor tile.
Joint plates can run Heavi-Watt Conductive Wires through walls without leaking gas or liquid.
