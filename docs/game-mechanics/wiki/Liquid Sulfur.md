Tips
Liquid Sulfur can easily be used to create natural tiles for pip planting, especially if you have access to a Liquid Sulfur Geyser. To create natural sulfur tiles, you must pour at least 400kg (this is 80% of 500kg which is the default mass of a solid sulfur tile) of Liquid Sulfur and then cool it to its freezing point. It will freeze into a solid tile and can be used by pips for planting.
