Uses
It has an exceptionally high thermal conductivity among all the
gases
, so it is ideal to be used as a heat transfer medium at high temperature ranges, especially when solid debris is involved in heat transfer. Other fluids that can be used for this purpose include
Molten Steel
,
Molten Aluminum
,
Molten Copper
and
Molten Lead
, which are all liquids. Mercury Gas is space-filling due to its gaseous nature, which makes it more suitable for some cases than liquids.
In
Metal Volcano
tamers, having a layer of Mercury at the bottom of a steam room can make debris cool down rapidly, due to the heat transfer between metal debris and cells being
bottlenecked
by the Thermal Conductivity of the cell, and both Liquid and Gas Mercury provide a thermal conductivity much higher than
Steam
.
