# Jet Suit Checkpoint

Jet Suit Checkpoint
Marks a threshold where Duplicants must change into or out of Jet Suits.
Must be built next to a Jet Suit Dock.
Can be rotated before construction.
A checkpoint must have a jet suit dock built on the opposite side its arrow faces.

Jet Suit Checkpoint
works the same way as the
Atmo Suit Checkpoint
, except it is for
Jet Suit
instead.
Checkpoints
Checkpoints have a Clearance setting. When a Duplicants wearing a the correct suit returns, there may be no empty Docks to store the used Suit:
Always Permitted
(default): will allow the Duplicant to return anyways, dropping the suit. This can leave other docks without any suits if Duplicants prefer one specific entrance, but won't strand duplicants
Vacancy
: will prohibit the Duplicant, forcing them to use another empty checkpoint, or be stranded outside
Suits store all fluids expelled by the duplicant. These will be released at the Checkpoint tile (not the dock tile), so expect various fluids here (careful with any nearby liquid locks or vacuums)
Carbon Dioxide from breathing
Polluted Water if they make a mess while in the suit
Notes
When Duplicants change out of a worn Suit, the oxygen will drop on the ground as bottled oxygen
When a duplicant is already wearing an
Oxygen Mask
, they can pass a suit checkpoint without equipping a suit.
Bugs
Duplicants will unequip
Atmo Suits
at this checkpoint, dropping them on the ground.
When an area is accessible through both a Jet Suit Checkpoint and Atmo Suit Checkpoint, duplicants will occasionally pass the Atmo Suit Checkpoint without equipping the available suit.
