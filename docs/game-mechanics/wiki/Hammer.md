Hammer
In its default orientation, the hammer strikes the building to the left when it receives a Green Signal.
Each building has a unique sound when struck by hammer. The hammer does no damage when it strikes.

Hammer
is a cosmetic
automation
building that makes a sound according to the building to its left when it receives a Green Signal. Can be rotated in every direction so it hits the building above, below or to the right instead.
Trivia
If the hammer hits a
Gas Pipe
,
Liquid Pipe
or
Wire
, it makes different notes depending on the length of the pipe/wire struck.
This can be used to make music as the pipe/wire the hammer hits always makes a tuned note.
Bugs
If the Hammer is off-camera and it gets activated, the visual effect that happens when the hammer is hitting a building, sometimes stays. However you won't hear anything and it is fixed as soon as you hover your mouse over it.
