# Dig

Duplicants
digging.
Digging
is a type of
Errand
which involves removing material from the map and turning it into its component materials. Digging speed is determined by Duplicants'
Excavation
attribute, and how
hard
the object is (
Sand
is very soft with a hardness of 10, whereas
Abyssalite
is nearly impenetrable with a hardness of 200). Digging out blocks creates
vacuum
. Dug materials will fall on the ground as debris, which creates negative
decor
.
Duplicants may need certain
skills
in order to dig harder materials (
Granite
,
Abyssalite
, etc.); see
below
for details.
Any excavated material will lose 50% of its mass. This loss can be prevented by melting and re-freezing the material, but it takes more work.
Range
Digging uses
Construction Reach
duplicants can reach 4 tiles up, 3 tiles down, and 2 tiles sideways
Duplicants can dig in a rectangle 4 tiles up from the floor they are standing on and 2 out to the side. They can dig 3 tiles down.
Duplicants WILL dig straight below them and fall thru if thats the only possible place to dig a tile.
