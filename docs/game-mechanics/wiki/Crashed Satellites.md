Crashed Satellites
are naturally occurring buildings found in the
Irradiated Forest Asteroid
and asteroids with the
Crashed Satellites World Trait
. They emit a large amount of
radiation
which can be helpful for
Radbolt
collection or
Mutated Plant
farming, but this can also pose a health risk to duplicants.
They can be demolished and rummaged through for 4 to 10
Data Banks
along with one of the following:
3 additional Data Banks
3
Wort Seeds
3
Morbs
and 1
Atmo Suit
.
Radiation Emission
Crashed Satellites emit
2,400 rads/cycle (Extreme Hazard)
, originating at the bottom center tile and lose approximately 200 rads per cycle per tile outwards.
This is helpful at providing consistent
radiation
for easy
plant mutation
or to
Radbolt Generators
.
