Tips
Pacu
fish are a great source of egg shells for creating Lime. Their eggs have a lot of mass, and they reproduce very quickly.
Shine Bugs
reproduce quickly but their egg mass is less than other critters.
Pokeshell Molts
have the highest mass measured in Lime.
The most desirable source of Lime is a care package of 150 kg of it from the
Printing Pod
. Still, a bit of Lime has to be produced through the usual means first before the Printing Pod begins to offer it. Even so, this is a rare care package.
In the vanilla game, the easiest way to aqcuire Lime is to mine diamond from 2 nearest carbon asteroids which gives approximately 100kg of renewable diamond per cycle which can be further converted to 10000kg fossil or 500kg lime (with the
Ancient Specimen
story trait enabled). This production rate easily outnumbers any of the regular ranching that requires grooming and typically produces 2kg eggshell per 6 cycles. This feature is especially useful when trapped oil
World Trait
is present, as the oil biome spawns with much less diamond or even no diamond compared with the regular oil biome.
