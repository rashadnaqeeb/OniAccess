Usage
As a
Refined Metal
, Nickel can be used to build advanced
Power
systems, buildings, and
Automation
systems.
Exosuit Forge
can produce
Atmo Suit
with Nickel. 300 kg of Nickel + 2 unit
Reed Fiber
= 1 unit
Atmo Suit
.
Production
The
Metal Refinery
can produce Nickel from
its ore
in 1:1 ratio. 100 kg Nickel Ore = 100 kg Nickel. It requires Duplicant operation.
An alternative way to produce Nickel is
Smooth Hatches
herding. A Smooth Hatch roughly eats 100 kg Nickel Ore and excretes 75 kg Nickel.
Rock Crusher
can produce Nickel from Nickel Ore too. This is not recommended due to lower efficiency of 50%. 100 kg Nickel Ore = 50 kg Nickel + 50 kg
Sand
. It requires Duplicant operation.
