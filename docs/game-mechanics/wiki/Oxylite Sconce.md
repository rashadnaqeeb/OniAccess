140 kg

400 s

Storage Bin comparison
Being a formalized Oxygen Building, using the Oxylite Sconce instead of a Storage Bin provides a few advantages:
The errand type is
Life Support
.
Less duplicant labour: the errand won't trigger until
100 kg
out of the
240 kg
Oxylite remains.
More space control: the Oxylite Sconce can be placed aloft, and is only 1x1 tile wide.
Clearer visual remaining indicator: the stored Oxylite chunk gets smaller and smaller as it is depleted.
It gives +5 Decor (Radius 1 tile) instead of Storage Bin -10 Decor (Radius 2 tiles).
