Sources
Copper ore blocks only exist in the Temperate Biome, usually in moderately large nodes surrounded by vacuum or an atmosphere of
Carbon Dioxide
. Strips of copper ore blocks can also line cavities. To a lesser extent, isolated blocks can be found buried in natural blocks.
Copper ore can be found in geodes.
Some

Copper ore can also be recovered from

on

Digging
Like many other natural blocks, copper ore blocks can be dug out by
Duplicants
