The
Metallic Swampy Asteroid
is a small starting asteroid with a
Swampy Biome
start. It additionally generates the
Marshes
,
Metallics
, and
Magma
.
Map Layout
Water vents and geysers
do not generate in this asteroid, leaving only the initial generation of water in the Swamps and scattered ice on the surface to be the player's main source of water until they can import it from the
Frozen Forest Asteroid
linked via
Teleport
or nearby Asteroids.
Example Metallic Swampy Asteroid Layout
Strategy
The crust of the asteroid is covered in ice, polluted ice, and brine ice that's exposed to space. The ice is initially at
-40 °C ↔ -60 °C
/
-40 °F ↔ -76 °F
, but it's not insulated from the rest of the asteroid, and will eventually melt (after many cycles) and be mostly lost to space. If the
Metallic Swampy Asteroid
isn't the starting asteroid, this "countdown" only starts once the player first places a rocket in orbit around the asteroid. While the interior of the asteroid is filled with plenty of water, the many volcanoes can still unexpectedly overheat the asteroid. Mining the ice is subsequently a desirable quest when attempting to harness the volcanoes.
