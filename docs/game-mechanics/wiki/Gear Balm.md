Mechanic
Bionic Duplicants have a
Gear Oil
attribute whose value spans from 0 to 200 kg. By time, Gear Oil will slowly decrease. If a Bionic Duplicant's Gear Oil depletes, it suffers from the
Grinding Gears
debuff, which impedes its mobility until new lubrication is applied.
Bionic duplicant can apply
Gear Balm
manually on themself or use a
Lubrication Station
to replenish their Gear Oil.

Apothecary
for 80 kg of
Gunk
and 200 kg of
Water
bottled. It'll also drop a bottle of 200 kg of
Polluted Water
on the ground.
