The
Printerceptor
is a
Story Trait
that can generate in
asteroids
or
planetoids
, which is focused around the
building
of the same name. Once the story trait is completed, the Printerceptor is capable of intercepting and storing
Printing Pod
charges to print a
Seed
or
Critter Egg
of your choice.
Description
The Printerceptor story trait generates two points of interest: A small room containing the Printerceptor itself alongside inconsequential lab equipment. The room is partially surrounded by
Window Tiles
made of
Glass
. Elsewhere on the asteroid, a toilet can be found in a small room with other non-functional bathroom-themed buildings.
The Printerceptor is made out of
800 kg
Copper
and can store up to 500
Data Banks
. A slider can be used to adjust the capacity the Printerceptor will hold.
Activation
When discovered, the Printerceptor must be initially activated by a Duplicant which notes that it is locked and requires an access code. Finding and Unclogging the toilet reveals an access code, which unlocks the final activation of the Printerceptor.
Choose a Blueprint
Printing Pod
charges can be intercepted and stored at the Printerceptor. Storing three Printing Pod charges unlocks the blueprint button. 3 printing pod charges can be consumed to print
Seeds
and
Critter Eggs
of your choosing at the cost of
Data Banks
. A blueprint initially costs 25 data banks. Each successive blueprint of the same object increases its cost by 25 data banks, up to a maximum of 275 data banks.
All seeds/critter eggs are available and do not require discovery of the items.
It is worth mentioning that the following
are
available:
Beetiny

,

, and

)
Sporechid Seed
(not available as a
Care Package
)
Saturn Critter Trap
(not available as a

)

(not available as a
Care Package
)
The following are not available:
Deprecated game content (such as the Hydrocactus Seed, which nonetheless is speciously available within the in-game farm selection and various database entries).
DLC Content without the appropriate DLC unlocked (i.e.
Lumb Egg
without the
Prehistoric Planet Pack).
Completion
After storing three
Printing Pod
charges and choosing a blueprint for the first time, a
