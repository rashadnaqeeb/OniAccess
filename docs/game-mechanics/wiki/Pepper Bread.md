Pepper Bread
is a food item made from 10
Sleet Wheat Grain
and 1
Pincha Peppernut
at the
Gas Range
. It has a
Food Quality
of Superb (+5) and provides 4,000 kcal.
Calorie-wise Pepper Bread is mostly equivalent to
Frost Buns
, albeit slightly more expensive in its resource cost. Compared to the
Frost Burger
, it may be preferable to cook Sleet Wheat into Pepper Bread, since they both provide the same morale bonus to duplicants without the Gourmet trait and Pepper Bread does not carry a penalty to the
Athletics
skill.
Feeding your Duplicants Pepper Bread indefinitely will require 2.5 domestic Sleet Wheat plants (or 10 wild plants) and 0.5 domestic Pincha Pepper Plants (or 2 wild plants) per duplicant.
There are no recipes using Pepper Bread as an ingredient to create even more sophisticated food.
For long-time storage it can be kept in
sterile
environments while being deep-frozen (item or surrounding gas below
-18 °C
/
-0.4 °F
) or be
dehydrated
.
