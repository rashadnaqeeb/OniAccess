# Micronutrient Fertilizer

Micronutrient Fertilizer
Specialized
Fertilizer
mixed by a Duplicant with the Crop Tending Skill.
Increases the Growth Rate of one
Plant
.
Effects
Applies Farmer's Touch effect
Cost
Fertilizer
: 5 kg
Micronutrient Fertilizer
is used to apply the
Farmer's Touch Effect
to a
Plant
, which will double its growth rate for 1
Cycle
. It is produced in a
Farm Station
by
Duplicant
with the Crop Tending
Skill
from 5 kg of
Fertilizer
. It can then be applied to a Plant with a
Farming
or
Supplying
Errand
. This too requires the Crop Tending Skill.
It cannot be used to speed up the growth of
Mealwood
.
Tips
One constantly running
Fertilizer Synthesizer
produces 72 kg of Fertilizer per cycle, which is enough to keep 14.4 Plants fertilized. In other words, five Fertilizer Synthesizers can support 72 Plants.
Bugs
When using Micronutrient Fertilizer on Plants, Duplicants train their
Machinery
attribute.
This is due to the game seeing the fertilization as a Tinkering errand.
Trivia
Although made from Fertilizer, the primary
Element
of Micronutrient Fertilizer is
Genetic Ooze
.
