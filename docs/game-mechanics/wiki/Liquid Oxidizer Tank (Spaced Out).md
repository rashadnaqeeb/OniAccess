The
Liquid Oxidiser Tank
holds 450 kg of
Liquid Oxygen
, which is the most efficient oxidizer.
Due to its outstanding efficiency compared to other oxidizers, it is best used when you need to keep modules and burden at a minimum.
Its oxidizer to fuel ratio is 1:4, meaning you can have 1800 kg of fuel with a single full tank.
