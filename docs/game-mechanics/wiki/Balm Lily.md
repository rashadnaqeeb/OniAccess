The regular
Drecko

ranching
them, since the plants are maintenance free once the proper atmosphere is set up. However, Glossy Dreckos cannot eat Lilies.

Database
Balm Lily
Medicinal Herb
The Balm Lily naturally contains high vitamin concentrations and produces acids similar in molecular makeup to acetylsalicylic acid (commonly known as aspirin).
As a result, the plant is ideal both for boosting immune systems and treating a variety of common maladies such as pain and fever.
