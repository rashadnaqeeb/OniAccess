Usage
Molten Glass quickly cools down into
Glass
due to the low thermal capacity, low mass of 25 kg per production cycle and decent thermal conductivity.
Due to it's high availability and renewability (via melting
Sand
), and the large difference of SHC between Liquid Glass and
Rock Gas
, melting and then vaporizing glass can generate immense amounts of power.
Production
Glass Forge
can produce Molten Glass from
Sand
. 100 kg Sand = 25 kg Molten Glass.
Due to the very limited rate and heat capacity at which Molten Glass can be produced, its overall heat output is lower than one might expect. Still, the molten glass is initially extremely hot and can potentially melt smaller, low-mass buildings like
Automation Wires
.
