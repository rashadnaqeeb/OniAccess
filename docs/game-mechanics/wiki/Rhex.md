Cannibalistic Starvation Ranching
When a Rhex eats the Tough Meat dropped by a dead Rhex, it gains 10 cycles of Just Ate, the scale growth allows shearing up to 10 feathers, and during this time, if happy, it gains 88% egg progress, which combined with egg progress from its starting calories is easily enough to ensure one egg will be laid per lifetime. Starvation ranching of Rhexes mostly just produces feather, egg shell and brine ice. With careful allocation of the tough meat, not just allowing one starving Rhex to eat it all, it is possible to produce more than one egg per lifetime, actually increasing the population over time.
Shearing
When a
Rhex
has the Just Ate status, it will grow feathers at a rate of 25% per cycle, allowing a well fed, satisfied, tame
Rhex
to be sheared for 4
Feather Fiber

Feather Fiber
every cycle. The duration of Just Ate is directly proportional to how many calories it consumed in that sitting. A well-fed, satisfied Rhex will always be under the effects of "Just Ate", while a well-fed glum Rhex will be under the effects of "Just Ate" for 1 cycle out of 5. When a baby
Rhex

Database
Rhex
Carnivorous Critter
For all their bluster, Rhexes are emotionally sensitive creatures that thrive on positive reinforcement, especially words of affirmation.
These sightless apex predators have exceptionally acidic gastric juices that kill most known bacteria and toxins, enabling them to safely digest their prey. This protective mechanism renders them susceptible to chronic ulcers if food is not readily available.
The Rhex's tailfeather can be shorn to harvest fibers for textile production. Rhexes don't mind this - some even enjoy it.
