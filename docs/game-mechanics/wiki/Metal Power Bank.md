Notes
When reaching 0 kJ, a Metal Power Bank will disappear. This means the 200kg of Ore used to create it is lost, which could be a big drain of resources as these Ores are used in many other crafts. This is equivalent to 0.6 kJ of energy per kg of Metal Ore.
It takes 60 seconds for a dupe with 0 machinery to craft the power bank.
Intentionally exploding power banks in water can be an effective means early on to create
Steam
for a
Steam Engine
.
