Blank Canvas
is a
decorative
building in the
Furniture category
that provides a
Decor
bonus to its surroundings and can
be painted on
to provide a further bonus. The quality of the painting influences this bonus with higher quality paintings from highly skilled artistic
Duplicants
producing the best
Decor
bonuses.
There is 1 kind of crude, 1 kind of quaint and 15 kinds of masterpiece paintings; 9 of the masterpieces are locked behind
Blueprints
. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +5 for Crude, +10 for Quaint, +15 for Masterpiece.
