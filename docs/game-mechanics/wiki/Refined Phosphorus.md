Refined Phosphorus
is a
Consumable Ore
. It does not spawn as tiles in the world, but rather must be processed from
Phosphorite
.
Usage
Azure Bugs
and
Abyss Bugs
can eat Refined Phosphorus.
The
Geotuner
uses Refined Phosphorus to boost both the output and temperature of metal volcanoes.
Liquid Phosphorus can form 7.5kg droplets before flowing into adjacent tiles. This has applications in high-temperature 1-tile liquid locks, as a Phosphorus droplet has ~10x the thermal capacity of a ~300g Petroleum or Crude-Oil droplet, reducing the risk of breaking the lock through vaporization.
The relatively small temperature window (
41 °C ↔ 283 °C
/
105.8 °F ↔ 541.4 °F
) limits this usefulness, and a 37.5kg
Naphtha
droplet would have a thermal capacity ~15x greater than the Phosphorus, and at a much wider liquid temperature range, though the production of Phosphorus does not require Plastic.
A tile of more than 160kg of Liquid Phosphorus will freeze into a natural tile of Refined Phosphorus rather than debris. This behavior can be used for building
pip wild-farms
, though only in areas guaranteed to not exceed
47 °C
/
116.6 °F
.
Production
Melting

at ~
247 °C

Care must be taken when freezing the Liquid Phosphorus as tiles of more than 160kg will freeze into natural tiles rather than debris, necessitating either mining them, thus losing half their mass, or re-melting.
