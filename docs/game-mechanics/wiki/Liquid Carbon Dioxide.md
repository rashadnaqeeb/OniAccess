Liquid Carbon Dioxide
is the liquid state of
Carbon Dioxide
. It is converted from Carbon Dioxide by cooling it down to
-51.15 °C
/
-60.07 °F
(condensation point - 3 °C).
Production
Carbon Dioxide Geysers
produce Liquid Carbon Dioxide at various rate. In normal game, the Liquid Carbon Dioxide quickly evaporates into Gas state if not pumped.
Tips
Liquid Carbon Dioxide can be pumped like other Liquids, but because of its liquid temperature range of just 8 degrees, it should only be moved through
Insulated Liquid Pipe
, otherwise you'll risk pipe damage.
Trivia
Under regular atmospheric conditions, real-life
Carbon Dioxide
actually bypasses the liquid phase entirely. Only above a pressure of 518 kPa, about five times standard atmospheric pressure, does liquid Carbon Dioxide actually form. However, ONI does not simulate the influence of pressure on phase transitions, and includes liquid Carbon Dioxide.
