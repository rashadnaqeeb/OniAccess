The
Alveo Vera
is a cold-loving plant that consumes
Carbon Dioxide
and
Ice
to produce
Oxylite
, which offgasses into breathable
Oxygen
after harvesting. Alveo Veras can be found growing naturally within the
Ice Cave Biome
.
Seed
Alveo Vera Seed
can be obtained by harvesting and uprooting
Alveo Vera
, or by digging up a
Buried Object
within the
Ice Cave Biome
. It grows into Alveo Vera when planted.
Usage
Due to their availability and the abundance of Ice in the starting
Ice Cave Biome
, Alveo Vera plants are often the first source of sustainable Oxygen that a player will make use of when starting on the
Ceres Asteroid
or its Spaced Out variants.
Domestic & Wild Growth
Domestic
Domestic
Alveo Veras inhale 1200g/cycle (2g/s) of
Carbon Dioxide
and consume 20kg/cycle (~33.33g/s) of Ice as fertilizer. They take 2 cycles to grow, dropping 36kg of Oxylite when harvested, resulting in a maximum theoretical output of up to 18kg/cycle (30g/s) of Oxylite per domestic plant (assuming no buffs).
Wild
Wild
Alveo Veras inhale 300g/cycle (0.5g/s) of Carbon Dioxide. They take 8 cycles to grow, dropping 36kg of Oxylite when harvested, resulting in a maximum output of 4.5kg/cycle (7.5g/s) of Oxylite per wild plant (assuming no buffs).

Note that since mature Alveo Veras temporarily stop producing Oxylite until they are harvested, the maximum amount of Oxygen that they can produce will always be less than the theoretical maximum during gameplay.
As shown in the graph above,
at least ~3.33 domestic (or ~13.33 wild) Alveo Veras are required to create enough Oxygen to sustain one Duplicant
. However, each domestic Alveo Vera consumes the same amount of Carbon Dioxide exhaled by one Duplicant (2 g/s). Therefore, assuming all of the Carbon Dioxide being exhaled by Duplicants is being fed back to Alveo Veras, the remaining plants will need to have additional Carbon Dioxide supplied.
This means that regardless of whether domestic or wild plants are used, at least 4.67 g/s of additional Carbon Dioxide will have to be created per Duplicant.
The ratio of Oxygen Produced to Carbon Dioxide Consumed can be substantially increased by applying growth speed buffs via Farmer's Touch/Grubgrub Rub/plant mutations.
Water to Oxygen Economy
Because one domestic Alveo Vera converts 33.33g/s of Ice into 30g/s of Oxylite, the regular domestic Alveo Vera's maximum theoretical conversion rate of
Water
to Oxygen is 90%.
The conversion rate can be substantially increased (even above 100%) by applying growth speed buffs via Farmer's Touch/Grubgrub Rub/plant mutations.

Database
Alveo Vera
Plant
The Alveo Vera's fleshy stems are dotted with small apertures featuring bidirectional valves through which
Carbon Dioxide
is absorbed and sticky oxygenated waste is secreted.
The buildup from this respiration cycle crystallizes into
Oxylite
ore.
Horticulturists have long been curious about the protective epithelium that prevents the
Oxylite
ore from sublimating while on the plant. Unfortunately, it is too fragile to survive handling, and has thus far proven impossible to study.
