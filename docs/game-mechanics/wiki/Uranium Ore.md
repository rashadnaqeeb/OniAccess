Uranium Ore
is a
Metal Ore
found in the
Radioactive Biome
.
It melts into
Liquid Uranium
, which, in turn, solidifies to
Depleted Uranium
.
Usage
The
Radiation Lamp
and
Manual Radbolt Generator
require Uranium Ore in order to function.
Uranium Ore can be mined for half the value of the original tile. This mined ore can then be enriched at 10% the value using the
Uranium Centrifuge
.
Uranium Ore can be mined naturally using a
Beeta Hive
for 90% of the value of the tile over time.

Space POIs

In Radioactive Asteroid Fields or Radioactive Gas Clouds at a rate of 9.1 – 36.4 kg per cycle.
