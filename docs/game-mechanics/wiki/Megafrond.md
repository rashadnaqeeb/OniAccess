-18 °C
/
-0.4 °F

Database
Megafrond
Cultivable Plant
Megafrondia Byronalis
, commonly known as "Megafrond," is a gigantic plant that dwarfs its surroundings.
Its size is not the only daunting factor in its cultivation: the Megafrond's gigantism is possible thanks to its singular adaptation to cold temperatures and a caustic gas environment that few other living things (including farmers) enjoy.
These challenges are considered a fair price for bragging rights and a useful grain harvest.
