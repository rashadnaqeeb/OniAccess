Landscape Canvas
is a
decorative
building in the
Furniture category
that provides a
Decor
bonus to its surroundings and can
be painted on
to provide extra.
There is 1 kind of crude, 1 kind of quaint and 11 kinds of masterpiece paintings; 7 of the masterpieces are locked behind
Blueprints
. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +5 for Crude, +10 for Quaint, +15 for Masterpiece.
