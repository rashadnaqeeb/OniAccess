Skewed Asteroid
A tilted world, at once strange and...strangely familiar. Its singular terrain is consistent across all universes.
This resistance to the fluidity of temporal shifts has the cosmos buzzing, as individual colonies compare notes on successes, failures and the bizarre redistribution of resources.
Released for Klei Fest 2023.
