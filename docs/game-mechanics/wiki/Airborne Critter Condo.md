# Airborne Critter Condo

The
Airborne Critter Condo
gives flying critters a way to relax to improve their happiness.
Airborne Critter Condo
A hanging respite area for adult Pufts, Gassy Moos and Shine Bugs.
Triggers natural nesting instincts and improves critter's moods.

Airborne Critter Condo

A tame, ungroomed, cozy critter will have a total happiness of 0 and therefore use their base reproduction and metabolism. This allows you to ranch Pufts for their resource output without needing dupe labor to groom them, unless you also want the extra eggs. Gassy Moo's Brackene Supply is unaffected by their mood.
In a full
Stable
with an active
Grooming Station
, the
Airborne Critter Condo
allows for an extra critter to be present as the happiness buff counteracts the debuff caused by overcrowding.
Wild critters will also use the
Airborne Critter Condo
, but this doesn't change their reproduction speed or their metabolism rate.
