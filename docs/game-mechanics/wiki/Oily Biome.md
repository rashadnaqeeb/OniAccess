# Oily Biome

The
Oily Biome
is a hot
Biome
with large amounts of
Crude Oil
,
Lead
, and
Iron Ore
. It contains the only renewable sources of Crude Oil. Its also highly dangerous; as the temperature is hot enough to scald
Duplicants
and
Sporechids
generate
Zombie Spores
which can cripple a dupe for several cycles.
Strategy
With how dangerous the
Oily Biome
is, exploration is still greatly rewarding.
Atmo Suits
are highly recommended as they protect against both the heat and the spores. Either prioritizing the removal of
Sporechids
or avoiding them completely is wise when starting to set up here.
Oil Reservoirs
are the primary resource here; as they allow for continuous
Crude Oil
generation at the cost of
Water
.
Slicksters
are capable of the same thing, instead costing
Carbon Dioxide
, but the throughput of these critters is so low its often not worth doing.
Lead
is only found here and in extraordinary amounts. Due to the
-20 °C
/
-36 °F
modifier to overheat temperature, it's not useful in most buildings; but it is great for use in
Conductive Wires
and
Automation Buildings
.

Oily Biome
Viscous pools of liquid
Crude Oil

Carbon Dioxide
-rich environment of the Oil Biome.
Though

Slicksters
. These critters can be domesticated as a renewable source of
Crude Oil
.
Diamond

equipped
.

and
Petroleum
