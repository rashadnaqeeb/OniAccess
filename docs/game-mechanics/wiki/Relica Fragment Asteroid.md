When enabled, the Relica Fragment asteroid will replace one of five outer asteroids that are otherwise guaranteed across most cluster configurations: Marshy, Moo, Water, Tundra, and Regolith. The Relica Fragment Asteroid attempts to place a
Polluted Water Vent
,
Cool Chlorine Gas Vent
, and 4 generic geysers alongside the Relica-related biomes
Garden Biome
,
Feather Biome
, and
Wetlands Biome
.
The Relica Fragment is a fixed size of 64x128, and may result in a substantial increase in the total amount of space the asteroid would otherwise take.
Map Layout Variations
The
Marshy Relica Fragment
does not generate Tungsten volcanoes in the Magma Biome, although
Experiment 52B
is still present.
The
Water Relica Fragment
does not generate
Lime
or its two water geysers, although
Graphite
is still present.
The
Tundra Relica Fragment
does not generate any Iron volcanoes, although the
Temporal Tear Opener
is still present.
The
Moo Relica Fragment
loses most of its Liquid
Chlorine
, although it still hosts
Gassy Moo
, Gassy Moo Meteor Showers, as well as a Chlorine Vent.
The
Regolith Relica Fragment
loses its Steam vents, although still has its deadly Regolith meteor showers.

Strategy
The Relica Fragment is a very forgiving asteroid, packed with useful resources like
Raw Mineral
and
Nickel Ore
.
Polluted Water
is usually abundant and renewable thanks to a
Polluted Water Vent
, and
Lumbs
,
Rhexes
, and
Jawbos
provide plenty of food to support early colonization.
