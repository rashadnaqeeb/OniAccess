# Jungle Biome

The
Jungle Biome
, also known as The
Caustic Biome
, is a hot
biome
with no source of breathable air. A
Cool Steam Vent
is often spawned here (depending on the asteroid).
Strategy
The
Jungle Biome
has no source of breathable air; meaning gas will have to be supplied from elsewhere. Annoyingly, this biome has both
Hydrogen
and
Chlorine
, which will float above and sink below any breathable air; respectively. This can make small tunnels particularly bad for airflow here; as gas flow can be completely blocked much easier.
The
Dreckos
are a very strong source of both
Reed Fiber
and
Plastic
; and, if paired with
Balm Lily
, can be ranched completely for free. The
Pincha Pepperplants
are primary used to produce higher-tier foods; notably
Pepper Bread
and
Spicy Tofu
.

Database
Jungle Biome
Initial investigations of the Jungle Biome reveal an ecosystem filled with unique flora but centered around
Liquid Chlorine
and
Hydrogen Gas
gas, toxic to Duplicants. When exploring here, it is worth setting up a good system.
The
Drecko
is a relatively benign critter which can be domesticated to aid in textile and food production.
The
Morb
's only function is to produce
Polluted Oxygen
which may be useful when establishing sustainable production loops with other critters.
Balm Lilies
would be useful to cultivate for the production of critical medical materials.
Pincha Pepperplants greatly improve the nutritional value and quality of a colony's food supply. Because of their unique relationship with gravity, the plants must be orientated upside-down for proper growing. This can be accomplished by using a
Farm Tile
.
Given the toxic gases I will have to build a proper Ventilation system to both protect my Duplicants and provide the optimum environments for the native plants and critters.
