Feather Biome
The chilly Feather biome is home to huge, flightless
Rhexes
that can be shorn for
Textile Production
.
Rhexes
are carnivorous critters who prey primarily on
Dartles
. While both critters can be harvested for
Food
, the
Rhexes'
meat is too tough to be eaten raw.
This biome's flora includes the massive
Megafrond
which enjoys a
Chlorine Gas
environment, the
Dew Dripper
which can be processed into
Brackene
, and the cooling
Wheezewort
.
Duplicants working and living in this environment will require protective
equipment
.
