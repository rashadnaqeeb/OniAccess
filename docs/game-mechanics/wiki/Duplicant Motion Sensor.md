Duplicant Motion Sensor
is an automation building that detects
Duplicants
in its range (two tiles to the right and left, and four tiles down).
Usage
Turn on the
Lamp
or
Ceiling Light
besides the operatable machines to provide +15% speed bonus when a Duplicant is operating the machine. Turn off the light to save power when no Duplicant is working.
Turn on the
Sun Lamp
only when Duplicants are using a
Beach Chair
can save a large amount of power.
Tips
Despite requiring a tile to be placed, the motion sensor will still work without one.
