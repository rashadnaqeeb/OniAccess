# Seakomb Leaf

This article is related to
The Prehistoric Planet Pack
content.
Seakomb Leaf
The leaf of a
Seakomb
.
It can be processed into
Phyto Oil
or used as an ingredient in
Allergy Medication
.
Type
Industrial Ingredient
Seakomb Leaf
is the product of
Seakombs
. It can be fed to
Pacus
, used in
Allergy Medication
or processed into
Phyto Oil
.
Production
Seakombs
produce 10 kg/cycle of Seakomb Leaves when fertilized with 10 kg/cycle of
Polluted Dirt
.
Usage
Seakomb Leaf can be fed to
Pacu
family. A Pacu eats 20 kg/cycle of Seakomb Leaves.
25 kg Seakomb Leaf and 75 kg Water can be used in a
Plant Pulverizer
to create 100 kg of
Phyto Oil
.
10 kg Seakomb Leaf and 1 kg of
Dirt
can be used in an
Apothecary
to create 10 units of
Allergy Medication
.
