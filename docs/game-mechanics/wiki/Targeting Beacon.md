Target Beacon
is a
building
that targets where
Interplanetary Payload
should land on the planetoid. The landing spread is 6 tiles (the beacon itself, 3 to the left, 2 to the right which can be visualized as TTTBTT where T is a tile and B is the beacon). The beacon consumes power even if no
Interplanetary Payload
on the way.
The Targeting Beacon is immune to meteor damage and thus can be safely placed above or on top of bunker doors. This is particularly useful for ensuring
Interplanetary Payloads
always land near a beacon.
