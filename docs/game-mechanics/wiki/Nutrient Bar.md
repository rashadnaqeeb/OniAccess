Nutrient Bar
A nourishing nutrient paste, sandwiched between thin wafer layers.

per 1
kg
800 kcal
Quality
Grisly (-1)

Never
Source
Printing Pod

Nutrient Bars
are
food
items. The
Ration Box
at the start of the game contains 20 kg (16000 kcal) of Nutrient Bars.
Nutrient Bars cannot be made by
Duplicants
, but the
Printing Pod
can also sometimes offer more Nutrient Bars as a care package. 1 kg of Nutrient Bars also can be found in
Vending Machines
by rummaging inside it.
Nutrient Bars never spoil.
Nutrient Bars can only be consumed directly, there are no recipes using them as an ingredient.
Like other foods that can't expire, Nutrient Bars cannot be
composted
.
Trivia
The Nutrient Bar used to be called 'Field Ration'.
