The
Folia Asteroid
is a small starting asteroid with a
Forest Biome
start. The starting area is surrounded by
Barren
,
Rust
,
Tundra
,
Jungle
and
Wasteland
Biomes with the
Magma Biome
at the bottom.

Strategy
Rapid exploration is needed to find metals early on.
There are several guaranteed sources of renewable water.
