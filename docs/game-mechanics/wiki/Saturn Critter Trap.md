Saturn Critter Trap
Carnivorous Plant
The Saturn Critter Trap plant is a carnivorous plant that lays in wait for unsuspecting critters to happen by, then traps them in its mouth for consumption.
The Saturn Trap Plant's predatory mechanism is reflective of the harsh radioactive habitat it resides in.
Once trapped in the deadly maw of the plant, creatures are gently asphyxiated then digested through powerful acidic enzymes which coat the inner sides of the Saturn Trap Plant's leaves.
Trivia
Saturn Critter Trap when mature requires duplicant interaction to harvest, if you are farming for the hydrogen output, it is better to wild plant the Saturn Critter Trap as it takes longer to grow before requiring duplicant interaction while producing the same amount of hydrogen per cycle.
Once planted,
Saturn Critter Traps
are effectively immortal. They cannot drown or die to extreme temperatures. The only way to remove them is to uproot them manually or remove the foundation they're planted on.
Saturn Critter Traps do not constantly check for its foundation like other plants. Unusual world generation circumstances may cause a Saturn Critter Trap to float without any foundation.
