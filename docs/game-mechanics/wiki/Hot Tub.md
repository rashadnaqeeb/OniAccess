Hot Tub
is used during
downtime
to improve a duplicants'
morale
. A dupe will spend 90 seconds (~4
schedule
blocks) in the hot tub. Up to 4 dupes are able to use the Hot Tub at the same time. Despite being a recreational building, it does not need to be in a recreational room.
Hot tub consumes
bleach stone
and cycles water, expelling it when the temperature drops below
37.7 °C
/
99.86 °F
. Any other
liquids
will damage the building and spill out.
Tips
The Hot Tub requires precise water temperature input and produces a large amount of heat (5KTDU/s).
Water must be kept heated (at least
37 °C
/
98.6 °F
) going into the Hot Tub but the surroundings must be cooled as the unit will not function if above
37.7 °C
/
99.86 °F
.
Steel raises the overheat of the Hot Tub to
237.7 °C
/
459.86 °F
making it able to handle much hotter water. However while this prevents overheat damage, Duplicants will still not be allowed to use it if it is hotter than
37.7 °C
/
99.86 °F
so this is of dubious value.
Heat exchange between the water in the hot tub and the Hot Tub is magical in nature and even defies thermodynamics: the water will keep cooling down to
37.7 °C
/
99.86 °F
even if the Hot Tub and environment is hotter than the water (functionally it is similar to the Refrigerator, it actively cools its contents). As such you don't have to be concerned low ambient temperatures increasing heat loss from the water.
