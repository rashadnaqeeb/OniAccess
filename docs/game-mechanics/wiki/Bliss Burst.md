Bliss Burst
Decorative Plant
The Bliss Burst is a succulent in the genus Haworthia and is a hardy plant well-suited for beginner gardeners.
They require little in the way of upkeep, to the point that the most common cause of death for Bliss Bursts is overwatering from over-eager carers.
