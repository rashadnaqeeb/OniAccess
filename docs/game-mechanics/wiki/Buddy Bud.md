Buddy Bud
is a flower with similar characteristics to the
Bluff Briar
but has higher temperature range. They can be found in
Swamp Biome
.
Buddy Buds produce the
Floral Scents
"germ" which give a temporary -5% Stress/cycle, but triggers Allergic Reaction to Dupes with the Allergies trait.
Tips
Because two types of germs cannot occupy the same space at once, a strategically placed Buddy Bud can stop
Slimelung
from moving though an area. Place it next to
Oxygen Diffusers
and
Algae Terrariums
in case your Duplicants bring contaminated algae into your base.
It can also be used to block
Zombie Spores
, but Buddy Buds rapidly wilt in the heat of the Oil Biome, and only work with CO2 contamination and cannot prevent
Crude Oil
from being polluted.
