Tungsten
is a
Refined Metal
obtained by melting or refining
Wolframite
. It has the highest melting point of any Refined Metal and the third-highest of any buildable material (after
Insulite
and
Diamond
).
Usage
The main usage of Tungsten is for
Thermium
production in
Molecular Forge
. 5 kg
Niobium
+ 95 kg Tungsten = 100 kg Thermium.
Tungsten may also be used for any other task that needs
Refined Metal
. This is generally not recommended, as
Thermium
is superior in all regards except melting point - Tungsten provides the same bonus to overheat temperature as
Copper
or
Gold
, but without a
Decor
bonus. However, when the building needs an extremely high melting point but does not have an overheat temperature, it is recommended to use Tungsten. Notable cases include:
Power and automation wires exposed to
Rocket
exhaust.
Rocket loaders and unloaders in
Spaced Out!
.
Conduction Panels
servicing machines near rocket launch sites.
Radiant Liquid Pipes
meant to handle extremely high temperatures (e.g.
Liquid Steel
systems).
Tempshift Plates
and
Metal Tiles
can also be made of tungsten - however,
Diamond
(with
Window Tiles
replacing metal tiles) offers both a higher melting point and greater thermal conductivity, which can serve as an alternative to Tungsten.
Tungsten can be used instead of
Raw Mineral
for
Liquid Pipes
and
Insulated Liquid Pipes
, though its relatively high thermal conductivity makes it a poor choice for the latter. Tungsten liquid pipes are inferior to almost every possible
Radiant Liquid Pipe
(except
Depleted Uranium
ones) and use twice as much material, but can be a short-term option if
Smelting
is researched before
Liquid Tuning
.
Production
Metal Refinery
can produce Tungsten from
Wolframite
with 100% efficiency. 100 kg Wolframite = 100 kg Tungsten.
Smooth Hatch
can eat Wolframite and excretes Tungsten. It is around 75% efficiency.
Rock Crusher
can produce Tungsten from
Wolframite
with 50% efficiency. 100 kg Wolframite = 50 kg Tungsten + 50 kg
Sand
.
Abyssalite
, can be melted at
3421.85 °C
/
6191.33 °F
into
Liquid Tungsten
, cooling this down will produce Tungsten. Though this requires an exceptionally high temperature.
Tungsten Volcanoes
can be found on the
Marshy Asteroid
, which can provide continuous tungsten production (on the same planet as
Experiment 52B
).
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Glimmering Asteroid Fields at a rate of 60 – 180 kg per cycle, as a molten liquid.
