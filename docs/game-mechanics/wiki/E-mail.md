# E-mail

E-mail
is a tab that can be found in the in-game database. It can only be accessed once you have found one of the files by inspecting certain buildings in the
ruins
.
The E-mails that you can find are those sent between employees of the Gravitas Facility. Their only purpose is to tell the lore of the game, and have no impact on actual gameplay as of the current version (Q1-303707).
In the E-mails you can find out the full names of some of the duplicants, their interpersonal relationships, and details about their jobs and day to day activity in the Gravitas Facility.
