Idylla Seed
The
Seed
of an
Idylla Flower
.
Digging up Buried Objects may uncover an Idylla Seed.
Type
Seed
Idylla Seeds
are the seeds of
Idylla Flowers
. They can be planted to grow new Idylla Flowers.
Production
Naturally-occuring Idylla Flowers that appear in the
Ice Cave Biome
drop Idylla Seeds after being uprooted.
Idylla Seeds can sometimes be revealed by digging up
Buried Objects
in the Ice Cave Biome.

: Introduced.
