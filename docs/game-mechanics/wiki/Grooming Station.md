Grooming Station
is the primary structure necessary to create a
Stable
, the
room
helpful for
maintaining
and breeding
critters
in your colony.
Used by
duplicants
with the Critter Ranching
skill
, Grooming is the means for taming most wild critters. A wild Critter kept groomed for two days in a row will tame them, to which they, and their offspring, will remain tamed. Grooming tame Critters grants +5 Happiness; under
satisfactory circumstances
, they can lay
Eggs
at an increased rate.
Usage
Critters placed in a Stable will be automatically groomed by Duplicants with the
Critter Ranching skill
. A Duplicant will groom the Critter for 12 seconds.
Light
does not affect operation time.
Tips
Critters groomed at the grooming station increases their
happiness
by 5 for 1 cycle, which can substantially increase their reproduction rate.
Gassy Moos require 1 additional cell unobstructed by tiles to the right of the Grooming Station in order for them to be groomed.
