Wood Tiles
are tiles built out of
Wood
. They can be used as walls and floors.
On the starting asteroids added by The Frosty Planet Pack, it can be unlocked thanks to the
Research Portal
.
Usage
Being made out of
Wood
, Wood Tiles have an inherently low
Thermal Conductivity
, which makes them somewhat useful for reducing thermal transfer through walls and floors. This characteristic makes Wood Tiles especially useful when starting on the freezing
Ceres Asteroid
, as the player will likely unlock Wood Tiles via the
Research Portal
before eventually unlocking
Insulated Tiles
.
Due to their exceptional
Decor
value, Wood Tiles are useful for increasing
Morale
.
