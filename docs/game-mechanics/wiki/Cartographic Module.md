The
Cartographic Module
allows for travel into unexplored hexes of the
Starmap
.
It will mark hexes that contain an asteroid or POI with a "?" and that are within a range of 2 hexes from the rocket (but not reveal them, unless directly adjacent).
Unlike a
Telescope
in the interior of the rocket, it cannot reveal any hexes beyond the hex the rocket resides in. Revealing hexes through the Cartographic Module does not drop any
Data Banks
.
