# Swampy Biome

This article is related to
Spaced Out
content.
The
Swampy Biome
one of the possible starting
Biomes
. Despite the heavy presence of polluted materials there is a complete lack of germs, unlike the
Marsh Biome
. While there is a lack of fresh
Water
; using a
Water Sieve
can convert the copious amounts of
Polluted Water
into it.
Pacu
and
Plug Slugs
are also found here.
The
Swampy Biome
actually generates the 2nd most amount of water of the five starter biomes, behind the
Ice Cave Biome
with its construction being primarily
Ice
and
Snow
.
Strategy
With the large amounts of
Polluted Mud
,
Polluted Dirt
, and
Polluted Water
, breathable air will not be a problem. All of these materials emit
Polluted Oxygen
so while
Duplicants
will suffer from
Yucky Lungs
(including the increased oxygen consumption); having to build air systems is something that can be delayed for a considerable amount of time.
Bog Buckets
are this biomes starter plant and it is not resource efficient, being the thirstiest plant that produces food. Thankfully feeding it is not an issue early on thanks to the large abundance of
Polluted Water
, but moving away from these quickly can help conserve resources.
Cobalt Ore
itself is not particularly interesting as a metal ore, but refined
Cobalt
has the third highest thermal conductivity of all the
Refined Metals
other than
Aluminum
,
Thermium
and
Iridium
.

Swampy Biome
With its abundance of
Polluted Oxygen
and lack of clean
Water
the Swampy Biome presents some challenges for a budding colony. But, with a little hard work, it can also turn into a great starting biome with some valuable resources.
Swamp Chard
can provide adequate nutrients for my Duplicants while they establish farms, but it cannot be planted or propogated.
Bog Buckets
, however, provide a sweet source of nutrients that are fairly easy to farm using
Polluted Water
and require no extra light to grow.
Fortunately
Polluted Water
is abundant in this biome and will require a
Water Sieve
to turn into something my Duplicants can drink. Additionally my Duplicants can use a
Sludge Press
to filter clean water from
Mud
(NOTE:
Polluted Mud
, however, does not make clean water).
Meanwhile, rudimentary power can be gained from the energy producing
Plug Slugs
that inhabit this biome.
Shiny
Cobalt Ore
can be found here, providing a adequate source of metal.
While much of this biome is dangerous for a new colony with some deliberate research and careful planning my Duplicants can not only survive but thrive here.
