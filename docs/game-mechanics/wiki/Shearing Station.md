Shearing Station
is used to shear specific
Critters
. When a wild or tame critter's scales are fully grown, a Rancher will call the Critter over to the station for processing, similar to how grooming works at a
Grooming Station
. Additional Critters that are eligible for operation will queue up to the Shearing Station when currently in use. The Shearing Station counts towards the requirements for a
Stable
and won't function if placed in a room that doesn't meet a Stable's requirements. It has an operation time of 12 seconds.
Light
does not affect operation time. The sheared product will have the same temperature as the Critter.
Critters do not grow scales while Miserable. Despite the flavor text implying a positive effect from being shorn, shearing provides no additional benefit beyond producing the resource.
