Display Shelf
is a decorative
Building
that can be configured to display up to 1 kg or 1 unit of various
resources
. A Display Shelf with nothing on it will grant +5
Decor
with a radius of 1 tile. A Display Shelf with an item on it gains additional Decor depending on the item being displayed:
Gains double the item's Decor value, or +5 Decor, whichever is greater.
Gains 2 plus the item's Decor radius.
Material that provides decor boosts like
Wood
also applies to the additional Decor. A display shelf does not require foundation to be built, but does require a back wall like
Drywall
beneath it.
Display shelves can showcase the same items as both a
Sweepy
and
Pedestal
. See
Displayables
to view the exhaustive list of objects that can be displayed on a
Display Shelf
.
Comparison to other Decor Buildings
Display shelves provide some of the highest
Decor
of any Decor Building, especially when displaying high-rarity artifacts.
A
Wood
display shelf displaying a Rarity 5 artifact (for example,
Moonmoonmoon
) provides 247.5 decor in a 10-tile radius while occupying only 1 tile.
While ornaments are finite, the
Regal Bammoth Crest
is a renewable alternative that still provides respectable decor (73.5 decor in a 6-tile radius).

Bugs
A missing back wall only prevents the display shelf from being supplied; it does not prevent an existing display shelf from functioning. In addition, a back wall need not be constructed - only planned. The drywall restriction can be bypassed by deconstructing the drywall after an item is displayed, or by canceling drywall construction plans once the item is displayed.
