Moo Biome
is a cold
Biome
that contains large amounts of
Chlorine
and
Bleach Stone
.
Gas Grass
and
Gassy Moos
can also be found here, and are exclusive to this biome. It can only be found on the
Moo Asteroid
.
Strategy
The
Moo Biome
is extremely cold; so cold that it is one of the few naturally occurring places where
Duplicants
can suffer Frostbite. This, along with the lack of any breathable air or way of producing it, makes
Atmo Suits
essentially required to explore and exploit this biome.

Moo Biome
The Moo Biome is the natural habitat of the charismatic
Gassy Moo
, a great source of
Natural Gas
.
In addition to
Natural Gas
, the highly toxic
Chlorine
is also present in gas form. In fact, Chlorine is present here in
gas
,
liquid
, and
solid
states, largely due to the presence of
Bleach Stone
.
Igneous Rock
and its denser form,
Granite
, provide some useful construction materials, but the real star of this biome are the
Gassy Moos
who consume
Gas Grass
and excrete
Natural Gas
. While Gassy Moos cannot be bred domestically, Gassy Mooteors regularly fall from space onto this biome, making it the best way to find a reliable source of these elusive creatures.
With no breathable
Oxygen
in this biome my Duplicants will need to be properly prepared before they venture too far into its depths.
