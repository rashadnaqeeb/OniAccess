Chlorine
is a
gas
found naturally occurring in the map. It is the second highest density gas after
Carbon Dioxide
at room temperature. Chlorine is a great deterrent against
germs
, and the best possible
heat
insulator among gases.
Chlorine is deadly to all germs including
Zombie Spores
and
Food Poisoning
. Surrounding any storage building with chlorine for a full cycle is a sure way to totally disinfect all contents of said building. A
Duplicant
traveling through Chlorine will not be cured of any diseases, but the surface germs on them will be subjected to the toxic gas.
Duplicants moving through Chlorine for more than three seconds will be hit by the
Eye Irritation
debuff, which reduces Athletics.
Production
Chlorine is a renewable resource, produced by the
Chlorine Gas Vent
.
Bleach Stone
in its item state emits Chlorine unless pressurized (for example, under
water
).
The
Rust Deoxidizer
produces chlorine along with its other outputs.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Chlorine Clouds at a rate of 135 – 405 kg per cycle.
In Radioactive Gas Clouds at a rate of 9.1 – 36.4 kg per cycle.
Usage
A Chlorine gas room is great at disinfection and heat insulation.
Contact with Chlorine gas will quickly kill all
germs
from the contents of machines and storage buildings. However, it is important to note that it will not remove germs from contents of pipes.
It is good for germy
slime
storage and
processing
(or any items you want to disinfect). Just remember that it takes a full cycle to destroy all germs.
While not perfect, it also can serve as early, more-resilient alternative to the
Vacuum
chamber.
As an insulator it is slightly worse than
Insulated Tile
made of
Ceramic
, however its low density makes it a poor buffer even when pressurized.
The
Ore Scrubber
removes germs from objects and it is a way to use chlorine. However, it needs a duplicant operator, who also needs to handle the infected goods.
The
Balm Lily
needs to be surrounded by chlorine and does not need any care at all. It has good synergy with
Stable
of
Dreckos
, generating infinite renewable
Phosphorite
.
Balm Lily Flower
and Phosphorite dropped by Dreckos, used together produce
Medical Pack
, which heals
Slimelung
.
Dasha Saltvine
requires Chlorine atmosphere and consumes small amounts of it (6 g/s, or 3.6 kg/cycle).
Gas Grass
consumes liquid Chlorine (500 g/cycle).
Squeaky Pufts
and Puft Princes can breathe Chlorine and excrete
Bleach Stone
, which is consumed by
Hand Sanitizers
,
Hot Tubs
, and
Waterweed
.
Megafrond
requires Chlorine atmosphere and consumes it at a rate of
90 g/s
.
Failing to use excess of Chlorine, you can always pump it out into the
void of Space
.
Misconceptions
For a time it was believed that deleting
Germs
consumes Chlorine, which is false.
This rumor started because

Polluted Oxygen
