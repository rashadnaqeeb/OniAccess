Ladder Bed
Provides a sleeping area for one Duplicant and also functions as a ladder.
Duplicants will automatically sleep in their assigned beds at night.
Duplicant's sleep will be interrupted if another Duplicant uses the ladder.

Ladder Bed
is a combination of a sleeping
Cot
and a
Ladder
.
It can be stacked vertically to save space in tight rooms, like rocket interiors. Ladder Beds can be connected at their ladder-part without becoming entombed, even if only one of them is mirrored.
When
dupes
climb the ladder of a currently used bed, the sleeping standard duplicant is disrupted and gains the "Unrested:BedJostling" debuff, which causes -5%/cycle
Stress
and -5%/cycle Immunity.  Bionic duplicants do not gain debuffs if jostled while defragmenting.
