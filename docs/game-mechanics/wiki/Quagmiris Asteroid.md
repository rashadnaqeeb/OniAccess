Quagmiris Asteroid
A large, polluted swamp world.
Quagmiris Asteroids are a great mass of frozen slush and wasteland. With a short distance to the surface, inter-world travel can be achieved quickly.

The
Quagmiris Asteroid
is a small starting asteriod with a
Swampy Biome
start. The starting area is surrounded by
Wasteland
,
Jungle
,
Tundra
and
Barren

Magma Biome

Strategy
Despite the flavour text the distance to the surface is the same on all three starting asteroids.
The
Quagmiris Asteroid
contains the
Swampy starting biome
, which is very messy, full of
Mud
and
Polluted Mud
,
Polluted Dirt
,
Polluted Water
,
Swamp Chard
plants, and some clean
Water
. It is rich in resources but they require refinement. It also contains the new
Wasteland Biome
, the
Tundra Biome
, and a hot
Barren Biome
with some free refined
Iron
.
The
Quagmiris Asteroid
contains no
Meteor Shower
,So that players can feel free to build in the
Space Biome
.
This is the DLC's unique start and there are several new
Buildings
designed to address it, such as the
Sublimation Station
and
Sludge Press
. The Swamp Start has a hard beginning but an easier mid-game because the Swamp Start's surroundings result in it slowly getting colder, not hotter as with the Terra Asteroid. The infinite water sources are both cold --
Cool Slush Geysers
and
Cool Salt Slush Geysers
-- which can be harnessed with a
Liquid Tepidizer
instead of requiring plastic to build an Aquatuner-Steam Turbine combo. A
Hydrogen Vent
and
Cool Steam Vent
can also be found in the nearby
Jungle Biome
along with a drecko spawn for making exosuits.
