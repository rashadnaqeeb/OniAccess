Plywood
is an alternative form of
Wood
that can be used as a substitute in recipes and the construction of buildings. It is exclusively obtained via the
Plywood Press
.
Usage
Duplicants can craft Plywood at the Plywood Press by combining 90kg of
Plant Husk
+ 10kg of
Resin
into 100kg of Plywood.
Plywood can be used as a substitute for
Wood
at a 1:1 ratio in most cases, including those of construction, crafting recipes (such as all of those in
Kilns
and
Smokers
), and it can also be turned into
Ethanol
via the
Ethanol Distiller
.
However, it is important to note that Plywood differs in some aspects from Wood, such as having a slightly higher thermal conductivity (0.350 versus Wood's 0.220), a slightly lower sublimation point (550°C versus Wood's 600°C), a slightly lower
Radiation Absorption
(0.58 versus Wood's 0.60), and no decor bonus value (compared to Wood's +50% decor bonus for wood-based buildings).
