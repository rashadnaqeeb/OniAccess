# Wash Basin

Wash Basin
Removes some Germs from Duplicants.
Germ-covered Duplicants use Wash Basins when passing by in the selected direction.
Germ spread can be reduced by building these where Duplicants often get dirty.

Wash Basin
is a
Medicine
Building
that cleanses
Duplicants
of
Germs
. The Germs removed from Duplicants are deposited in
Polluted Water
in the building's storage. When the amount of Polluted Water reaches 200 kg, it will be dropped as a bottle on the bottom-left tile.
Dupes will wash their hands in a Wash Basin when passing by it and the Wash Basin is supplied with water.
Wash Basin has a Set Direction setting that determines which direction/s Duplicants will wash their hands when passing by.
