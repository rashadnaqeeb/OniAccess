Tips
Although Gas Grass does not require a specific atmosphere to grow, some gases absorb light which can result in the plants not reaching the required illumination. Natural Gas absorbs the most light out of the elements that are gases at the required temperatures, followed by Chlorine, Carbon Dioxide and Polluted Oxygen. Gases absorb more light at higher pressures. Oxygen absorbs no light.
