# The Frosty Planet Pack

The
Duplicants
find themselves out in the cold, trying to keep the colony - and themselves - alive in a world of ice and snow!
In The Frosty Planet Pack, you'll excavate the frigid landscape of a brand-new starting planet to explore new biomes, master thermoregulation and harness new technology to build a megacolony that can truly conquer the elements.
Investigate brand-new biomes bound by relentless cold, gathering useful new building materials. The only thing cooler than a snow fort is a snow fort in outer space! Unless the room gets warm enough to melt the floor, that is.
Get up close and personal with curious critters who have useful things to teach colonies about adapting to this glacial environment.
Discover new elements and unlock new buildings that help manage frozen resources, keep Duplicants warm enough to do their favorite activities (i.e. work) and produce delicious, nutritious meals.
Harvest peculiar cold-resistant plants not found anywhere else in the universe, and devise ways to use them as sustainable sources of food, oxygen, and fuel for your industrial ambitions.
Customize your colony with a selection of new cosmetic skins that make everything from clothing to furniture feel cozier and more rustic.
Plunge into the deepest depths of the frosty planet's core to find an abandoned engineering project left behind by mysterious predecessors. Restore it to its former glory, and it could be really... power-full.
New technology to master, biomes to explore and critters to meet - break the ice in
The Frosty Planet Pack
!
