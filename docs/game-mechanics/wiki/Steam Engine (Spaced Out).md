Tips
Even though its range of 10 seems to only allow 5 tiles distance when round trip is taken into consideration, its cheap fuel and height allowance can stretch that distance considerably. By putting a rocket platform on a planetoid with a
Gas Rocket Port Unloader
powered by the rocket's own battery module, it can unload steam right out of the rocket's
Gas Cargo Canister
, allowing the
Steam Engine
to go to any planetoid within 10 hexes of its starting location, and 5 hexes roundtrip to any Space POI.
Thanks to it being slow, the
Steam Engine
provides much more opportunities for a Duplicant to interact with the rocket controls, helping to ensure that Duplicant will max out piloting in only a few missions.
A rocket with the
Steam Engine
obviously emits a large amount of
Steam
on launch or landing, which can be recycled using
Gas Pumps
made of
Steel
,
Niobium
, or
Thermium
. This is usually more than the amount of Steam consumed as fuel, especially when the distance to be traveled is short, since the amount of Steam produced is unrelated to distance traveled and thus fuel consumption, allowing a
Steam Engine
to be resource-positive, producing renewable
Water
. The first bit of Steam can be produced by heating Water with hot
Regolith
, or
submerging a full
Metal Power Bank
or
Eco Power Bank
under 100-300 kg of Water, allowing the power bank to explode and produce enough heat to turn Water into Steam of suitable temperature.
