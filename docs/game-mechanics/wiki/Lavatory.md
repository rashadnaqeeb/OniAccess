Lavatory
is a
building
used by
Duplicants
to empty their bladders. It requires
Water
intake and produces
Polluted Water
and
Food Poisoning
Germs
. Some of these germs are left on each
Duplicant
that uses it. It is an upgrade from the
Outhouse
, as it uses plumbing to dispose of waste and produces fewer Germs.
Usage
The Lavatory provides a place for Duplicants to relieve themselves while reducing the spread of
Diseases
.
It fulfills the 'Flush Toilet' Criteria for a
Washroom
, providing +2 Morale.
The Lavatory outputs more liquid than it takes in, so it can be used to generate a small amount of Water or Dirt when paired with a
Water Sieve
. One Duplicant with a normal bladder will produce at least ~11.16 g/s Water, which can be used in an
Electrolyzer
to cover a ninth of their oxygen intake. However, the amount produced is small enough that to keep an
Electrolyzer
running non-stop, it would require 52 Duplicants to use the bathroom each cycle, or 90 if the bathroom is also sustained by the toilets' output.
If adding the water produced to your supply, you would have to worry about disinfecting the Polluted Water, which will have
millions
of germs in them.
Mechanics
The Lavatory uses 5 kg of Water and produces 11.7 kg of Polluted Water, both of which are carried through pipes. The resulting Polluted Water is the combination of the 5kg of input Water temperature and 6.7kg of
37 °C
/
98.6 °F
newly created Polluted Water, which means the Lavatory will equalize a
Bathroom Loop
temperature to
37 °C
/
98.6 °F
over time.
One use of the Lavatory produces 105,000 Food Poisoning germs. These germs are divided among the Duplicant, the Polluted Water, and the Lavatory itself as follows:
Duplicant: +3,350 germs
Polluted Water: +100,000 germs
Lavatory: +1,650 germs
Bionic Duplicants
will use a Lavatory (or
Outhouse
) when no
Gunk Extractor
is available. This does not use water, but does clog the toilet, requiring it to be unclogged with a
Tidying
errand. After unclogging, any
Gunk
expelled will leave through the Lavatories liquid output port.
Tips
Water Sieves
do not remove
Germs
from
Polluted Water
. It's not recommended to refine Polluted Water from Lavatories and mix it into your main water supply without cleansing it first.
The Lavatory is a recommended upgrade from the
Outhouse
, as it is more hygienic and does not require Duplicant maintenance.
Having an equal number of wash stations will ensure all Dupes wash their hands. Fewer may cause some Dupes to skip washing if all are in use at the same time.
This can be made easier with proper
Scheduling
to ensure less Duplicants are using the washroom at any one time.
