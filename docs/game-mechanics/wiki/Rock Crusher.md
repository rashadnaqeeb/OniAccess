# Rock Crusher

Rock Crusher
Inefficiently produces refined materials from raw resources.
Duplicants will not fabricate items unless recipes are queued.
Rock Crushers loosen nuggets from raw ore and can process many different resources.

Temperature
Rock crushers do not exchange heat with their contents, but the contents are not insulated from the environment, unlike the
Metal Refinery
. The output material is produced at the same temperature as the input.
Most of the products are dropped as solid debris by default, which can immediately melt if the input is too hot. For example, when crushing
Aluminum Ore
at above
663.3 °C
/
1225.94 °F
, instead of forming a bottle, the
Aluminum
output immediately turns into a puddle of
Molten Aluminum
, which can exchange heat directly with the Rock Crusher itself.
Note that when crushing
Cinnabar Ore
to produce
Mercury
, the output would be bottled by default. If the input is colder than
-41.85 °C
/
-43.33 °F
, the output forms a natural tile of
Solid Mercury
, entombing the Rock Crusher, and the tile can melt into a puddle of liquid Mercury if the Rock Crusher or the surroundings is warm enough.
