Neutronium
was an hypothetical chemical element with zero protons. Among its theorized properties was being the most resistant element, just like Neutronium in the game is unbreakable.
Lore
Despite the Duplicants inability to mine Neutronium, their human versions were able to devise a way to extract Neutronium from 'corrupted' surfaces. This method was never perfected, as stated in certain data entries, although it is possible that they intended to implement this technology with the Duplicants mining beam, as that was their stated goal in the data entry titled <
Possible Applications
>, which can be found under the research notes tab of the index.
The scientists of
Gravitas
believed the formation of Neutronium to be an autoimmune reaction of the universe, acting as an attempt to quarantine foreign materials to prevent temporal contamination.
