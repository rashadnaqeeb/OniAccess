Biobot
Main article:
Biobot
Biobots are robots that can do basic tasks, similar to a
Rover
. Their Steel body allows then to work in hostile environments, for example inside Magma.
Each biobot has 10 cycles worth of energy stored. Upon its depletion, biobot dies, droping its pod, which has deconstruct command automatically applied. Once deconstructed, all 300 kg of steel used to build a biobot are retrieved. With 10
sporechids
(the maximum number of germs the builder can intake), a maximum of 6 active Biobots can be active at any given time.
Thus, operational cost of continuously running a Biobot Builder is 240 W of power to run a gas pump plus occasional duplicant labor. Energy cost can be lowered by cycling gas from gas exhaust of Biobot Builder to its intake until it runs out of germs. Then old packets of gas have to be replaced with new ones.
