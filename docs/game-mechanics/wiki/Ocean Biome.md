# Ocean Biome

The
Ocean Biome
, also known as the
Tide Pool Biome
, is a warm
Biome
with large amounts of
Salt
,
Salt Water
, and
Sand
. There is no
Metal Ore
in this biome.
Strategy
Despite the lack of any and all metal; the
Ocean Biome
is full of valuable materials that are relatively easy to access compared to its composition. The large horizontal caverns mean that very little digging has to be done to navigate. If you want to avoid the risk of spilling
Salt Water
or
Hydrogen
into your base though; its also easy to completely avoid any generated caves as long as you make sure to avoid falling
Sand
.
Fossil
is an excellent source of
Lime
for obtaining large amounts of
Steel
.
Sedimentary Rock
is useful when ranching
Hatches
. It increases the chance of laying eggs for
Stone Hatches
, which can eat a wider variety of common minerals such as
Igneous Rock
.
