# Combat

Combat
is an
errand
which makes
Duplicants
fight
critters
. To create a Combat errand, either select a critter and click on the "Attack" button, or select the attack tool (press T) and drag a rectangle over the critter(s) you want to be attacked. A Duplicant's
Excavation
skill affects this job's speed.
Tips
Attacking critters is usually only useful for harvesting
meat
(most critters will drop fresh meat upon death), getting them out of the way, or pruning their populations,
Only
Pokeshell
are hostile to the player (when protecting their
Pokeshell Roe
, other critters will ignore the dupes unless attacked
Attacking uses
Supply reach
meaning you can attack across a 1 tile gap without being hurt in turn
