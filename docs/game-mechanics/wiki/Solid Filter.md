Solid Filter
Separates one Solid from the conveyor, sending it into a dedicated Conveyor Rail.
All solids are sent into the building's output conveyor, except the solid chosen for filtering.

is a building out of the
Shipping
tab and works the same as
Liquid Filter
and
Gas Filter

Usage
The Solid Filter has 2 outputs, the standard green output and another orange output in the center. The orange output is for the solid that was selected to be filtered out, and the green is for all the other solids that come through the filter. For example, using a
Conveyor Loader
, you may send the mixture of materials produced by a
Pip ranch
into the solid filter and send
Wood
to a separate pipe for use in a
Wood Burner
.
It only draws Power while it is in use (if it has solids moving through it). This means it is optimal to sort out the expected solids first. For example: a solid filtration system that primally finds
Polluted Dirt
with
Pacu Eggs
and
Pacu Fillet
sometimes should have a Polluted Dirt Solid Filter first and then the second most common solid, then the third.  Note: you can work out how common a given solid is by
storing
solids from the area into a
Storage Bin
until full, then looking at the contents.
When disabled by automation, the Solid Filter does not allow items to move through it, whether or not they are the selected item to be filtered. Therefore, in order to avoid backing up the conveyor network, a bypass must be used.
