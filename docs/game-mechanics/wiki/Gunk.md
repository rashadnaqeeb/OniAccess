Gunk
Gunk is the built-up grime and grit produced by Duplicants' bionic mechanisms.
It is unpleasantly viscous.

Usage
An
Apothecary
can be used to convert
80 kg
Gunk and
200 kg
of Water into
Gear Balm
, which is an early game substitute for a
Lubrication Station
.
Gunk may also be heated in order to convert 92% of its mass into
Petroleum
and 8% into
Sulfur
respectively.
Production
A
Gunk Extractor
produces Gunk when a
Bionic Duplicant
uses it, mass depending on how full their gunk tank is when they use it.
An
Outhouse
will become clogged with gunk when used by a
Bionic Duplicant
and will need to be cleaned manually, creating a bottle of gunk whose mass depends on how full their gunk tank is when they use it.
A
Lavatory
will become clogged with gunk when used by a
Bionic Duplicant
and will need to be cleaned manually, converting the water flushed and producing an extra
5 kg
of Gunk. This may be a bug.
A
Bionic Duplicant
will vomit
80 kg
Gunk once their gunk tank is full.
A
Bionic Duplicant
with major or extreme
Radiation Sickness
will vomit around
800 g
to
900 g
of Gunk, about 8 times per cycle. This does not reduce its Gunk tank level.
Tips
If you do not make a
Gunk Extractor
, be sure to assign a specific
Lavatory
to your Bionic Duplicants with separate plumbing in order to collect the Gunk and keep it separate from your polluted water.
Notes
Gunk is considered slippery, so dupes will slip on it and gain stress.
