The
Radbolt Chamber
is a
Radbolt
storage device.
Unlike a
Radbolt Generator
, which draws Radbolts from a high
Radiation
environment and launches them immediately as soon as it reaches a set capacity (between 50 and 500), the Radbolt Chamber can store twice as many Radbolts (up to 1,000) indefinitely, instead controlling its discharge via
Automation
. It also consumes one quarter the energy (120W instead of 480W) and generates one quarter the heat while powered, making it a far more reliable way of storing accumulated Radbolts while waiting for your dupes to get around to
Materials Study Terminal
research, or a
Radbolt Engine
-powered rocket to return home.
The downsides are it can't generate its own Radbolts, requiring generators to fill it up, and the discharge direction can't be changed - it only ever fires straight up, forcing practical design considerations before being built.
