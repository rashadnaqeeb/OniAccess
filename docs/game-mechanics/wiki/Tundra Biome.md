The sub-zero temperatures of the
Tundra Biome
provide large frozen deposits of
Ice
and
Snow

Wolframite
.
Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifiers
can also be present; providing a possible cooling solution.
Strategy
The
Tundra Biome
is bitter cold; although not cold enough to cause Frostbite. This along with the breathable air makes early exploration trivial, if not slow. The
Sleet Wheat
is an excellent late-game food source and
Wheezeworts
are a good low-power alternative to the
Aquatuner
+
Steam Turbine
combo.
Melting the
Ice
rather than mining it results in twice the
Water
than through mining and melting. Given the large amount of Ice; using this biome as a heat-sink for early industrialization can alleviate for quite some time.

Tundra Biome
The sub-zero temperatures of the Tundra Biome provide rare frozen deposits of
Ice
and
Snow
, necessary for a colony's
Heat
regulation needs.
Far from devoid of life, this biome contains some much needed plant life, ripe for cultivation.
Sleet Wheat
provides a nutrient rich ingredient for creating complex foods, though the plants do require sub-zero temperatures to thrive.
Fortunately
Wheezewort
can been planted on farms to lower surrounding temperatures.
Crucially, small deposits of
Wolframite
have been detected here. This is an extremely rare metal that should be preserved for
Tungsten
production.
Though my Duplicants appear more than happy to work in the Tundra Biome for short periods of time, I will need to provide proper
equipment
for them to avoid adverse affects to their well-being if they are working here for longer periods.
