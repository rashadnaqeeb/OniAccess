Sandstone
is one of the first materials someone will encounter. It is a
Raw Mineral
mostly found in the
Sandstone Biome
, the starting biome of
Terra Asteroid
or equivalent planetoid in
Planetoid Clusters
. Sandstone can be used in construction or as food for hatches.
Usage
Due to its ease of access and availability, Sandstone is a very convenient and reliable source of building material and is very likely to be the first construction material to be used in the start of a new game. Its +10% Decor bonus can be beneficial for buildings such as
paintings
and
sculptures
, though note that
Granite
is a better choice that gives a +20% bonus.
Sandstone's very low hardness translates to a tendency to break under high liquid pressures (above
4,000 kg
), which will lead to leaks. This can be circumvented by building
Tiles
three spaces thick.
Rock Crusher
can break Sandstone into
Sand
. 100 kg Sandstone = 100 kg Sand.
Hatches
can eat Sandstone and excrete
Coal
.
Production
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Sandy Ore Fields at a rate of 216 – 648 kg per cycle.
Tips
Sandstone Biome
is mostly made out of
Sandstone
natural tiles. The closest natural tiles to the
Printing Pod
come in 700 kg and 1840 kg and range from 600 kg to 1200 kg elsewhere.
Sandstone has a hardness of 10 (Soft), making it as easy to dig as sand but it is not collapsible like sand.  Its low heat capacity and relatively high thermal conductivity makes it a poor insulator against higher temperatures.
