Carbon Dioxide Geyser
is a renewable source of
Carbon Dioxide
, albeit in liquid form, which can be found as a randomly generated geyser in any biome or
Radioactive Biome
where it is guaranteed to spawn.
While erupting, emission can be blocked by over-pressurization. The maximum pressure it will emit at is 50 kg. When Carbon Dioxide is emitted, it will quickly absorb heat from the surrounding environment and evaporate into its gaseous form if the surrounding environment is warmer and it is not pumped away.
In some cases, especially in variants of
Radioactive Biome
, the surrounding temperature can be colder than the freezing point of Carbon Dioxide, leading to an accummulation of
Solid Carbon Dioxide
.
Tips
Alveo Vera
makes excellent use of the geyser, sitting at comfortable temperatures. However, the liquid Carbon Dioxide must first be evaporated.
Make sure to keep an eye out on your gas pressure. The geyser can easily overpressurise areas of one's base with carbon dioxide.
In desperate measures, liquid Carbon Dioxide can be used for cooling. Despite its relatively high
Thermal Conductivity
(more than twice that of
Water
), liquid Carbon Dioxide has a low
Specific Heat Capacity
. When carried in pipes, it can easily burst the pipes if not
managed properly
, due to its narrow temperature range.
