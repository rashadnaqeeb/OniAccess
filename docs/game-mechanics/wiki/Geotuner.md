Effects
The Geotuner can be used to increase the output of an analyzed geyser by 20%. The output of the geyser is also heated; the degree of temperature increase varies by geyser type.
Duplicants
with the Field Research
skill
must use resources to generate data at the Geotuner, which is consumed only when the geyser is erupting. Each geyser can be targeted by up to 5 Geotuners, although each Geotuner can only target one geyser at a time. Multiple Geotuning Amplifications are additive when combined, meaning 5 Geotuners targeting the same geyser will multiply its output by 2.0x. The base duration one tuning task takes is 30 seconds.
UI
The UI of the Geotuner allows you to see any unearthed geysers.  The geyser must have been previously selected in the world to appear on this list, regardless of any activity or if it has been excavated fully, despite whether or not they have been analyzed. Any unanalyzed geysers will be greyed out, and are unselectable. Analyzed geysers can be selected, and when hovered over will display the amount that the temperature and volume will be boosted by, as well as the
Resource
needed to complete the geotuning. The type of material needed to boost each geyser varies by type of geyser, but the amount is not shown until selected. The amount of Geotuners targeting each geyser is also shown on the UI.
