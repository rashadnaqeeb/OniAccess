Deodorizer
is a building that reduces the threat of
Slimelung
infection by purifying
Polluted Oxygen
in its vicinity.
The Deodorizer needs to be periodically supplied
Filtration Medium
to work. It will drop the created
Clay
on the ground.
The temperature of the output materials is the combined one of the input materials. Deodorizers require 5 Watts of power to operate.
Mechanics
The Deodorizer seems to be able to catch Polluted Oxygen 2 tiles away (a 5 tile tall and wide star/cross shaped area). It will not catch anything behind mechanical doors or (non-mesh/airflow) tiles however.
The deodorizer converts 90% of the
Polluted Oxygen
to
Oxygen
. The rest of the Polluted Oxygen, as well as the Filtration Medium it consumes, is converted into
Clay
.
Usage
Use Deodorizers to clean the air near structures that will emit
Polluted Oxygen
,
Polluted Water
, or
Polluted Dirt
when working, such as
Outhouses
and
Wash Basins
. When exploring the asteroid, you can use Deodorizers to clean pockets of Polluted Oxygen and then pump the clean Oxygen in your colony. Note that the Deodorizer does
not
remove
germs
from the air, although germs will slowly die off once the oxygen has been cleaned.
Tips
It can overpressure the area despite normal gas vent allowing 2000g of gas in an area (this is an early game
high pressure gas vent
when filtering
Polluted Oxygen
).  This makes it perfect for filtration to a gas tank for
oxygen
storage.
Since
Polluted Water
naturally converts itself into Polluted Oxygen over time, an array of Deodorizers over an open pool of Polluted Water can (very slowly) convert the Polluted Water into Oxygen. Even though this process is too slow to be your primary source of Oxygen, it also produces Clay which is an important resource for
Ceramic
production.
Since Deodorizers can collect Polluted Oxygen from two tiles away, and since liquids don't block this reach, it is possible to create a perfect seal between the Oxygen and Polluted Oxygen by placing a liquid tile in between the Deodorizer and Polluted Oxygen.
