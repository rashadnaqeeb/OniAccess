The
Sugar Engine
is one of the first
engines
available to the Dupes. It stores 450 kg
Sucrose
. The
Sugar Engine
needs oxidizer to operate.
Without
The Frosty Planet Pack
, Sucrose can only be acquired by ranching
Divergents
, and can be used as fertilizer, meaning the similarly early researched
Carbon Dioxide Engine
is usually the more common option. The
Sugar Engine
, however, has the advantage of double the range of the Carbon Dioxide Engine specifically for round trips due to the ability to refuel the
Sugar Engine
immediately after landing by storing Sucrose fuel in the spacefarer module via a storage bin.
However, with
The Frosty Planet Pack
, the
Sugar Engine
can be available early, since Sucrose can be found in
Cool Pool Biome
in large amounts as natural tiles, or obtained by boiling or freezing
Nectar
, and for oxidizer,
Oxylite
can be found in
Ice Cave Biome
or obtained by farming
Alveo Veras
.

at
