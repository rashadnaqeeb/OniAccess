Beach chair
is used during
downtime
to improve a duplicant's
morale
. Despite being a recreational building, it does not need to be in a recreational
room
. However, it will provide greater morale bonus when its left bottom tile has brightness over 10000 Lux.  Each use of the beach chair takes 145 seconds.  This is the longest usage time of any recreation building equaling roughly 6 in game hours/schedule blocks. The
stress
reduction (-15%/cycle) and stamina drain reduction (+50%/cycle) buff called "Totally Chill" is only active while using the building and occurs whether it is lit or not.
Tips
Sun Lamp
is not obligatory for this building, it is possible to build "beach room" in
Space Biome
, it will not necessarily interfere with
Solar Panels
either.
The stamina recovery provided by Beach chair is only enough to slow down base stamina drain from -70%/cycle to -20%/cycle and thus cannot replace beds.
The building will tile together similarly to the
Mess Table
and
Planter Boxes
.
After gaining all available recreation buffs, a dupe will begin reusing buildings if a recreation room is not present. Read more on
Recreation
for strategies to prevent the massive loss of 145/600 seconds in a cycle.
If downtime occurs before bedtime in a schedule, the long duration may cause dupes to miss sleep entirely.
