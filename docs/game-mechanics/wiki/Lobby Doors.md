found in
Ruins

, except that it cannot be built and does not require
power
.
The Lobby Doors are similar to other doors which can be round in
Ruins
, such as the
Security Door
or
Gravitas Door
; unlike the former, it does not require a bioscan to open initially, and unlike the latter, it is not possible to change access for individual
Duplicants
.
The Lobby Doors are made of 200 kg of
Steel
, and can be deconstructed to reclaim that amount of steel.
