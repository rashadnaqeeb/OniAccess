Cool Salt Slush Geysers are a renewable source of cold
Brine
. They can be found in the
frozen biome
and, much like the
Cool Slush Geyser
, can effectively provide cooling for bases or a small amount of usable
water

salt water geysers

They can overpressurize like all other geysers when the tile
above
their tile of interest exceeds 500kg of pressure.
Cool slush geysers average 1500g/s of brine overall, placing them in the second tier of water-related geysers.
