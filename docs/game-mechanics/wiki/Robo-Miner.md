# Robo-Miner

Robo-Miner
Automatically digs out all materials in a set range.
A robo-miner's range can be viewed at any time by selecting the building.

Robo-Miner
will mine out a space 7 tiles around itself, in a rectangular shape. It cannot destroy player-built tiles/buildings or any tiles that require
Superhard digging
proficiency (diamond, abyssalite, etc).
The Robo-Miner cannot dig itself out when entombed.
The Robo-Miner is rarely used to dig out new areas of the asteroid, but rather to automatically remove tiles that keep building up in the same area over and over again, e.g.
Regolith
from
Meteors
or in
Shove Vole
ranches, or
Igneous Rock
from
Magma
in boilers and volcano tamers.
Due to significant heat production, some form of cooling is generally required. It is also recommended to build the robo-miner out of
steel
to increase the overheat temperature.
Tips
Robo-Miner can have line-of-sight through diagonal openings and closed
Pneumatic Doors
. This means that a Robo-miner can clear tiles within a sealed room.
