A Power Bank
holds a small amount of energy and can power
Bionic Duplicants
,
Flydos
,
Compact Discharger
or
Large Discharger
.
All Power Banks have a capacity of 120 kJ.
Eco Power Banks are made in the
Soldering Station
with 200kg of
Abyssalite
, and start out as Empty Eco Power Banks. To fill or refill Empty Eco Power Banks, a
Power Bank Charger
is used.
Power Bank chargers
consume 480 W of
power
, but only delivers 400 W into the Eco Power Bank (a -80 W inefficiency). As Eco Power Bank has a capacity of 120 kJ, charging one will take 300 seconds.
When reaching 0 kJ, an Eco Power Bank will be replaced by an Empty Eco Power Bank. Eco Power Banks weigh 20 kg.
In contact with water-based liquids, charged Eco Power Banks rapidly take damage, exploding on death. On death, it drops
Power Bank Scrap
, which can be recycled into
Abyssalite
at the
Rock Crusher
. Empty power banks do not take damage.
Strategy
Each
Power Bank Charger
can fill 2 power banks every cycle, as
bionic duplicants
consume one power bank every cycle or 200 W continuously it is best to put one charger for every 2 duplicants.
Eco Power banks are typically the preferred method of charging bionic dupes or flydos, the other applicable power banks will drain a lot of your metal supplies.
