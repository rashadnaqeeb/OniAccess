# Achievements

Achievements
are various short-term and long-term goals and challenges. Achievements are tracked by each colony within the Colony Summary screen, as well as globally through Platform Achievements.
[
1
]
Platform Achievements are disabled on saves where
Sandbox mode
is enabled or where
Debug Commands
have been used (indicated by a red "X" in the Colony Summary screen), but are not disabled by Mods.
Selecting an achievement in the Colony Summary screen shows detailed progress towards that achievement.
Some achievements (namely "Carnivore", "Locavore" and "Super Sustainable") have certain conditions that will permanently disqualify the achievement if broken. Disqualified achievements are indicated by their text and icon being extra gray.
There are two types of Achievements: Imperatives and Initiatives.
