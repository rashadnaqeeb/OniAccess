# Guide/Boil Polluted Water

Intro
You may want to boil polluted water for the following reasons:
Get clean water
Kill food poisoning / slimelung
Get small amounts of dirt/sand
Builds
Build 1
Below I will demonstrate a proof of concept solution that can be adapted and used to fit your needs. The benefits of the following idea are the following.
Produces power using natural heat sources and therefore should cover the cost of the aquatuner and auto miners
Bulleted list item
Reasonably easy to make and quite affordable
Can easily cure 1000kg of water per cycle with just one steam turbine
There following might also be downsides.
Creating a geothermal spike requires some mid-late game materials such as steel for the control door.
Using a geothermal spike in conjunction with a heat deleter such as a steam turbine will eventually cool and solidify the lava, in other words it is a finite resource. However you can use a volcano instead.
The clean water will obviously be hot when it comes out, so ideally you would have a use for all that heat otherwise it is just a waste of geothermal energy.
This build is just an adaption of a geothermal build, there are a few different types of geothermal builds you could adapt to boil polluted water. Please note that this build is just a quick proof of concept that I made and is by no means perfect. If you intend to do this please plan ahead and test your build in sandbox mode and if you make a great build you can add the details to this page.
Build 2
Boiling polluted water using the heat from a coolant loop for your industrial brick or air conditioning may seem sensible but you will need a very large amount of heat (oxygen stores far less heat than water) and a lot of aquatuners or a lot of time. Therefore I don't think this is a viable early/mid game option and using a water sieve plus a chlorine treatment room will be much easier and use less power.
