# Conductive Wire Bridge

Conductive Wire Bridge
Carries more Wattage than a regular Wire Bridge without overloading.
Runs one wire section over another without joining them.
Can be run through wall and floor tile.
Splitting generators onto separate systems can prevent overloads and wasted electricity.
