Interplanetary Launcher
sends
Interplanetary Payloads
to other asteroids, allowing for an autonomous transfer of
Solids
,
Liquids
,
Gases
across worlds.
Despite its animation sending payloads in a very narrow, single tile-trajectory, it requires a 4-tile clearance to the sky (the very right of its 5 tiles doesn't need clearance).
Each payload can hold up to 200 kg and costs 10
radbolts
per hex distance to launch. After being launched, payloads can be seen traveling on the
Starmap
at a speed of 1 hex per minute (6 hexes per cycle). Every 6 launches, it requires automatic maintenance, which causes a 60-second delay.
The launcher itself can store up to 1200 kg and 200 radbolts. Heat is only produced while launching.
It is loaded via conveyor rail input.
