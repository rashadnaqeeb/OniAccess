Granite
is a
Raw Mineral
found in the "Granite ring" that surrounds the starting
Temperate Biome
or
Forest Biome
. It is also found in the
Frozen Biome
,
Oil Biome
and
Barren Biome
. It can be used as a general construction material. A
Duplicant
must learn the Hard Digging Skill to dig Granite.
Usage
Granite is typically accessed quite early as it surrounds the starting point. It has high thermal conductivity but a low thermal capacity, which makes it better suited to transfer Heat between areas rather than insulating it.
Granite carries a +20% decor bonus, 10% higher than the very common
Sandstone
. Thus, Granite is valuable for constructing buildings that are used for decor, such as
paintings
or
sculptures
, or for building
Tiles
for areas frequented by Duplicants.
Granite has the highest
Thermal Conductivity
of all the Minerals, making it useful as a poor man's Radiant Pipe. At the same time, this might also be a danger as it might transfer undesired temperatures.
Marble Block
requires 400 kg Granite or
Obsidian
.
Rock Crusher
can break Granite into
Sand
. 100 kg Granite = 100 kg Sand.
Stone Hatches
can eat Granite and excrete
Coal
.
Production
The Rocky Asteroid Field provides a renewable source of Granite.
The
Geothermal Power Plant
emits Granite as impurities.
