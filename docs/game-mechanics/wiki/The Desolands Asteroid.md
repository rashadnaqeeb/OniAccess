The Desolands Asteroid
A barren location with an overabundance of mineral resources and oil.
The rocky terrain of The Desolands poses no immediate threats, but the limited variety of resources will make expansion and technological progress challenging.

The
Desolands Asteroid
is a small starting asteroid with a
Sandstone Biome
start.
Map Layout
The layout is much similar to
The Badlands
, with an abundance of raw materials like
Granite
due to Barren Biomes. This planetoid also has  a lot of
Crude Oil
and
Oil Resevoirs
as oily biomes are common.
Example The Desolands Asteroid Layout
Strategy
Reed fiber can be obtained through
Dreckos
, but
Thimble Reeds
do not spawn. For food
Bristle Blossoms
,
Mealwood
and
Pincha Pepperplants
are present, and for ranching there are
Dreckos
,
Shine Bugs
,
Hatches
and
Slicksters
.
Without the
Radioactive Crust World Trait
,
Uranium Ore
is unavailable on the asteroid. Radiation for
Materials Science Research
and other purposes will need to be sourced from uranium from the
Radioactive Ocean Asteroid
via
teleporter
, the
Crashed Satellites
world trait, space,
Shine Bugs
, or duplicants with the
Glow Stick
trait.
