# Ration Box

Ration Box
Stores a small amount of Food.
Food must be delivered to boxes by Duplicants.
Ration boxes keep food safe from hungry critters, but don't slow food spoilage.

Ration Box
is an item used to store and maintain your
Food
. One is always generated to the left of the
Printing Pod
at the start of every game with 16,000 kcal of
Nutrient Bars
inside, and its filter set to Nutrient Bars only. It can store 150 kg of food.
Tips
Ration Boxes can be renamed.
Food stored in the Ration Box will slowly grow stale and
rot
. See
Food Storage
for ways to slow this down and eventually prevent it altogether.
An easy way to slow down spoilage is to keep your Ration Boxes in pits filled with
Carbon Dioxide
. Being one of the heaviest gasses, it will stay in the pit.
Some kinds of food like
Nutrient Bars
,
Muckroot
, and
Hexalent Fruit
do not spoil and might serve as a backup food source.
The starting Ration Box is made of
Sedimentary Rock
. Destroying it allows Sedimentary Rock to be filterable in Storage Boxes & Critter Feeders for Stone Hatch farming, which is often inaccessible early on due to the
Granite
barriers.
Food stored in a CO2-filled pit rots more slowly.
Trivia
Tile of interest
for deliveries is the bottom left square.
