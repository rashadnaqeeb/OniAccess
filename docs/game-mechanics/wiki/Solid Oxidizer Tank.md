The
Solid Oxidizer Tank
is a
rocket
module that stores the oxygen needed for the engines to work in the vacuum of space in the form of
oxylite
. The mass of oxylite carried can be adjusted between 0 kg and 2700 kg. The oxylite and
petroleum
are burnt at a 1:1 mass ratio, so one completely full solid oxidizer tank is enough for three full
Liquid Fuel Tanks
.
A
gantry
is required for Duplicants to make deliveries to this module.
Oxidizer tanks (either solid or liquid) are required by the
Petroleum Engine
and
Hydrogen Engine
.
Bugs
Duplicants may sometimes not deliver
oxylite
to the Solid Oxidizer Tank - reason is unknown. Fix: Restart the game.
