Production
The
Metal Refinery
can produce Cobalt from
its ore
in 1:1 ratio. 100 kg Cobalt Ore = 100 kg Cobalt. It requires Duplicants operation.
An alternative way to produce Cobalt is
Smooth Hatches
herding. A Smooth Hatch roughly eats 100 kg Cobalt Ore and excretes 75 kg Cobalt.
Rock Crusher
can produce Cobalt from Cobalt Ore too. This is not recommended due to lower efficiency of 50%. 100 kg Cobalt Ore = 50 kg Cobalt + 50 kg
Sand
. It requires Duplicants operation.
Tips
Cobalt has an extremely high thermal conductivity so it can be used for temperature shift plates instead of diamond if needed.
