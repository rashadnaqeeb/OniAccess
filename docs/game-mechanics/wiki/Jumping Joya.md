Jumping Joya
Decorative Plant
The Jumping Joya is a decorative plant that brings a feeling of calmness and wellbeing to individuals in its vicinity.
Their rounded appendages and eccentrically shaped polyps are a favorite of interior designers looking to offset the rigid straight walls of an institutional setting.
The Jumping Joya's capacity to thrive in many environments and the ease in which they propagate make them the go-to house plant for the lazy gardener.
