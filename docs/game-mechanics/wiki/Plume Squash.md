Usage
Plume Squash can be eaten by
Duplicants
to restore
Calories
. They have a
Food Quality
of 0 (Terrible).
4000kcal of Plume Squash and 1000g of
Tallow
can be cooked into 5400kcal of
Squash Fries
at the
Deep Fryer
, increasing its kcal and Food Quality by 1400 and 3, respectively.
Plume Squash can be eaten by
Critters
.
Hatches
eat Plume Squash at a rate of 700kcal/cycle and convert them into 131.3g/cycle of
Coal
.
Sage Hatches
eat Plume Squash at a rate of 700kcal/cycle and convert them into 175g/cycle of Coal.
Bammoths
eat Plume Squash at a rate of 1778kcal/cycle and convert them into 30kg/cycle of
Bammoth Patty
.
Production
Domestic
Plume Squash Plants
produce Plume Squash at a rate of 4000 kcal per 9 cycles, totaling 444kcal/cycle (111kcal/cycle if wild).
Tips
Plume squash is the most dense food that can be produced fully automated (without any duplicant action). This makes it particularly appealing as a food source for
