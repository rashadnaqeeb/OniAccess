# Compostable

Compostable
s are a class of resource that can be put into a
Compost
to transform it into
Dirt
and heat.
There are the following compostables in the game:

Composting cultivated food is wasteful most of the time, and only makes sense with
Bristle Blossoms
, since they do not consume
dirt
.
Excess of wild produce also can serve as a source of dirt. Composting infected food can be done to prevent
Food Poisoning
.
Egg Shell
Egg shells are valuable as a source of
Lime
but you can choose to compost them instead.
Plant seeds
Excess seeds that you do not need can be manually composted.
Balm Lily Flower
Similarly, excesses of plant products can be manually turned into a bit of free dirt.
