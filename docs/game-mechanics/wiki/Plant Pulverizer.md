The Plant Pulverizer is interesting if you need only small amounts of
Brackene
that doesn't justify the cost of setting up a
Gassy Moo
Stable
.
It can also be useful if you overproduce
Nosh Bean
,
Pincha Peppernut
or
Sleet Wheat Grain
to produce a complementary amount of
Brackene
rather than letting your overproduced supplies rot.
It may also be used in mid-game as a way to get
Brackene
earlier on and not having to wait until being able to explore space and retrieve
Gassy Moos
with a
Biological Cargo Bay
.
The Plant Pulverizer is useful in mid-game for
Bionic Duplicants
that need to replenish their
Gear Oil
as it converts
Slime
to

used in the
Lubrication Station
. Be aware that if the
Slime
contained by the building contains
Slimelung
germs, the transporting
Duplicant
, the building, and the output
Phyto Oil
will contain germs.
