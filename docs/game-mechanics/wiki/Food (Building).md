# Food (Building)

This article is about the building category. For food consumed by Duplicants, see
Food (Resource)
.
Food
buildings
focus on food production and storage. Food is one of the most important
resources
in Oxygen Not Included.
Every duplicant needs 1000 kilocalories per cycle (1500 if the duplicant has the Bottomless Stomach trait). Food items that are not refrigerated, nor stored in sterile environment will spoil and eventually turn into
Rot Piles
. All food loaded into cooking stations is kept in a stasis, preventing spoiling.
Sources
In the starting
Temperate Biome
,
Forest Biome
,
Swampy Biome
or
Ice Cave Biome
, there is
Buried Muckroot
,
Hexalent
,
Swamp Chard Heart
, or
Sherberry
that could be harvested. 16,000 kcal
Nutrient Bars
(or
18,571 kcal
Pemmican
) are available in the
Ration Box
near the
Printing Pod
.
Plants can be farmed in
Planter Boxes
,
Farm Tiles
or
Hydroponic Farms
as a main source of food. These products may be cooked on
Electric Grill
,
Gas Range
or
Microbe Musher
(for
Berry Sludge
and
Pemmican
), which will increase food's quality (and possibly calories), at the cost of additional duplicant labor and careful planning of what combination of foods to use.
Another source of food is hunting down
Critters
. These creatures may defend themselves, which may lead to a duplicant being incapacitated, if they were especially low on health. Critters can be domesticated using a
Grooming Station
, increasing their metabolism, reproduction rate and thus meat (and other resources) production, but also making them require a steady supply of resources as foods for them, which can involve a variety of resources including food, plants, minerals and metals.
In case of emergency
Microbe Musher
may be used to produce
Mush Bar
and
Liceloaf
, but it will consume great amount of water.
Tips
In the early game, it is recommended to not produce a great surplus of food because of spoiling, if deep freeze and sterile atmosphere are not readily available.
Storing food in a refrigerator or below
4 °C
/
39.2 °F
will slow spoil rate, Keeping the food in deep freeze (below
-18 °C
/
-0.4 °F
) and a sterile environment (such as immersing the food in
Carbon Dioxide
,
Chlorine
or
Hydrogen
) will keep the food from spoiling.
Polluted Oxygen
, as a Contaminated Atmosphere, will increase how quickly food spoils.
Spice Grinder
can be used to add Freshener Spice on food, which keeps food from spoiling when refrigerated in a normal atmosphere (such as
Oxygen
), or even make food regain freshness in more preservative environments, at the cost of
Mealwood
seeds and
Salt
.
