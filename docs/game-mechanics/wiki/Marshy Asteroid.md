Marshy Asteroid
A small location with an abundance of marshland.
While Marshy Asteroids offer an abundance of organic resources like Slime Mold and Algae, their air quality poses a significant disease risk for Duplicants.

Strategy
For food, the player can dig
Slime
(and expose slime to cosmic radiation or using other methods to sterilize
Slimelung
) and fertilize
Dusk Caps
for food. Also,
Slime
can be heated for
Dirt
, and the player can bring seeds to make
Mealwood
farms (for
Drecko
ranching or otherwise), or they can bring
Bog Bucket
seeds and use the straight polluted water. The player can also ranch
Pacus
, but it is recommended to bring large amounts of food (for 20+ cycles) while the ranch starts up. If
Pips
are brought, they can wild-plant
Thimble Reeds
to feed themselves, or wild-plant
Arbor Tree
acorns, and they can be sustainably ranched for
Dirt
and
Omelette
. Domestic Thimble Reeds can be used as an immediate food source for Pips.
To deal with the
Magma Biome
at the bottom of the asteroid (where
Tungsten Volcanoes
usually locate in), a
Liquid Airlock
can be placed to keep a
Vacuum
in the Magma Biome, and then apply strategies similar to the
Superconductive Asteroid
. Note that the Magma Biome might contain milligrams of
Oxygen
, which can break the vacuum and scald
Duplicants
, so it might be a good idea to build tiles on the cells with oxygen to crush and delete it.
The
Tungsten Volcanoes
in the Magma Biome often come with a layer of
Tungsten
natural tiles above their
Neutronium
base, which can be leveraged for efficient heat dispersal, but the second tile from the left must be removed in order to analyze the volcanoes.
