Oxygen Not Included
(sometimes shortened to
ONI
) is a space-colony simulation video game developed by
Klei Entertainment
. It allows players to manage their
Duplicants
and help them
dig
,
build
and maintain a subterranean asteroid colony. Duplicants will need food, oxygen, sanitation and ambient temperatures to keep them alive, and even more than that to keep them happy.
The alpha release date of
Oxygen Not Included
was the 15th of February 2017 on Steam, moving into early access on 18th of May 2017. It was officially published on 30th of July 2019.
