Large Sculpting Block
is a
decorative
building in the
Furniture category
that provides a
Decor
bonus to its surroundings and can
be sculpted
to provide a further bonus. The quality of the sculpture influences this bonus with higher quality sculptures from highly skilled artistic
Duplicants
producing the best Decor bonuses.
Large Sculpting Blocks are most efficient in high traffic areas and can also be constructed in gaps between buildings to add decor to a room.
There is 1 kind of crude, 1 kind of quaint and 6 kinds of masterpiece sculptures; 3 of the masterpieces are locked behind
Blueprints
. Each artistic quality provides an additional decor bonus: +5 for Crude, +10 for Quaint, +15 for Masterpiece.
