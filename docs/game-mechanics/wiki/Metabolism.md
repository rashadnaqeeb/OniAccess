# Metabolism

Metabolism controls how fast a
critter
gets hungry.
The metabolism of a critter can be viewed in its "Properties" tab.
If its metabolism is at 100%, a critter will eat its diet as stated in the in-game tooltips (e.g. a
Hatch
will eat 140 kg per
cycle
). If its metabolism is below 100%, it will eat less (or less often) - and thus produce less resources. For example, a wild hatch (metabolism: 25%) will eat its 140 kg only every 4 cycles and thus only produces its 70 kg of
coal
only every 4 cycles.
Modifiers
The happiness of a Critter affects their critter metabolism. A critter can either be Miserable (-10 or less), Glum (-9 to -1), Satisfied (0 to 3), or Happy (4 or more). Miserable and glum tame critters have a -80% metabolism offset. Satisfied, tamed critters have no metabolism offset.
While wild, satisfied critters have a base metabolism rate of 25% and the glum metabolism offset is only -15%.
Babies further multiply their metabolism by a factor of 0.1.
Satisfied × Tame: 100%
Satisfied × Tame × Baby: 10%
Glum × Tame: 20%
Glum × Tame × Baby: 2%
Satisfied × Wild: 25%
Satisfied × Wild × Baby: 2.5%
Glum × Wild: 10%
Glum × Wild × Baby: 1%
