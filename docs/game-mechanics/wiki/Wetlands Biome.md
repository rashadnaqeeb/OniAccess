Wetlands Biome
This hot, sticky biome features plentiful pools of
Polluted Water
, as well as raw ingredients useful in establishing industrial systems.
The carnivorous
Lura Plant
traps airborne critters such as pesky
Gnits
and produces
Amber
that can be converted to
Resin
used for
Plastic
production. Local foliage also includes
Seakombs
, which can be processed into
Phyto Oil
.
Aquatic behemoths called
Jawbos
prey on
Pacus
and excrete hefty chunks of
Rust
.
