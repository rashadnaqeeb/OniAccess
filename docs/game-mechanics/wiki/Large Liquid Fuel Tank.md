The
Large Liquid Fuel Tank
holds 900 kg of
Liquid
Fuel for the engine of the rocket that it is put on. Some engines require a
Large Liquid Fuel Tank
,  such as the
Petroluem Engine
, for some it will increase the fuel that is available, such as the
Small Petroleum Engine
and some it will not benefit from, such as the
Carbon Dioxide Engine
.
Tips
Avoid placing too many as to not make the burden too high, and make sure the fuel has the correct amount of oxidizer, with a 1:1 ratio for
Fertilizer
, a 1:2 ratio for
Oxylite
, and a 1:4 ratio for
Liquid Oxygen
.
