Analysis
Shale has an unusually low Specific Heat Capacity, meaning it takes less energy to change its temperature, but it also has lower than average Thermal Conductivity, only beat out, amongst the Raw Minerals, by
Mafic Rock
,
Ceramic
, and
Graphite
.  Shale has above-average radiation absorption, tied with
Igneous Rock
and
Obsidian
, and only beaten (amongst the Raw Minerals) by
Graphite
.
However, its melting point is also on the lower end of minerals, below even
Sedimentary Rock
and
Sandstone
, though still above
Granite
.  It is also a quite soft mineral, with similar Strength and Hardness to
Sedimentary Rock
.  The practical effect of this is that it is particularly prone to damage from liquid pressure in adjacent cells, though it is also one of the easier minerals to dig through.
Trivia
Although its description states that Shale is brittle, this does not have any direct gameplay impact, instead simply referring to its rather low Hardness and Strength for a Raw Mineral.
