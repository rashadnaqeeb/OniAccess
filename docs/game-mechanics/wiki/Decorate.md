# Decorate

From left to right: Crude Painting, Quaint Painting, and Masterpiece Painting
Decorating
is the errand involving producing artworks out of various types of canvases and sculpting blocks to increase their
Decor
bonus, as well as operating certain types of artistic buildings and machinery. The time spent on a Decorate errand depends on the duplicant's
Creativity
stat, with each attribute increase adds 10% decorating speed. Duplicants which lack any decorating skills cannot do Decorating errands.
Artwork
Artworks
are buildings which can be Decorated to increase their Decor bonuses. The artwork buildings themselves (blocks and canvases) are built as a
|building
errand, however painting or sculpting them occurs as a Decorating errand. The quality of an artwork is dependent on a duplicant's
skill
. Artwork decoration is the main type of Decorate errand; these errands are prioritized as Decorating, are sped up by a duplicant's Creativity attribute, give Creativity experience, and require at least one Decorating skill (i.e.
Art Fundamentals
or better) to perform.

Sculptures
There are five types of sculptures:
Sculpting Block
(2 high),
Large Sculpting Block
(3 high),
Metal Block
(3 high),
Marble Block
(2x3), and
Ice Block
(2x2). Ice Blocks will melt at the melting temperature of water in-game. Most sculpture block types have exactly 1 crude and 1 quaint sculpture each, plus either 6 masterpieces (for Sculpture Blocks or Large Sculpture Blocks) or 5 masterpieces (for Metal Blocks or Marble Blocks). Ice Blocks, however, have

Excavate Fossil
Decorating errands also include
Excavate Fossil
, which is used to uncover or remove fossil structures as part of the
Ancient Specimen
story trait
. Excavate Fossil is much the same as other Decorating errands, except that it
requires
the Masterworks skill.
The
Ancient Specimen
,
Frozen Fossil
,
Amber Fossil
, and
Petrified Fossil
can all be excavated;
Fossil Fragments
can also be excavated, although doing so is functionally akin to deconstructing them - the Fossil Fragments are converted into, and dropped as,
Fossil
.
