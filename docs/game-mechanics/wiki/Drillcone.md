Usage
The

mines resource fields, transferring anything it mines into a free-floating resource on the same hex. Free-floating resources have no maximum storage limit. It mines at
7.5 kg/s
and consumes
1000 kg

20000 kg

20000 kg
of resources before running out of diamond after
4.44 cycles

of
Diamond

1000 tons
