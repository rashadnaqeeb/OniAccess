# Power (Building)

Power
is needed to run
machinery
in a subterranean asteroid
colony
.
Generators can produce power automatically, but they will consume various
resources
(in case of
renewables
:
heat
above
125 °C
/
257 °F
and
daylight
).
All carbon burners produce
110 °C
/
230 °F
+ hot
Carbon Dioxide
in varying amounts, which significantly contributes to their heat-footprint.
For the general behavior of electricity see
Power Circuits
.
List of power buildings
Generators
Manual Generator
– available at the beginning. Quite clean, however it occupies valuable duplicant attention to run.
Coal Generator
– obtained from
Internal Combustion
. Coal Generators are more efficient and clean than Wood Burners or Petroleum Generators, and
their fuel
is also renewable in
a relatively easy way
.
Wood Burner
– obtained from
Internal Combustion
. The most heat-inefficient of generators, it also produces copious amounts of
CO
2
, however
its fuel
is easily accessible and renewable via
Arbor Trees
.
Hydrogen Generator
– obtained from
Advanced Power Regulation
. The cleanest and the most heat-efficient of continuous burners. However,
Hydrogen
is sparse and has other important applications.
Natural Gas Generator
– obtained from
Fossil Fuels
. The cleanest, most efficient carbon-burner, however supply of its
fuel
can be somewhat limited without late-game conversion installations.
Petroleum Generator
– Obtained from
Fossil Fuels
. Burns through the fuel and creates a lot of by-products, including heat.
Steam Turbine
– obtained from
Renewable Energy
. It allow to get energy from heat directly, but requires some complicated setups.
Solar Panel
– makes power from natural sunlight found in
space
, clean and renewable, but must be protected from
Meteor Showers
. Also there is only so much asteroid surface.
Peat Burner
– Obtained from
Internal Combustion
. A
Lumb
farm might be needed to sustain it.
Wires
Wire
— available at the beginning
Wire Bridge
— Obtained from
Power Regulation
. Bridges allow wires to run past each other without merging.
Heavi-Watt Wire
and
Heavi-Watt Joint Plate
— obtained from
Advanced Power Regulation
. Heavi-Watt wires can carry 20 times more power than normal wires, but are very bad for Decor. They also can't be run through or inside tiles, but the Joint Plate lets it run through walls/floors that are 1 tile wide.
Conductive Wire
and
Conductive Wire Bridge
— Obtained from
Low-Resistance Conductors
. Conductive Wires carry twice as much power as normal wires without any decor penalties, but they can only be built from
Refined Metal
which makes them more expensive.
Heavi-Watt Conductive Wire
and
Heavi-Watt Conductive Joint Plate
— Obtained from
Low-Resistance Conductors
. This is to Heavi-Watt wire as the Conductive wire is to ordinary wire; even better power flow (50 times better than normal wire) and better Decor with the same level of convenience, but it requires lots of refined metal.
Batteries
Batteries
store extra power that your duplicants create. They must be tied via power wires to run. They are used when no power is currently produced until they run out of stored power. Batteries are not limited in their output, if the network currently demands more than the wire can support even one battery can overload the wire.
Batteries slowly discharge when not in use. The amount of discharge (called "Power runoff") depends on the battery type.
Although not a battery themselves, power transformers also store some power - with the benefit that they do
not
discharge over time.
Battery
- available at the beginning. Formerly known as "Tiny Battery" in previous versions.
Jumbo Battery
- Obtained from
Power Regulation
.
Jumbo Batteries hold 40 kJ of electricity, which is 4 times more than the normal Battery.
Smart Battery
- Obtained from
Sound Amplifiers
. Smart Batteries store 20 kJ of electricity, less than the Jumbo Battery, but they can utilize
Automation
to manage your power grid,
i.e by turning off a power-producing building so it doesn't waste resources generating power when nothing can accept it.
Regulators
Power Transformer
- obtained from
Advanced Power Regulation
, protect your networks from overload, however they are not powerful enough to fully utilize
Conductive Wires
without being placed in couples.
Large Power Transformer
- obtained from
Low-Resistance Conductors
, more powerful transformer, but can overload
Conductive Wires
.
Switch
- Obtained from
Power Regulation
. The Switch is controlled directly by the player, allowing you to control the flow of electricity.
Power Shutoff
- Obtained from
Advanced Power Regulation
. The Power Shutoff uses automation signals to do the same thing as the Switch without the need for player interaction.
Exclusive Buildings
The Bionic Booster Pack DLC added a new system, Power Banks, craftable portable batteries that hold 120 kJ of energy and can power
Bionic Duplicants
and
Flydos
. They come with three specialized buildings to integrate them to a base power grid.
Power Banks
Metal Power Bank
- obtained from
Employment
, are made in the
Crafting Station
with 200kg of Ore. It will disappear when fully discharged, meaning the 200kg of Ore used to create it will be lost.
Eco Power Bank
- Obtained from
Sound Amplifiers
, are made in the
Soldering Station
with 200kg of
Abyssalite
. To fill an Empty Eco Power Bank, a Power Bank Charger is needed.
Uranium Ore Power Bank
- obtained from
Materials Science Research
, are made in the Crafting Station with 10 kg of
Uranium Ore
. It will disappear when fully discharged. While used, emits Radiation at 60 rads/cycle.
Atomic Power Bank
- obtained from
Radiation Refinement
, are made in the
Molecular Forge
with 10 kg of
Enriched Uranium
. It auto-charges itself at a rate of 60 W over 150 cycles,  generating 5.4 MJ of energy in total. After its lifespan, it's destroyed, producing 10 kg of
Nuclear Waste
. While used it emits 120 rads/cycle.
Power Banks Buildings
Compact Discharger
- obtained from
Power Regulation
, produces energy if provided with
Power Banks
. Produces more heat and require more space relative to power production compared to the Large Discharger but they may be rotated and mounted onto walls and ceilings.
Large Discharger
- Obtained from
Low-Resistance Conductors
, produces energy if provided with
Power Banks
. Produces less heat and require less space relative to power production compared to the Compact Discharger but they cannot be rotated and can only be built on floor.
Power Bank Charger
- obtained from
Sound Amplifiers
, is used to charge empty
Eco Power Banks
. It consumes 480 W of power, but only delivers 400 W into the Eco Power Bank, so it takes 300 seconds to fully charge one.
