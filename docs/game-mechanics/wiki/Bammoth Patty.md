Rock Crusher

Production
Bammoth Patties are produced exclusively by Bammoths:
Tame, happy Bammoths consume ~44.44%/cycle of a Plume Squash Plant's growth or ~1777.77kcal/cycle of Plume Squash, converting them into 30kg/cycle of Bammoth Patty.
