# Atmospheric Pressure

Atmospheric Pressure
is based on the amount, mass, and type of
gas
filling a space at a time.
If two gases interact, the gas with the most pressure will force the other gas out of its area (mostly seen between
Oxygen
and
Carbon Dioxide
).

On

Most

Nosh Sprout
,
Dasha Saltvine

Oxyfern

Waterweed

Muckroot

Wheezewort

Thimble Reed

Jumping Joya

On Machinery
Machines whose main purpose is to generate gasses such as the
Electrolyzer
and
Oxygen Diffuser
will stop running and give an overpressure warning at 1.8kg. While a
Petroleum Generator
which produces
Carbon Dioxide
as a side product, does not.
