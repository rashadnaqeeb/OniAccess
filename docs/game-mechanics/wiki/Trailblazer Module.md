The
Trailblazer Module
sends a Duplicant in a
Lander
to a new
Planetoid
without a
Rocket Platform

It needs to be filled manually with 400 kg Refined Metal, the same metal type as the Module itself has been built with. This material will later become the
Trailblazer Lander

Deployment is available while in a
Starmap hex
adjacent to a Planetoid.
Tips
Sending two Dupes to a new Planetoid and deconstructing their
Trailblazer Landers
(400 kg) gives exactly the amount of material needed for a
Rocket Platform
(800 kg)
The
Trailblazer Lander
provides no life support. It is only a means to transport a
Duplicant
to the surface of an asteroid that has no
Rocket Platform
. It also cannot be used to return to the
Rocket
from which it deployed. Therefore, the player must be careful that a deployed
Duplicant
doesn't suffocate before building a
Rocket Platform
, enabling the
Rocket
to land.
This is especially difficult when starting a game on the
Radioactive Ocean Asteroid
, where
Reed Fiber
is not available to make
Atmo Suits
until the player colonizes a distant asteroid. However, the player may utilize
Oxygen Mask
as a substitute.
When a Dupe emerges from a Trailblazer Lander, they will have many of their parameters reset as if they had been freshly printed. For example Calories, Breath, Stress, Bladder and Rad Accumulation are all reset. The value is usually quite close to the optimal (e.g. Calories is set to 3500). Because of this reset it is unnecessary and a waste of resources to take care of a dupe who is going to use a Trailblazer, provided they don't actually die from neglect.
