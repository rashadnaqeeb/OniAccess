Interplanetary Payload
Payloads are deployed from an
Orbital Cargo Module
in orbit around a planetoid or shot from an
Interplanetary Launcher
.
The Payload will land on a random position unless there is a
Targeting Beacon
on the planetoid.
If there is a beacon, it will land on top, within 3 places to the left,  or within two places to the right of the beacon.
Payloads carry at most 200kg at once, and can be either opened manually by a
Duplicant
or delivered it to a
Payload Opener
, which will then open them automatically.
Payloads can be delivered by an
Auto-Sweeper
, duplicants or rovers.
An
Auto-Sweeper
can only deliver the payloads directly to a
Payload Opener
as they can't be put in storage or in a
Conveyor Loader
.
The internal storage is sealed, preventing the contents from offgassing. It does not insulate its content.
