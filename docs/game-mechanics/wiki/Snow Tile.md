Snow Tiles
are used as basic wall and floor tiles in sub-zero climates. They are ideal for use in situations where
Raw Minerals
are scarce or unavailable, and where
Snow
is plentiful.
Usage
Being made out of Snow, their thermal conductivity is quite low compared to that of regular
Tiles
, which can make them useful for reducing temperature transfer between walls and floors. However, they are also prone to melting when their internal temperature exceeds
0 °C
/
32 °F
, which makes them unreliable for use in above-freezing temperatures.
Due to special
thermal properties
, snow blocks have good block-to-air thermal transfer rates and can be used to quickly move heat in a tiny below zero environment. i.e: flash condensing sour gas for only 5 generators. paired with conveyors (they also have special thermal  properties) a sub-zero heater or cooler made with snow tiles can move significant heat energy.
