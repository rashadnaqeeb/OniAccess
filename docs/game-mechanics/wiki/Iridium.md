Iridium
increases Building Overheat Temperature by
500 °C
/
932 °F
. As Iridium is a Metal Ore, it can be be used as a general replacement  for
Steel
in most cases for handling steamy temperatures. This is particularly useful for
Thermo Aquatuner
, which allows it to heat up to temperatures to boil
Crude Oil
and
Petroleum
without overheating, where Steel could not.

Thermal Conductivity

Production
Letting the
Demolior
impact a players' asteroid generates 284.8 t (142.4 t after mining) of Iridium in the impact zone.
It can be harvested on rocket missions to renewable
Space POIs
:
In Conductive Ore Fields at a rate of 7.89 kg – 26.3 kg per cycle.
After destroying
Demolior
:
In Demolior Debris at a rate of 119 kg - 153 kg per cycle.
In Molten Demolior Debris at a rate of 814 kg – 1017.5 kg per cycle, in its molten form.
A one-time Iridium Meteor Shower begins immediately after destroying Demolior, rewarding the players with 1800 - 3200 kg of Iridium.
