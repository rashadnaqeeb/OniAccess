Mud
is a resource found in
Swampy Biome
on the starting asteroid. Similar to Sand, it is affected by Gravity and will fall down if the tile beneath it is mined.
Usage
Sludge Press
decomposes Mud into
Dirt
and
Water
:
150 kg Mud = 60 kg Dirt + 90 kg Water
Heating up Mud turns it into the same ratio of Dirt and Steam. The Dirt will always be created as debris, regardless of Mud being in debris or tile form. This is heat creating, as Steam and Dirt both have a higher SHC than Mud.
Mud
is one of a few materials that have a
specific heat change during phase change
.
