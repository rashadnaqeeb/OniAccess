Arboria Asteroid
A location with a natural abundance of trees.
Colonizing Arboria will require careful planning right from the start. Because of its lack of metals, finding biological solutions to problems is a must.

The
Arboria Asteroid
is one of the available
Asteroid Types
available. It is variant of
Verdante
with lower temperatures and no
Swamp Biome
. Due to this, the only way to obtain
Gold Amalgam
is from meteors, thus, hot liquid handling and
Crude Oil
processing will require
Steel
early on.

Strategy
Arboria
has very limited
Water
in the early game, making it best used for advanced research rather than irrigation. The
Ocean Biome
has
Salt Water
, which can be desalinated, while Ice from the cold Biomes can be melted using
Tempshift Plates
.
The lack of
Water
makes
Oxygen
production difficult, and the lack of
Algae
compounds on this problem. O2 production early is very difficult. Using the available
Rust
and
Salt
with a
Rust Deoxidizer
can be a good way to generate
Oxygen
early on. But fast expansion and geyser exploitation is necessary to survive long term.
All critters spawn here except
Pacu
, although only 1
Hatch
can be found buried near the
Printing Pod
. To get more hatches if this one is lost, you will need a
Care Package
from the pod or to extract one from a
Space Destination
There are 3 plants found nowhere on the asteroid:
Bristle Blossoms
,
Dusk Caps
, and
Thimble Reeds
. These can be obtained from Care Packages or Space Destinations, same as Hatches and Pcau.
The lack of Reeds means the only early method of obtaining
Reed Fiber
is through
Drecko
shearing.
