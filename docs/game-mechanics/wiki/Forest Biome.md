# Forest Biome

The
Forest Biome
is the starting
Biome
in several
Asteroid Types
and
Planetoids
. Compared to the
Sandstone Biome
, the Forest Biome is much drier with fewer deposits of
Water
.
Aluminum Ore
and
Igneous Rock
are the primary
Metal Ore
and
Raw Mineral
of choice here; with deposits of
Dirt
and
Phosphorite
as well.
Strategy
Compared to the
Sandstone Biome
; the
Forest Biome
is more difficult to survive in early on. The oxygen generated by
Oxyferns
is poor while wild; but when domesticated they are more effecient at converting
Water
into
Oxygen
than an
Electrolyzer
.
Both
Pips
and
Arbor Trees
are incredibly powerful; as wild-planting means free plant resources like food or
Wood
; at the cost of being 4x slower than domestic plants.

Single Guaranteed Hatch
While the
Forest Biome
doesn't feature
Hatches
, a single one is guaranteed at a specific location near the
Printing Pod
.
Database
Forest Biome
Temperate and filled with unique
Plant
life, this biome contains all the necessities for life support, although not in quantities sufficient to sustain a long term colony. Exploration into neighboring biomes should be a priority.
Small pockets of
Oxylite
and
Water
are present in the Forest Biome, but calculations reveal they will only sustain the colony for a limited time.
Analysis shows two native plants which should be prioritized for cultivation: The
Oxyfern
, which releases
Oxygen
but requires
Water
; and the Arbor Tree which provides
Wood
as a fuel source.
A symbiotic relationship exists with the Arbor Tree and the native
Pips
which appear to be the only critter that can find the elusive
Arbor Acorns
.
This biome is really quite beautiful. I've noted that
Shine Bugs
and
Mirth Leaf
both evoke feelings of serenity in my Duplicants.
