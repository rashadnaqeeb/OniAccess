Gunk Extractor
is the equivalent of a toilet for a
Bionic Duplicant
.
As a Bionic Duplicant performs its tasks,
Gunk
will build up in its system, which must occasionally be drained. A
Gunk Extractor
is the ideal way to facilitate drainage, which directs the liquid
Gunk
into a pipe for storage or transport.
A Gunk Extractor Produces
Gunk
when a
Bionic Duplicant
uses it. Produced mass is based on the Dupe's Gunk Tank, upwards of 80 kg. Although the game text doesn't mention it, the extracted gunk will have
Food Poisoning
germs.
Behavior
If no Gunk Extractor is available, a Bionic Duplicant will instead drain into any conventional toilet such as an
Outhouse
or
Lavatory
, but the Gunk will clog the pipes, rendering the toilet inoperable until a Duplicant cleans it out.
If a Bionic Duplicant does not have access to any facility to drain the Gunk, it will overflow and spill the Gunk into the environment or any Exosuit the Duplicant may be wearing.
Trivia
Like
Lavatory
,
Gunk Extractor
can be assigned to a specific Duplicant or kept public.
