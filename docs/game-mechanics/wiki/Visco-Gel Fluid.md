Visco-Gel Fluid
is a special
liquid
which does not flow horizontally like normal liquids when stacked on top of another tile with Visco-Gel Fluid.
Usage
The main use for the liquid are
Liquid Airlocks
without the need to add a depression to the floor. The duplicants will still be affected by the
sopping wet
debuff. Note that the Visco-Gel Fluid airlock can be destroyed by other liquids - for example, if a duplicant makes a mess next to it,
Polluted Water
might push the Visco-Gel Fluid from the bottom tile.
Visco-Gel Fluid has a very low density - 100 kg fills an entire tile and forcing 350 kg into a single tile will cause pressure damage to adjacent blocks.
Solid Visco-Gel counts as a
plastic
after freezing and can be used to construct
Plastic Tiles
and
Plastic Ladders
, so it can serve as an alternative method of delivery. With this method, it is preferable to build from the bottom, so when Visco-Gel melts it will not stick to the ceiling or to the wall but will fall on the ground.
