Berry Sludge
is a food item made from 5
Sleet Wheat Grain
(or
Megafrond Grain
) and 1600 kcal
Bristle Berry
(or
Pikeapple
). It has a
Food Quality
of Good (+3) and provides 4,000 kcal.
Berry Sludge is one of the best mid-tier foods, and is superior in quality to both
Frost Bun
and
Gristle Berry
from
Electric Grill
.
Its unlimited shelf life allows it to be stored outside of the regular food storage. It and
Pemmican
are the only
Duplicant
-craftable foods that cannot spoil.
There are no recipes using Berry Sludge as an ingredient to create more sophisticated food.
Feeding your Duplicants Berry Sludge indefinitely will require 1.25 Sleet Wheat plants and 1.5 Bristle Blossom Plants per duplicant.
Tips
In the
DLC, Berry Sludge is an excellent recipe for long term rocket ventures, due to being everlasting as well as having a decent morale bonus.
Pickled Meal
and
Grubfruit Preserve
are relatively easier to produce, but
only
last 32 cycles instead of forever (And in case of Pickled Meal, its quality is Grisly (-1), a significant drawback).
Trivia
Its inability to spoil may be a reference to the myth that fruit cake lasts forever.
