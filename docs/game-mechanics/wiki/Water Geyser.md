Water Geyser
is a renewable source of water. It is in the highest tier of water-related
geyser
, averaging 3000 g/s of water overall.
Tips
Since the temperature of the water is
95 °C
/
203 °F
, it must be reserved for heat-tolerant applications (e.g. feeding to
Electrolyzers
or
Oil Wells
) or else cooled with
Thermo Aquatuners
.
Alternatively, since the temperature it is emitted at is close to the boiling point of water (
102.35 °C
/
216.23 °F
), it is suitable for absorbing heat from anything hotter than that, turning the water into
Steam
; if it can absorb enough heat to reach
125 °C
/
257 °F
or hotter, the steam can then power a
Steam Turbine
. Note, however, that the turbine will turn the steam back into water at
95 °C
/
203 °F
, so this is only useful for removing heat from something else which is already that hot.
