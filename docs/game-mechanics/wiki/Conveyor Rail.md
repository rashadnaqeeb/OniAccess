# Conveyor Rail

Conveyor Rail
Transports Solid Materials on a track between Conveyor Loader and Conveyor Receptacle.
Can be run through wall and floor tile.
Rails move materials where they'll be needed most, saving Duplicants the walk.

Tips
Conveyor system might be the superior method of
heat
transfer due to its high throughput (enabled by their mass). However, to get effects similar to insulated or radiant pipes it must be run under
Insulated Tile
or
Metal Tile
.
However, this requires considerable amounts of Refined Metal, the acquisition of which can be troublesome and with significant opportunity costs.
Sublimating items will continue to do so while on conveyor line and in related containers, thus transporting them manually is still preferable.
Alternatively conveyor line might be run through pressurized rooms or walls.
Bugs
Conveyor rails have a peculiar heat-exchange behavior, transported items exchange heat with the tile they are on
and
the tile under it (even if it is heavily insulated). It is likely due to them being counted as an item "lying on the floor" of the passed tile.
