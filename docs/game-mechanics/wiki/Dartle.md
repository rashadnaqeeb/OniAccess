A
Dartle

Dew Dripper

Note that
Dartle
uses the same navigation as
Dreckos

Dew Dripper

Diet
Dartle
consume 1 unit of
Dewdrips
each cycle. In addition to excreting 10kg of
Bleach Stone
every cycle, Dartles contain a secondary excretion effect where they emit 10kg/cycle of
Chlorine
directly into the environment provided the cell it is in is less than 2kg of pressure. If the Dartle is over pressured, then the
Chlorine
is lost.
Food Economy
Happy
Dartles have a reproduction rate of 1 egg every 3 cycles and can reasonably lay 14 eggs in its lifetime. As a Dartle drops 800 kcal of
Meat
on death, it produces about 224 kcal of Meat every cycle.

Rhexes

Rhexes

Rhexes
'
Brine Ice
bonus from consuming Live Prey.
Database
Dartle
Domesticable Critter
Dartles are docile reptilian critters whose existence centers around maximum energy conservation.
This species was once known as the fastest land-based critter in existence. However, its deep aversion to physical exertion has grown stronger than its desire to escape predation. Researchers are uncertain what, if anything, the Dartle is saving its energy for.
