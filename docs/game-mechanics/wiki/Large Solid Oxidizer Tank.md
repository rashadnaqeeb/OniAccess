The
Large Solid Oxidizer Tank
holds 900 kg of Solid Oxidizer, either
Oxylite
or
Fertilizer
.
Oxylite has twice the efficiency of Fertilizer, meaning twice as less mass is needed when using Oxylite compared to using Fertilizer;
Each kg of fuel requires 1kg of Fertilizer, but only 0.5kg of Oxylite.
Therefore, 900kg of fuel (eg.
Petroleum
) requires 900kg of Fertilizer, but only 450kg of Oxylite.
Oxylite stored inside the tank will not offgas.
Tips
This tank should never be used. Using 2
Small Solid Oxidizer Tanks
will carry the same 900kg of Oxidizer while using just 4 tiles (1 less) and 4 burden (1 less), along with not using
Steel
, unless looks are more important and you can spare the space and resources.
