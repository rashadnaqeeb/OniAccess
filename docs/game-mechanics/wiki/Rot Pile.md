# Rot Pile

Rot Pile
An inedible glop of former foodstuff.
Rot piles break down into
Polluted Dirt
over time.
Type
Organic
Decor
-15 (3 tile radius)
Rot Pile
is leftover from expired
Food
that will convert to
Polluted Dirt
after one cycle. It does not (as of build 526946) release Polluted Oxygen until it finishes decaying into Polluted Dirt.
Usage
Rot Pile will break down into
Polluted Dirt
after one cycle at a 1:1 ratio.
Compost
can turn Rot Pile into
Dirt
at a 1:1 ratio.
Pokeshell
can eat Rot Pile and excretes
Sand
at a 2:1 ratio.
Production
When Food reaches 0% freshness, it turns into a Rot Pile.
The size of the Rot Pile produced is based off mass, not calories.
For example, 1kg of Raw Egg has 1,600 kcals, whereas 1kg of omelette has 2,800 kcals. Both foods will decay into a 1kg Rot Pile, then 1kg of Polluted Dirt.
If the intention is to deliberately rot foodstuffs to produce polluted dirt, higher quality foods will typically have worse returns than low-quality, high mass foods such as raw lettuce or
grubfruit.
Exuberant plants produce 4000g of Rot Pile when harvested.
Notes
Rot Pile is not an element. Instead it is an item with element of
Genetic Ooze
.
