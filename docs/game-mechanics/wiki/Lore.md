# Lore

Oxygen Not Included
's lore can be found as personal
emails
,
Log Entries
,
Investigations
,
Research Notes
,
Journals
, and
Company Notices
in the in-game database.
Those can be collected by inspecting certain buildings in the
Ruins
(and also the
Printing Pod
). Each building type delivers a specific type of records. They can be re-inspected on each map reload, and will deliver new records of their class until they are exhausted.
Lore unlocks are save-game independent and, once obtained, are recorded in the
unlocks.json
file, which is stored adjacent to the
save_files folder
and keybindings file.
