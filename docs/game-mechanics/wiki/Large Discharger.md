Large Discharger
is a building that produces energy if provided with
Power Banks
.
Large Dischargers produce less heat and require less space relative to power production compared to the
Compact Discharger
but they cannot be rotated or mounted to walls. They can only be built on floor. They turn idle when no more buildings require power, removing the need for a
Smart Battery
.
