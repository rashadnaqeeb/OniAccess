Mafic Rock
is a construction material that exists in the
Space Biome
and the
Rust Biome
. When Mafic Rock is heated to
1411.85 °C
/
2573.33 °F
(melting point + 2 °C), it will turn into
Magma
.
Usage
Mafic Rock is first and foremost attractive due to its comparatively low
Thermal Conductivity
, which makes it a quite decent insulator. However, due to it not being a Plumbable, it cannot be used to build (
Insulated
)
Liquid Pipes
. So, if
Insulation
or
Ceramic
aren't available in bulk, Mafic Rock is a good substitute for use in
Insulated Tiles
and
Insulated Gas Pipes
.
Trivia
Although its description states that Mafic Rock is Iron rich, there is no way to extract any Iron from it.
Mafic Rock is notable for being the only Raw Mineral that cannot be crushed into Sand in the
Rock Crusher
and which also lacks any other alternative processing recipe.
The other two Raw Minerals that cannot be crushed into sand,
Graphite
and
Fossil
, have recipes for processing them into
Fullerene
and
Lime
, respectively.
