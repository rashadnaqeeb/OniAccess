Ruins
is the unofficial name for random features that are randomly spread through the
biomes
. They contain a few interactable buildings such as:
Neural Vacillator
Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifier
(only in the
Frozen Biome
)
Lockers
Vending Machine
Computer Desk
When inspected, they will deliver
Lore
records.
Ruins are made of
mosaic Tiles
(usually made of
Obsidian
), and also may contain
Copper
-made
Batteries
,
automation
elements, and
wiring
, as well as
Plastic Ladders
,
Light Fixtures
,
Tables
, or
Clocks
. While some of these are of these are deconstructible in the same manner as regular buildings, others require the skill
demolition
(3
rd
tier
building
skill) to deconstruct. Many elements will yield the resources used to construct them, though some will not - most notably anything made from
Neutronium
, as well as Plastic Ladders, probably to deny the player early access to Plastic. Wiring and automation are usually arranged into simple puzzles that prevent direct access inside, although most of them can be deconstructed (with the exception of directional
Security Doors
).
Certain ruins are covered by the Fog of War until a door is opened or a
duplicant
is forced into the room.
Any
slime
that is part of the ruins does not have any
Slimelung
. Ruins also tend to disrupt biome borders made of
abyssalite
if spawned close to them, and therefore can be identified by anomalous conditions in the vicinity.
