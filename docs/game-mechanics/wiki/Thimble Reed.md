Database
Thimble Reed
Textile Plant
The Thimble Reed is a wetlands plant used in the production of high quality fabrics prized for their softness and breathability.
Cloth made from the Thimble Reed owes its exceptional softness to the fineness of its fibers and the unusual length to which they grow.
